## Lexicon Definition

Of Hebrew origin (H3091); Jesus (i.e. Jehoshua), the name of our Lord.

The Greek form transliterates the post-exilic Hebrew/Aramaic contraction יֵשׁוּעַ (Yeshua) of the earlier יְהוֹשׁוּעַ (Yehoshua), meaning "YHWH is salvation" or "YHWH saves." This etymology is made explicit in Mat 1:21 — "thou shalt call his name JESUS: for he shall save his people from their sins."
