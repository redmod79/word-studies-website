## Lexicon Definition

From an unused root meaning to hold together; a cup (as a container), often figuratively, a lot (as if a potion); also some unclean bird, probably an owl (perhaps from the cup-like cavity of its eye):—cup, (small) owl.

Note: This is a homonymic entry. The lexicon treats kos "cup" and kos "owl" as a single head-word because the forms are identical, but the referents are unrelated. The owl meaning occurs only 3x (Lev 11:17; Deut 14:16; Psa 102:6); the cup meaning accounts for the remaining 26 verses.
