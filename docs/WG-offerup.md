# WG: Offer Up / Sacrificial Bearing

## Overview
The offering/bearing-upward concept in the NT centers on the act of presenting sacrifices to God and vicariously bearing sin. Seven Greek words form a cluster that spans the root verb of carrying (phérō), its sacrificial compounds (anaphérō, prosphérō), the cognate offering-noun (prosphorá), the sacrifice itself (thysia), the burnt offering (holokautōma), and the sin being dealt with (hamartia). Hebrews is the primary convergence point, where 3–4 members appear together in single verses.
