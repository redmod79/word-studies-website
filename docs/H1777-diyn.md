## Semantic Categories

### 1. Divine Judgment of Peoples and Nations
God as universal judge of all humanity and the nations.
- **Gen 15:14** — "that nation, whom they shall serve, will I judge: and afterward shall they come out with great substance"
- **Psa 9:8** — "he shall judge the world in righteousness, he shall minister judgment to the people in uprightness"
- **Psa 96:10** — "he shall judge the people righteously"
- **1 Sam 2:10** — "the LORD shall judge the ends of the earth"

### 2. Advocacy: Judging/Pleading the Cause of the Poor
Executing justice on behalf of the oppressed — the word carries the sense of championing a legal cause.
- **Prov 31:9** — "Open thy mouth, judge righteously, and plead the cause of the poor and needy"
- **Jer 22:16** — "He judged the cause of the poor and needy; then [it was] well with him"
- **Jer 5:28** — "they judge not the cause, the cause of the fatherless... the right of the needy do they not judge"
- **Psa 72:2** — "He shall judge thy people with righteousness, and thy poor with judgment"

### 3. Eschatological / Cosmic Judgment
Divine judgment at a climactic or final reckoning.
- **Psa 7:8** — "The LORD shall judge the people: judge me, O LORD, according to my righteousness"
- **Deut 32:36** — "the LORD shall judge his people, and repent himself for his servants"
- **Psa 50:4** — "He shall call to the heavens... that he may judge his people"
- **Psa 110:6** — "He shall judge among the heathen, he shall fill [the places] with the dead bodies"

### 4. Prophetic Commands to Execute Justice
Imperative calls to rulers and leaders to actively govern with justice.
- **Jer 21:12** — "Execute judgment in the morning, and deliver [him that is] spoiled out of the hand of the oppressor"
- **Zech 3:7** — "thou shalt also judge my house, and shalt also keep my courts"
- **Isa 3:13** — "The LORD standeth up to plead, and standeth to judge the people"

### 5. Striving / Contending (dûwn nuance)
The alternate root form dûwn emphasizes dispute and contest rather than formal judging.
- **Gen 6:3** — "My spirit shall not always strive with man, for that he also [is] flesh"
- **2 Sam 19:9** — "all the people were at strife throughout all the tribes of Israel"
- **Eccl 6:10** — "neither may he contend with him that is mightier than he"
