## Lexicon Definition

(Aramaic) corresponding to H1881; — decree, law.

*BDB expanded:* dâth noun feminine — (1) decree of a king (Dan 2:13, 15; Ezr 7:26); with suffix "decree against you" (Dan 2:9). (2) Unchangeable law of the Medes and Persians (Dan 6:9, 13, 16). (3) Law of God, in the mouth of non-Israelites (Dan 6:6; Ezr 7:12, 14, 21, 26; Dan 7:25). Persian loan-word; see also Biblical Hebrew H1881.
