## Lexicon Definition

A primitive root; to slaughter an animal (usually in sacrifice): — kill, offer, (do) sacrifice, slay.

Zabach denotes the specific act of ritually killing an animal, typically in a cultic context. It is the verbal counterpart of the noun zebach (H2077) — the slaughtered victim. The verb encompasses both legitimate worship-slaughter (covenantal, peace, and thank offerings) and illegitimate slaughter to rival deities or at unauthorized high places.

## Semantic Categories
