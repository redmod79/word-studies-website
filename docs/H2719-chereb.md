The LXX translators split chereb between two distinct Greek words: rhomphaia (the large Thracian broadsword) and machaira (the short sword or dagger). This split carries into the NT, where rhomphaia becomes the apocalyptic sword from Christ's mouth (Revelation) while machaira is the everyday sword (Peter's sword, the Spirit's sword in Eph 6:17).
