## Semantic Categories

### 1. Prophetic Revelation — Vision Received
- **Isa 21:2** — "A grievous vision (chazuwth qashah) is declared unto me; the treacherous dealer dealeth treacherously, and the spoiler spoileth." The word denotes the revelatory content — what the prophet has been shown.
- **Isa 29:11** — "And the vision (chazuwth) of all is become unto you as the words of a book that is sealed." The entire prophetic revelation has become incomprehensible, like a sealed document.

### 2. Striking Appearance — Notable/Conspicuous
- **Dan 8:5** — "the goat had a notable horn (qeren chazuwth) between his eyes." Here chazuwth modifies the horn — describing it as conspicuous, visually striking.
- **Dan 8:8** — "for it came up four notable ones (chazuwth arba') toward the four winds of heaven." The four replacement horns are described as conspicuous or prominent.

### 3. Compact / Agreement
- **Isa 28:18** — "your covenant (b'rith) with death shall be disannulled, and your agreement (chazuwth) with hell shall not stand." Parallel to b'rith, chazuwth here means a pact or agreement — the idea being something "seen to" or mutually looked upon as binding.
