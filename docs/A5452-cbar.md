## Semantic Categories

### 1. Deliberate Intent / Purpose to Act

The sole occurrence appears in a sequence of purposeful acts by the little horn: speaking against God, wearing out the saints, and *intending* to alter divinely fixed times and law. The verb carries the sense of forming a resolve in the mind directed toward action (followed by the infinitive לְהַשְׁנָיָה, "to change").

- **Dan 7:25** — "And he shall speak [great] words against the most High, and shall wear out the saints of the most High, and **think** to change times and laws: and they shall be given into his hand until a time and times and the dividing of time."
