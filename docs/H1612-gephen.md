**Note on "first vine" terminology:** Gen 9:20 — Noah's planting after the Flood — uses כֶּרֶם (*kerem*, H3754, "vineyard"), not *gephen*. ## Semantic Categories

### 1. Literal Grape Vine (agricultural / botanical)
- **Gen 40:9–10** — chief butler's dream: a vine with three branches budding and bringing forth clusters
- **Num 6:4** — Nazirite law: "he shall eat nothing that is made of the vine tree (*gephen ha-yayin*)"
- **Deut 8:8** — the Land described as "a land of wheat, and barley, and vines (*gephen*), and fig trees"
- **2 Kings 4:39** — a "wild vine" (*gephen sadeh*, vine of the field) found while gathering herbs

### 2. Covenant Blessing Formula: "every man under his vine and fig tree"
Canonical Shalom formula for peace and prosperity under YHWH's rule.
- **1 Kings 4:25** — Solomonic peak: "Judah and Israel dwelt safely, every man under his vine and under his fig tree"
- **Micah 4:4** — eschatological Zion: "they shall sit every man under his vine and under his fig tree; and none shall make them afraid"
- **Zech 3:10** — "In that day... shall ye call every man his neighbour under the vine and under the fig tree"
- **2 Kings 18:31 / Isa 36:16** — Rabshakeh's counterfeit offer ("eat every one of his own vine") — an Assyrian parody of the formula

### 3. Israel as the Vine (national metaphor)
- **Ps 80:8, 14** — "Thou hast brought a vine out of Egypt… return, we beseech thee, O God of hosts: look down from heaven… and visit this vine"
- **Jer 2:21** — "I had planted thee a noble vine, wholly a right seed: how then art thou turned into the degenerate plant of a strange vine unto me?"
- **Hos 10:1** — "Israel is an empty vine (*gephen boqeq*), he bringeth forth fruit unto himself"
- **Ezek 17:6–8** — allegory of the eagle and the vine (Zedekiah's covenant-breaking)
- **Ezek 19:10** — lament: "Thy mother is like a vine in thy blood, planted by the waters"

### 4. Judgment / Withering of the Vine
Failure of the *gephen* signals covenant curse and Day-of-the-Lord disaster.
- **Isa 24:7** — "The new wine mourneth, the vine languisheth" (global apocalyptic judgment)
- **Isa 34:4** — "all their host shall fall down, as the leaf falleth off from the vine" (cosmic dissolution)
- **Jer 8:13** — "there shall be no grapes on the vine, nor figs on the fig tree"
- **Hos 2:12** — "I will destroy her vines and her fig trees"
- **Joel 1:7, 12** — "He hath laid my vine waste… the vine is dried up"
- **Hab 3:17** — "neither shall fruit be in the vines"

### 5. Eschatological Restoration of the Vine
The vine's renewed fruitfulness marks the age to come.
- **Joel 2:22** — "Be not afraid… for the tree beareth her fruit, the fig tree and the vine do yield their strength"
- **Hag 2:19** — "from this day will I bless you… the vine, and the fig tree, and the pomegranate, and the olive tree"
- **Zech 8:12** — "the seed shall be prosperous; the vine shall give her fruit"
- **Mal 3:11** — "neither shall your vine cast her fruit before the time in the field"
- **Hos 14:7** — "they shall revive as the corn, and grow as the vine"

### 6. Deviant / Alien Vine (the false vine)
- **Deut 32:32** — "their vine is of the vine of Sodom, and of the fields of Gomorrah: their grapes are grapes of gall"
- **Jer 2:21** — "the degenerate plant of a strange vine"
- **Hos 10:1** — "empty vine" (*boqeq*, emptying itself)

### 7. Judah's Messianic Vine (Gen 49)
- **Gen 49:11** — Shiloh oracle: "Binding his foal unto the vine, and his ass's colt unto the choice vine; he washed his garments in wine"

### 8. Judges 9 Fable
- **Jdg 9:12–13** — Jotham's parable: the trees offer the kingship to the vine, which replies, "Should I leave my wine, which cheereth God and man…?"
