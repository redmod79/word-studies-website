## Lexicon Definition

A primitive root; to be safe (in mind, body or estate); figuratively, to be (causatively, make) completed; by implication, to be friendly; by extension, to reciprocate (in various applications): — make amends, (make an) end, finish, full, give again, make good, (re-)pay (again), (make) (to) (be at) peace(-able), that is perfect, perform, (make) prosper(-ous), recompense, render, requite, make restitution, restore, reward, surely.

## Semantic Categories

### 1. Legal Restitution (Restoring Loss)
The core legal usage: making good for injury, theft, or property damage.

- **Exo 22:1** — "he shall restore five oxen for an ox, and four sheep for a sheep"
- **Lev 5:16** — "he shall make amends for the harm that he hath done in the holy thing"
- **Lev 6:5** — "he shall even restore it in the principal, and shall add the fifth part"
- **Exo 21:34** — "The owner of the pit shall make it good, and give money"

### 2. Divine Retribution (God Repaying According to Deeds)
God as the agent who shalems — repaying evil to the wicked, rendering judgment.

- **Deut 7:10** — "And repayeth them that hate him to their face, to destroy them"
- **Isa 59:18** — "According to their deeds, accordingly he will repay, fury to his adversaries"
- **Jer 16:18** — "And first I will recompense their iniquity and their sin double"
- **Jer 51:24** — "I will render unto Babylon and to all the inhabitants of Chaldea all their evil"

### 3. Completion / Finishing a Work
A task brought to its full end — especially construction and divine plans.

- **1Ki 7:51** — "So was ended all the work that king Solomon made for the house of the LORD"
- **2Ch 5:1** — "Thus all the work that Solomon made for the house of the LORD was finished"
- **Neh 6:15** — "So the wall was finished in the twenty and fifth day of the month Elul"
- **Isa 44:28** — "That saith of Cyrus, He is my shepherd, and shall perform all my pleasure"

### 4. Making Peace (Covenantal Harmony)
Peace as a completed state of relationship — nations, individuals, even creation.

- **Josh 10:1** — "how the inhabitants of Gibeon had made peace with Israel"
- **Josh 11:19** — "There was not a city that made peace with the children of Israel"
- **2Sam 10:19** — "they made peace with Israel, and served them"
- **Job 22:21** — "Acquaint now thyself with him, and be at peace: thereby good shall come"

### 5. Vow Fulfillment (Paying What Was Vowed)
Religious obligation brought to completion — the devout payment of vows.

- **Deut 23:21** — "When thou shalt vow a vow unto the LORD thy God, thou shalt not slack to pay it"
- **Eccl 5:4** — "When thou vowest a vow unto God, defer not to pay it; for he hath no pleasure in fools"
- **Jon 2:9** — "I will sacrifice unto thee with the voice of thanksgiving; I will pay that that I have vowed"
- **Job 22:27** — "Thou shalt make thy prayer unto him, and he shall hear thee, and thou shalt pay thy vows"

### 6. Positive Reward / Restoration
Good rendered for good — divine blessing, comfort, restoration.

- **1Sam 24:19** — "the LORD reward thee good for that thou hast done unto me this day"
- **Prov 25:22** — "thou shalt heap coals of fire upon his head, and the LORD shall reward thee"
- **Joel 2:25** — "I will restore to you the years that the locust hath eaten"
- **Isa 57:18** — "I will lead him also, and restore comforts unto him and to his mourners"
