## Roles in the Concept

- **Holder:** λυχνία (G3087) — the stand that holds and elevates the lamp
- **Source:** λύχνος (G3088) — the lamp/candle that burns
- **Output:** φῶς (G5457) — the light emitted
- **Variant object:** λαμπάς (G2985) — torch/lamp, particularly in the ten virgins parable and Revelation

## Key Passages Where Multiple Group Words Appear Together

- **Mat 5:15** — [G3087 + G3088] "light a candle (λύχνος) and put it on a candlestick (λυχνία)"

- **Rev 1:12–20** — [G3087] seven golden candlesticks = seven churches
