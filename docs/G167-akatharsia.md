## Semantic Categories

### 1. Vice-List Member — Moral Impurity in the Flesh
The dominant usage: akatharsia appears as a standard member of Pauline vice catalogues, consistently grouped with sexual sin and greed.
- **Gal 5:19** — "Now the works of the flesh are manifest, which are these; Adultery, fornication, uncleanness, lasciviousness"
- **Eph 5:3** — "But fornication, and all uncleanness, or covetousness, let it not be once named among you, as becometh saints"
- **Col 3:5** — "Mortify therefore your members which are upon the earth; fornication, uncleanness, inordinate affection, evil concupiscence, and covetousness"

### 2. Moral Condition Resulting from Rejection of God
Uncleanness as the state God gives people over to when they reject Him, or the sphere from which believers are called out.
- **Rom 1:24** — "Wherefore God also gave them up to uncleanness through the lusts of their own hearts, to dishonour their own bodies between themselves"
- **Rom 6:19** — "for as ye have yielded your members servants to uncleanness and to iniquity unto iniquity; even so now yield your members servants to righteousness unto holiness"
- **1Th 4:7** — "For God hath not called us unto uncleanness, but unto holiness"

### 3. Holiness-Antithesis — The Opposite of Sanctification
In two passages akatharsia is explicitly contrasted with ἁγιασμός (hagiasmos, sanctification/holiness), framing the Christian life as a binary: uncleanness vs. holiness.
- **1Th 4:7** — "God hath not called us unto uncleanness, but unto holiness"
- **Rom 6:19** — "yielded your members servants to uncleanness… even so now yield your members servants to righteousness unto holiness"

### 4. Inner Corruption — Hidden Moral Filth
The single non-Pauline use applies the word to hidden internal decay.
- **Mat 23:27** — "ye are like unto whited sepulchres, which indeed appear beautiful outward, but are within full of dead men's bones, and of all uncleanness"

### 5. Unrepented Habitual Sin
Linked to ongoing sinful practice that grieves the apostle.
- **2Co 12:21** — "I shall bewail many which have sinned already, and have not repented of the uncleanness and fornication and lasciviousness which they have committed"
- **Eph 4:19** — "Who being past feeling have given themselves over unto lasciviousness, to work all uncleanness with greediness"

### 6. Apostolic Integrity — What Ministry Is Free From
Paul denies that his motivation comes from moral impurity.
- **1Th 2:3** — "For our exhortation was not of deceit, nor of uncleanness, nor in guile"
