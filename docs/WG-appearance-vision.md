# WG: Appearance and Vision (Hebrew)

## Overview
This cluster covers how Hebrew Scripture talks about outward appearance, beauty, and prophetic
vision. The concepts are linked through the root H7200 (ra'ah, to see) and cluster around
two poles: *what something looks like* (physical/assessed appearance) and *what was shown*
(prophetic revelation).

## Roles in the Concept

- **Root action:** ra'ah (H7200) — to see (the perceptual act underlying all the nouns)
- **Primary noun:** mar'eh (H4758) — the thing seen; appearance OR vision (broadest term)
- **Feminine/mirror form:** mar'ah (H4759) — vision (narrower, often prophetic) and mirror

- **Quality of appearance:** yapheh (H3303) — beautiful; the adjective applied to the noun (tob / yapheh + mar'eh/to'ar)
