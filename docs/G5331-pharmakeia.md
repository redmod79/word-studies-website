Listed immediately after εἰδωλολατρεία (idolatry) in Paul's "works of the flesh" catalogue (Gal 5:19–21). The pairing with idolatry places pharmakeia in the sphere of false worship and spiritual rebellion. Nominative singular.

### 2. Apocalyptic Deception and Impenitence

In Revelation, pharmakeia is an instrument of Babylon's deception over nations (18:23, dative singular — the means by which deception occurs) and a sin from which the impenitent refuse to turn (9:21, genitive plural — listed alongside murders, fornication, and thefts).
