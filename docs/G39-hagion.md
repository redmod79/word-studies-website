## Lexicon Definition

Neuter of ἅγιος (hagios); a sacred thing (i.e. spot) — holiest (of all), holy place, sanctuary.

Note: G39 is assigned as a distinct Strong's number for the substantivized neuter use of G40 ἅγιος when it denotes a sacred place or sanctuary, as opposed to the adjective's broader range ("holy," "saints," etc.). All 10 KJV occurrences appear in Hebrews and refer to the tabernacle/temple sanctuary.
