## Semantic Categories

### 1. Legal Case / Lawsuit
The formal legal cause brought before a judge or before God for adjudication.
- **Exo 23:3** — "Neither shalt thou countenance a poor man in his cause."
- **Exo 23:6** — "Thou shalt not wrest the judgment of thy poor in his cause."
- **Deu 25:1** — "If there be a controversy between men, and they come unto judgment, that the judges may judge them"
- **2Sa 15:2** — "when any man that had a controversy came to the king for judgment"

### 2. God Pleading / Vindicating a Cause
YHWH or God acting as advocate, taking up the legal case of the oppressed or the psalmist.
- **1Sa 25:39** — "Blessed be the LORD, that hath pleaded the cause of my reproach"
- **Psa 43:1** — "Judge me, O God, and plead my cause against an ungodly nation"
- **Psa 119:154** — "Plead my cause, and deliver me: quicken me according to thy word"

### 3. YHWH's Covenant Lawsuit
The prophetic *riyb* pattern: God brings a formal legal complaint against Israel or the nations for covenant violation.

- **Mic 6:2** — "Hear ye, O mountains, the LORD'S controversy, and ye strong foundations of the earth: for the LORD hath a controversy with the inhabitants thereof"
- **Jer 25:31** — "the LORD hath a controversy with the nations, he will plead with all flesh"

### 4. Interpersonal Strife / Quarrel
