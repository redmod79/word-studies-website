## Continuity and Shifts

**What stays the same across testaments:**
- The ordinal meaning "fifth" is unchanged from Hebrew through LXX into NT Greek
- Both testaments use the word in sequential enumerations (days/months in OT; seals/angels/stones in NT)

**What shifts:**
- The OT fractional sense ("a fifth part" = 20% surcharge in restitution law) has no NT equivalent. The NT never uses pemptos as a fraction.
- OT usage spans historical, legal, and chronological contexts; NT usage is entirely apocalyptic (Revelation only).
