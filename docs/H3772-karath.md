## Semantic Categories

### 1. Covenant-Making (karath beriyth)
The idiom karath beriyth — "cut a covenant" — accounts for ~75 verses (beriyth co-occurs 80x). The metaphor derives from cutting animals and passing between the pieces (Gen 15).

- **Gen 15:18** — "In the same day the LORD made a covenant with Abram, saying, Unto thy seed have I given this land"
- **Deut 5:2** — "The LORD our God made a covenant with us in Horeb"
- **1 Sam 18:3** — "Then Jonathan and David made a covenant, because he loved him as his own soul"
- **Haggai 2:5** — "the word that I covenanted with you when ye came out of Egypt"

### 2. Judicial Cutting Off (Covenant Penalty)
The Niphal formula "that soul shall be cut off from his people" is the standard Mosaic penalty for covenant violation. Appears ~30x in Leviticus-Numbers.

- **Gen 17:14** — "the uncircumcised man child... shall be cut off from his people; he hath broken my covenant"
- **Lev 7:20** — "the soul that eateth of the flesh of the sacrifice of peace offerings... shall be cut off from his people"
- **Num 15:30-31** — "the soul that doeth ought presumptuously... shall be cut off from among his people"
- **Exod 12:15** — "whosoever eateth leavened bread from the first day... that soul shall be cut off from Israel"

### 3. Divine Judgment on Nations and Leaders
God or his agents "cut off" enemies, dynasties, and peoples — the language of divine warfare and prophetic judgment.

- **2 Kings 9:8** — "I will cut off from Ahab him that pisseth against the wall"
- **Mic 5:9-11** — "all thine enemies shall be cut off... I will cut off thy horses... I will cut off the cities of thy land"
- **Zeph 1:3-4** — "I will cut off man from off the land... I will cut off the remnant of Baal"
- **Obad 1:10** — "thou shalt be cut off for ever"

### 4. Physical Cutting (Trees, Timber, Body)
The literal sense: felling trees, cutting wood, circumcision. Concentrated in Solomon's temple-building narratives and circumcision accounts.

- **Exod 4:25** — "Zipporah took a sharp stone, and cut off the foreskin of her son"
- **1 Kings 5:6** — "command thou that they hew me cedar trees out of Lebanon"
- **Judg 9:48** — "Abimelech... cut down a bough from the trees"
- **2 Chron 2:16** — "we will cut wood out of Lebanon, as much as thou shalt need"

### 5. Prophetic Cessation (cut off = ceasing to exist)
Used to describe the end of provision, prophecy, or life — something is "cut off" when it simply stops.

- **Joel 1:9** — "The meat offering and the drink offering is cut off from the house of the LORD"
- **Prov 23:18** — "thine expectation shall not be cut off"
- **Dan 9:26** — "shall Messiah be cut off, but not for himself"
- **Jer 9:21** — "death is come up into our windows... to cut off the children from without"
