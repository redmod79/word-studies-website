## Semantic Categories

### 1. The Guiding Cloud (Wilderness Pillar)
The primary use — Yahweh's cloud-pillar guiding and protecting Israel in the wilderness. Commands Israel's movement.

- **Exo 13:21** — "And the LORD went before them by day in a pillar of a cloud, to lead them the way; and by night in a pillar of fire"
- **Num 9:17** — "When the cloud was taken up from the tabernacle, then after that the children of Israel journeyed: and in the place where the cloud abode, there the children of Israel pitched their tents."
- **Psa 78:14** — "In the daytime also he led them with a cloud, and all the night with a light of fire."
- **Neh 9:19** — "Yet thou in thy manifold mercies forsookest them not in the wilderness: the pillar of the cloud departed not from them by day"

### 2. The Cloud of Divine Communication (Sinai / Tent of Meeting)
The cloud as the medium through which God speaks and appears.

- **Exo 19:9** — "And the LORD said unto Moses, Lo, I come unto thee in a thick cloud, that the people may hear when I speak with thee"
- **Exo 24:16** — "The glory of the LORD abode upon mount Sinai, and the cloud covered it six days: and the seventh day he called unto Moses out of the midst of the cloud."
- **Num 11:25** — "And the LORD came down in a cloud, and spake unto him"
- **Psa 99:7** — "He spake unto them in the cloudy pillar: they kept his testimonies, and the ordinance that he gave them."

### 3. The Glory Cloud (Sanctuary / Temple)
The cloud that fills and consecrates the sanctuary as the visible kabod of Yahweh.

- **Exo 40:34** — "A cloud covered the tent of the congregation, and the glory of the LORD filled the tabernacle."
- **Lev 16:2** — "for I will appear in the cloud upon the mercy seat"
- **1 Ki 8:10–11** — "the cloud filled the house of the LORD...for the glory of the LORD had filled the house of the LORD."
- **Psa 105:39** — "He spread a cloud for a covering; and fire to give light in the night."

### 4. The Covenantal Cloud (Noah)
The cloud that bears the rainbow as covenant sign.

- **Gen 9:13** — "I do set my bow in the cloud, and it shall be for a token of a covenant between me and the earth."
- **Gen 9:14** — "when I bring a cloud over the earth, that the bow shall be seen in the cloud"
- **Gen 9:16** — "And the bow shall be in the cloud; and I will look upon it, that I may remember the everlasting covenant"

### 5. The Judgment Cloud (Day of the LORD)
Prophetic use — cloud as harbinger of divine wrath; the warrior-God advancing on his enemies.

- **Joel 2:2** — "A day of darkness and of gloominess, a day of clouds and of thick darkness"
- **Eze 30:3** — "the day of the LORD is near, a cloudy day; it shall be the time of the heathen."
- **Zep 1:15** — "a day of clouds and thick darkness"
- **Nah 1:3** — "the LORD hath his way in the whirlwind and in the storm, and the clouds are the dust of his feet."

### 6. Metaphorical / Poetic Uses
Cloud as image of transience, obstruction, and blotting out.

- **Job 7:9** — "As the cloud is consumed and vanisheth away: so he that goeth down to the grave shall come up no more."
- **Isa 44:22** — "I have blotted out, as a thick cloud, thy transgressions, and, as a cloud, thy sins"
- **Hos 6:4** — "your goodness is as a morning cloud, and as the early dew it goeth away"
- **Lam 3:44** — "Thou hast covered thyself with a cloud, that our prayer should not pass through."
