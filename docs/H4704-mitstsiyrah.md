## Lexicon Definition

Feminine of H4705 (מִצְעָר); properly, *littleness*; concretely, diminutive:—little.

The form is a prepositional compound: מִן ("from") + the feminine of צָעִיר ("small, young, least"), producing the sense "from smallness" or "out of littleness." In Dan 8:9 it functions adjectivally, describing the initial insignificance of the horn before it grew exceedingly great.
