## Lexicon Definition

From ἐκ (out of) and χέω (to pour); to pour forth; figuratively, to bestow, shed forth, spill, or gush out. Used of liquids (wine, water, blood), of the Spirit poured out as a gift, of wrath poured from vials in judgment, and of love shed abroad in the heart. Encompasses both literal outpouring and figurative lavish distribution.

## Semantic Categories

### 1. Christ's Blood Shed for Atonement
The Last Supper formula and the blood of the new covenant.
- **Mat 26:28** — "this is my blood of the new testament, which is shed for many for the remission of sins"
- **Mark 14:24** — "This is my blood of the new testament, which is shed for many"
- **Luke 22:20** — "This cup is the new testament in my blood, which is shed for you"

### 2. Outpouring of the Holy Spirit
Eschatological and Pentecostal gift-language; the Spirit as a liquid poured from heaven.
- **Acts 2:17** — "I will pour out of my Spirit upon all flesh"
- **Acts 2:18** — "on my servants and on my handmaidens I will pour out in those days of my Spirit"
- **Acts 2:33** — "he hath shed forth this, which ye now see and hear"
- **Acts 10:45** — "on the Gentiles also was poured out the gift of the Holy Ghost"
- **Tit 3:6** — "Which he shed on us abundantly through Jesus Christ our Saviour"

### 3. Wrath Poured from Vials (Apocalyptic Judgment)
The seven bowl plagues of Revelation 16 — the most concentrated cluster in the NT.
- **Rev 16:1** — "pour out the vials of the wrath of God upon the earth"
- **Rev 16:2–4, 8, 10, 12, 17** — each angel in turn "poured out his vial" on earth, sea, rivers, sun, throne, Euphrates, air

### 4. Violent Shedding of Innocent Blood
Martyrdom and murder — blood of prophets, righteous Abel, Stephen.
- **Mat 23:35** — "all the righteous blood shed upon the earth, from the blood of righteous Abel"
- **Luke 11:50** — "the blood of all the prophets, which was shed from the foundation of the world"
- **Acts 22:20** — "when the blood of thy martyr Stephen was shed"
- **Rom 3:15** — "Their feet are swift to shed blood"
- **Rev 16:6** — "they have shed the blood of saints and prophets"

### 5. Love Poured into the Heart
A metaphorical outpouring of divine love via the Spirit.
- **Rom 5:5** — "the love of God is shed abroad in our hearts by the Holy Ghost"

### 6. Spilled or Gushed Liquid (Literal, Non-Theological)
Ordinary physical spilling — wine, bowels, money.
- **Mat 9:17** — "the wine runneth out, and the bottles perish"
- **Mark 2:22** — "the wine is spilled, and the bottles will be marred"
- **Luke 5:37** — "the new wine will burst the bottles, and be spilled"
- **John 2:15** — "poured out the changers' money, and overthrew the tables"
- **Acts 1:18** — "he burst asunder in the midst, and all his bowels gushed out"

### 7. Figurative "Running After" in Error
A Septuagintal idiom for rushing headlong into sin — "poured out" in the sense of abandoning self to something.
- **Jude 1:11** — "ran greedily after the error of Balaam for reward"
