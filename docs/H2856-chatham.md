## Semantic Categories

### 1. Legal and Documentary Sealing
Authenticating official letters, deeds, and covenants by impressing a signet ring — the act that made a document binding and tamper-evident.
- **1 Kgs 21:8** — "she wrote letters in Ahab's name, and sealed them with his seal, and sent the letters unto the elders"
- **Jer 32:10** — "I subscribed the evidence, and sealed it, and took witnesses, and weighed him the money"
- **Est 8:10** — "he wrote in the king Ahasuerus' name, and sealed it with the king's ring, and sent letters by posts"
- **Neh 9:38** — "we make a sure covenant, and write it; and our princes, Levites, and priests, seal unto it"

### 2. Divine Sealing of Hidden Things
God as agent sealing what is kept in reserve — transgressions, treasures, and natural phenomena — inaccessible until his appointed purpose.
- **Deut 32:34** — "Is not this laid up in store with me, and sealed up among my treasures?"
- **Job 14:17** — "My transgression is sealed up in a bag, and thou sewest up mine iniquity"
- **Job 9:7** — "Which commandeth the sun, and it riseth not; and sealeth up the stars"
- **Job 37:7** — "He sealeth up the hand of every man; that all men may know his work"

### 3. Prophetic Sealing of Revelation
Sealing a vision or book to preserve it until the time of fulfillment; the sealed content is inaccessible until the end.
- **Isa 8:16** — "Bind up the testimony, seal the law among my disciples"
- **Isa 29:11** — "the vision of all is become unto you as the words of a book that is sealed"
- **Dan 9:24** — "to finish the transgression, and to make an end of sins...and to seal up the vision and prophecy"
- **Dan 12:4** — "shut up the words, and seal the book, even to the time of the end"

### 4. Closure and Enclosure
The basic physical sense: stopping a flow or enclosing something completely.
- **Lev 15:3** — "whether his flesh run with his issue, or his flesh be stopped (Hiphil) from his issue" (closing a bodily discharge)
- **Song 4:12** — "A garden inclosed is my sister, my spouse; a spring shut up, a fountain sealed"
- **Ezek 28:12** — "Thou sealest up the sum, full of wisdom and perfect in beauty" (the king as perfect pattern/exemplar)
