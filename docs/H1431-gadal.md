## Lexicon Definition

## Semantic Categories

### 1. Divine Magnification — God's Name or Acts Declared Great
God's deeds and name are the object of magnification; often liturgical or prophetic doxology.
- **2 Sam 7:22** — "Wherefore thou art great, O LORD God: for there is none like thee"
- **Joel 2:21** — "Fear not, O land; be glad and rejoice: for the LORD will do great things"
- **Mal 1:5** — "The LORD will be magnified from the border of Israel"
- **Ps 34:3** — "O magnify the LORD with me" (cf. Ps 18:50; 35:26)

### 2. Human Physical Growth — Children, Animals, Plants
Biological/developmental growth of persons or creatures; predominantly Qal stem.
- **Gen 21:8** — "the child grew, and was weaned" (Isaac)
- **Judg 13:24** — "the child grew, and the LORD blessed him" (Samson)
- **2 Ki 4:18** — "when the child was grown, it fell on a day..."
- **Ezek 31:4** — "The waters made him great, the deep set him up on high" (cedar/Assyria)

### 3. Making Someone Great — Divine or Royal Promotion
Granting greatness, honor, or status to another; predominantly Hiphil/Piel.
- **Gen 12:2** — "I will bless thee, and make thy name great"
- **Josh 3:7** — "This day will I begin to magnify thee in the sight of all Israel" (Joshua)
- **1 Ki 1:47** — "God make the name of Solomon better than thy name"
- **Esth 3:1** — "Ahasuerus promote Haman... and advanced him, and set his seat above all the princes"

### 4. Self-Exaltation / Arrogant Pride — Nations or Rulers
Hithpael and Hiphil reflexive uses; magnifying oneself against God or a neighbor; always condemned.
- **Isa 10:15** — "shall the saw magnify itself against him that shaketh it?"
- **Jer 48:26, 42** — "Moab magnified himself against the LORD"
- **Dan 8:4** — the ram "magnified himself" (cf. 8:8, 11, 25; 11:36–37)
- **Zeph 2:8, 10** — Ammon "magnified themselves against the people of the LORD"

### 5. Nourishing / Raising Children
Caring for children through rearing; Piel stem ("bring up, nourish up").
- **Isa 1:2** — "I have nourished and brought up children, and they have rebelled"
- **Lam 2:22** — "those that I have swaddled and brought up hath mine enemy consumed"
- **2 Ki 10:6** — "the king's sons... which brought them up"

### 6. Worldly Achievement / Accumulation
Human accumulation of power, wealth, or reputation; Qal or Hiphil.
- **Ecc 1:16** — "I am come to great estate, and have gotten more wisdom than all"
- **Ecc 2:9** — "So I was great, and increased more than all that were before me"
- **1 Ki 10:23** — "Solomon exceeded all the kings of the earth for riches and wisdom"
- **Gen 26:13** — "the man waxed great... and grew until he became very great"
