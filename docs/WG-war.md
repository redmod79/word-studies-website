# WG: War / Armed Conflict

## Overview
The NT war/conflict vocabulary covers organized warfare (polemos), the act of fighting (polemeo, machomai), military service (strateuomai), weapons (machaira), personal disputes (mache), and the outcome of conflict (nikao). These words cluster most densely in Revelation and James 4.

## Roles in the Concept

- **The conflict:** polemos (πόλεμος) — war or battle as an event or condition
- **Waging it:** polemeo (πολεμέω) — the act of making war
- **The weapon:** machaira (μάχαιρα) — the sword, instrument of warfare and judgment
- **Personal scale:** mache (μάχη) — quarrels, disputes, fightings
- **Personal action:** machomai (μάχομαι) — to fight or strive at the individual level
- **Military service:** strateuomai (στρατεύομαι) — to serve as a soldier, to campaign
- **The outcome:** nikao (νικάω) — to overcome, to conquer — the resolution of conflict
