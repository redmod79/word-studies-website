## Semantic Categories

### 1. Physical Hand / Body Part
The literal open hand used for grasping, holding, touching.
- **Gen 3:22** — "lest he put forth his hand, and take also of the tree of life"
- **Gen 22:10** — "Abraham stretched forth his hand, and took the knife to slay his son"
- **Exo 4:6** — "Put now thine hand into thy bosom"
- **Jdg 3:21** — "Ehud put forth his left hand, and took the dagger from his right thigh"

### 2. Power, Authority, Control
Yad denotes the capacity to act, govern, or dominate — power that can be exercised or withheld.
- **Gen 31:29** — "It is in the power of my hand to do you hurt"
- **Num 11:23** — "Is the LORD's hand waxed short?"
- **Deut 8:17** — "My power and the might of mine hand hath gotten me this wealth"
- **Isa 59:1** — "The LORD's hand is not shortened, that it cannot save"

### 3. Deliverance and Transfer ("into / out of the hand")
The most frequent idiomatic construction. "Into the hand" = surrender to someone's control; "out of the hand" = rescue from someone's control.
- **Gen 14:20** — "which hath delivered thine enemies into thy hand"
- **Exo 3:8** — "to deliver them out of the hand of the Egyptians"
- **Jdg 2:14** — "he delivered them into the hands of spoilers"
- **2 Sam 24:14** — "let us fall now into the hand of the LORD; for his mercies are great"

### 4. Divine Action ("hand of the LORD")
God's hand represents his direct, active intervention — in power, judgment, prophetic inspiration, or blessing.
- **Exo 9:3** — "the hand of the LORD is upon thy cattle"
- **1 Ki 18:46** — "the hand of the LORD was on Elijah; and he girded up his loins, and ran"
- **2 Ki 3:15** — "the hand of the LORD came upon him" (prophetic inspiration)
- **Eze 1:3** — "the hand of the LORD was there upon me"

### 5. Agency and Instrumentality ("by the hand of")
The mediating agent through whom something is accomplished — especially prophets, leaders, and messengers.
- **Exo 9:35** — "as the LORD had spoken by the hand of Moses"
- **Lev 8:36** — "all things which the LORD commanded by the hand of Moses"
- **2 Sam 11:14** — "David wrote a letter to Joab, and sent it by the hand of Uriah"
- **1 Ki 2:25** — "king Solomon sent by the hand of Benaiah"

### 6. Work, Labor, Craftsmanship
The hand as instrument of productive effort — farming, building, artistry.
- **Gen 5:29** — "our work and toil of our hands"
- **Deut 2:7** — "the LORD thy God hath blessed thee in all the works of thy hand"
- **Exo 35:25** — "all the women that were wise hearted did spin with their hands"
- **Isa 64:8** — "we are the clay, and thou our potter; and we all are the work of thy hand"

### 7. Ritual Laying On / Consecration
Placing hands on sacrificial animals, persons being ordained, or the scapegoat — transferring identity, guilt, or authority.
- **Lev 1:4** — "he shall put his hand upon the head of the burnt offering"
- **Lev 16:21** — "Aaron shall lay both his hands upon the head of the live goat, and confess over him all the iniquities"
- **Num 27:18** — "lay thine hand upon him" (commissioning Joshua)
- **Deut 34:9** — "Moses had laid his hands upon him"

### 8. Direction, Side, Border
Spatial reference — beside, alongside, boundary.
- **Num 34:3** — "your south quarter shall be from the wilderness of Zin along by the coast of Edom"
- **Deut 23:12** — "Thou shalt have a place also without the camp"
- **2 Ki 8:20** — "Edom revolted from under the hand of Judah"
- **Neh 3:12** — "next unto him repaired Shallum"
