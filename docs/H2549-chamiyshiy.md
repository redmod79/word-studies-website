## Semantic Categories

### 1. Restitution Fraction — "Add the Fifth Part" (20% Surcharge)

The distinctive legal use. In cultic and civil restitution law, the offender restores the principal plus a *chamiyshiy* (one-fifth / 20%) surcharge.

- **Lev 5:16** — "And he shall make amends for the harm that he hath done in the holy thing, and shall add the fifth part thereto, and give it unto the priest"
- **Lev 6:5** — "he shall even restore it in the principal, and shall add the fifth part more thereto, and give it unto him to whom it appertaineth, in the day of his trespass offering"
- **Num 5:7** — "he shall recompense his trespass with the principal thereof, and add unto it the fifth part thereof, and give it unto him against whom he hath trespassed"
- **Lev 22:14** — "if a man eat of the holy thing unwittingly, then he shall put the fifth part thereof unto it, and shall give it unto the priest"

This fractional use also appears in redemption of vows and dedications:
- **Lev 27:13** — "if he will at all redeem it, then he shall add a fifth part thereof unto thy estimation"
- **Lev 27:15, 19, 27, 31** — same formula for redeeming houses, fields, unclean beasts, and tithes

### 2. Chronological Marker — "The Fifth Day/Month/Year"

The majority use: marking time in dates and narrative chronology.

- **Gen 1:23** — "And the evening and the morning were the fifth day" (creation narrative)
- **2 Ki 25:8** — "in the fifth month, on the seventh day of the month... came Nebuzaradan... and burnt the house of the LORD"
- **Jer 36:9** — "in the fifth year of Jehoiakim... in the ninth month, they proclaimed a fast"
- **Eze 1:2** — "In the fifth day of the month, which was the fifth year of king Jehoiachin's captivity"

### 3. Enumeration / Birth-Order / Roster Position

Ordinal ranking in genealogies, tribal allotments, and priestly courses.

- **Gen 30:17** — "she conceived, and bare Jacob the fifth son"
- **2 Sam 3:4** — "the fifth, Shephatiah the son of Abital"
- **1 Chr 2:14** — "Nethaneel the fourth, Raddai the fifth"
- **1 Chr 24:9** — "The fifth to Malchijah, the sixth to Mijamin"

### 4. Taxation / Economic Portion

- **Gen 47:24** — "ye shall give the fifth part unto Pharaoh, and four parts shall be your own" (Joseph's 20% tax on Egyptian harvests)

### 5. Architectural Proportion

- **1 Ki 6:31** — "the lintel and side posts were a fifth part of the wall" (Solomon's temple doorframe)

### 6. Fasting Calendar — "The Fast of the Fifth Month"

- **Zec 7:3, 5** — "Should I weep in the fifth month...?" / "When ye fasted and mourned in the fifth and seventh months"
- **Zec 8:19** — "the fast of the fifth... shall be to the house of Judah joy and gladness"

The fifth-month fast commemorated the destruction of the temple (2 Ki 25:8).
