## Roles in the Concept

- **The person:** melek H4428 / H4430 — the king who holds the kingdom
- **The act:** malak H4427 — the verbal act of reigning
- **The abstract dominion:** malkûw H4437 / malkûwth H4438 — the reign/rule in the abstract; what is given, taken, established, or eternal
- **The territorial realm:** mamlâkâh H4467 — the political-geographic kingdom; what is divided or conquered
- **Greek counterpart:** basileía G932 — bridges all senses in LXX and NT
