# WG: Crown / Consecration

## Overview
Hebrew has multiple words translated "crown" in the KJV, each carrying distinct connotations. The nazar word family (H5144, H5145, H5139) centers on consecration and separation — the crown as marker of being set apart. The remaining crown words (H5850 atarah, H3804 kether, H2213 zer) denote different physical or honorific headpieces without the inherent consecration meaning.

## Roles in the Concept

- **Root action:** nazar (H5144) — the act of separating/consecrating

- **Consecrated person:** naziyr (H5139) — the one who bears the nezer
