## Semantic Categories

### 1. Cornet — Musical Signal Instrument (Dan 3)
The qeren heads the orchestra list commanded for Nebuchadnezzar's golden-image dedication. Hearing it is the cue for universal prostration. The instrument is a literal animal horn used as a wind instrument (ram's horn / signal trumpet).

- **Dan 3:5** — "at what time ye hear the sound of the cornet, flute, harp, sackbut, psaltery, dulcimer, and all kinds of musick, ye fall down and worship the golden image"
- **Dan 3:7** — "when all the people heard the sound of the cornet... all the people, the nations, and the languages, fell down [and] worshipped"
- **Dan 3:10** — "every man that shall hear the sound of the cornet... shall fall down and worship the golden image"
- **Dan 3:15** — "if ye be ready that at what time ye hear the sound of the cornet... ye fall down and worship the image which I have made"

### 2. Prophetic Symbol — Horns as Kingdoms / Kings (Dan 7)
In the night visions, the horns of the fourth beast represent successive kingdoms and rulers. The "little horn" that uproots three and makes war with the saints is the focal prophetic symbol of Dan 7.

- **Dan 7:7** — "a fourth beast... and it had ten horns"
- **Dan 7:8** — "I considered the horns, and, behold, there came up among them another little horn, before whom there were three of the first horns plucked up by the roots: and, behold, in this horn [were] eyes like the eyes of man, and a mouth speaking great things"
- **Dan 7:21** — "the same horn made war with the saints, and prevailed against them"
- **Dan 7:24** — "the ten horns out of this kingdom [are] ten kings that shall arise: and another shall rise after them; and he shall be diverse from the first, and he shall subdue three kings"
