## Lexicon Definition

From κατά (down) + αἱρέω (take): to take down, lower, pull down, cast down. Used literally of taking a body down from a cross/tree, pulling down structures (barns, walls), and figuratively of deposing rulers from seats of power, demolishing strongholds/imaginations, or destroying nations in conquest. Covers both physical descent/removal and violent demolition.
