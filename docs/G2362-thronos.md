## Semantic Categories

### 1. God's Throne in Heaven (Divine Sovereignty)
The dominant use in Revelation: heaven as the seat of God's cosmic authority, surrounded by living creatures and elders.
- **Rev 4:2** — "a throne was set in heaven, and [one] sat on the throne"
- **Heb 1:8** — "Thy throne, O God, is for ever and ever: a sceptre of righteousness is the sceptre of thy kingdom"
- **Mat 5:34** — "neither by heaven; for it is God's throne"
- **Acts 7:49** — "Heaven is my throne, and earth is my footstool" (quoting Isa 66:1)

### 2. The Messianic / Davidic Throne
Jesus is promised, inherits, and occupies the eternal Davidic throne; divine and messianic thrones merge in Rev 22.
- **Luk 1:32** — "the Lord God shall give unto him the throne of his father David"
- **Acts 2:30** — "knowing that God had sworn...he would raise up Christ to sit on his throne"
- **Rev 3:21** — "I also overcame, and am set down with my Father in his throne"
- **Rev 22:1,3** — "the throne of God and of the Lamb" — final union of divine and Davidic thrones

### 3. The Throne of Grace (Priestly Access)
Hebrews reframes the heavenly throne as the locus of mercy and intercession, approachable by the high priest.
- **Heb 4:16** — "come boldly unto the throne of grace, that we may obtain mercy"
- **Heb 8:1** — "set on the right hand of the throne of the Majesty in the heavens"
- **Heb 12:2** — "endured the cross...is set down at the right hand of the throne of God"

### 4. The Judgment Throne
The throne as seat of final adjudication over individuals and nations.
- **Mat 25:31** — "Son of man shall come in his glory...shall he sit upon the throne of his glory"
- **Rev 20:4** — "I saw thrones, and they sat upon them, and judgment was given unto them"
- **Rev 20:11** — "I saw a great white throne, and him that sat on it"

### 5. Delegated Thrones (Reward for Overcomers)
Thrones promised to the apostles and redeemed as eschatological co-rulership.
- **Mat 19:28** — "ye also shall sit upon twelve thrones, judging the twelve tribes of Israel"
- **Luk 22:30** — "sit on thrones judging the twelve tribes of Israel"
- **Rev 20:4** — "I saw thrones, and they sat upon them, and judgment was given unto them"

### 6. Satan's Seat and the Beast's Throne (Counterfeit Authority)
Hostile powers receive or occupy thrones — a deliberate mirror of divine throne imagery.
- **Rev 2:13** — "where thou dwellest, even where Satan's seat is"
- **Rev 13:2** — "the dragon gave him his power, and his seat, and great authority"
- **Rev 16:10** — "the fifth angel poured out his vial upon the seat of the beast"

### 7. Cosmic Powers (Thrones as Angelic Ranks)
Plural "thrones" used abstractly for a class of celestial beings or dominion structures.
- **Col 1:16** — "whether they be thrones, or dominions, or principalities, or powers: all things were created by him, and for him"
