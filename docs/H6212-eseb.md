### 2. Food for Animals and Humans
- **Gen 1:30** — every green herb is given for meat to living creatures
- **Gen 3:18** — the man will eat "the herb of the field"
- **Gen 9:3** — moving creatures are given "even as the green herb"

### 3. Field Vegetation Under Plague or Judgment
- **Exo 9:22, 25** — hail strikes "every herb of the field"
- **Exo 10:12, 15** — locusts eat every herb left after the hail
- **Jer 12:4** — "the herbs of every field wither"
- **Isa 42:15** — YHWH dries up herbs in judgment imagery

### 4. Grass as Transience or Weakness
- **2 Kgs 19:26 / Isa 37:27** — inhabitants are "as the grass of the field"
- **Ps 92:7** — the wicked spring as grass
- **Ps 102:4, 11** — heart/life withers like grass

### 5. Rain/Dew/Renewal Imagery
- **Deut 32:2** — speech drops "as the small rain upon the tender herb"
- **Mic 5:7** — the remnant is like dew/showers on grass
- **Zech 10:1** — YHWH gives showers and grass in the field
