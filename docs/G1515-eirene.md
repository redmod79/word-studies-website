## Semantic Categories

### 1. Greeting Formula ("Grace and Peace")
Formulaic opening in Pauline and general epistles.
- **Rom 1:7** — "Grace to you and peace from God our Father, and the Lord Jesus Christ"
- **1 Cor 1:3** — "Grace be unto you, and peace, from God our Father"
- **Eph 1:2** — "Grace be to you, and peace, from God our Father"
- **1 Pet 1:2** — "Grace unto you, and peace, be multiplied"

### 2. Inner Spiritual Peace (with God)
Reconciliation and tranquility resulting from justification.
- **Rom 5:1** — "being justified by faith, we have peace with God through our Lord Jesus Christ"
- **Rom 8:6** — "to be spiritually minded is life and peace"
- **John 14:27** — "Peace I leave with you, my peace I give unto you: not as the world giveth"
- **John 16:33** — "in me ye might have peace. In the world ye shall have tribulation"

### 3. Interpersonal Harmony
Peaceful relations between people or groups.
- **Eph 2:14** — "he is our peace, who hath made both one" (Jew/Gentile unity)
- **Eph 4:3** — "keep the unity of the Spirit in the bond of peace"
- **Rom 14:19** — "follow after the things which make for peace"
- **Jas 3:18** — "the fruit of righteousness is sown in peace of them that make peace"

### 4. Dismissal/Blessing ("Go in Peace")
Formula of wellbeing at departure.
- **Mark 5:34** — "go in peace, and be whole of thy plague"
- **Luke 7:50** — "Thy faith hath saved thee; go in peace"
- **Acts 15:33** — "they were let go in peace from the brethren"
- **Luke 2:29** — "Lord, now lettest thou thy servant depart in peace"

### 5. Messianic/Eschatological Peace
Peace as characteristic of God's kingdom.
- **Luke 2:14** — "on earth peace, good will toward men"
- **Rom 14:17** — "the kingdom of God is not meat and drink; but righteousness, and peace, and joy"
- **Isa 9:6 context** — "Prince of Peace" (OT background via H7965 shalom)
- **Rev 6:4** — "power was given to him... to take peace from the earth" (eschatological removal)

### 6. Divine Attribute ("God of Peace")
Peace as a characteristic of God himself.
- **Rom 15:33** — "the God of peace be with you all"
- **Rom 16:20** — "the God of peace shall bruise Satan under your feet shortly"
- **1 Thess 5:23** — "the very God of peace sanctify you wholly"
- **Heb 13:20** — "the God of peace, that brought again from the dead our Lord Jesus"

### 7. Fruit of the Spirit
Peace as a produced character quality.
- **Gal 5:22** — "the fruit of the Spirit is love, joy, peace, longsuffering"
- **Col 3:15** — "let the peace of God rule in your hearts"
- **Phil 4:7** — "the peace of God, which passeth all understanding"
