## Roles in the Concept

- **Substance:** haima (αἷμα) — the blood itself, literal and figurative
- **Shedding Action:** ekcheo (ἐκχέω) — to pour out, shed blood
- **Shedding Event:** haimatekchysia (αἱματεκχυσία) — the shedding of blood (nominal)
- **Slaughter:** sphazo (σφάζω) — the killing that produces the blood
- **Covenant:** diatheke (διαθήκη) — the covenant/testament ratified by blood
- **Problem Addressed:** hamartia (ἁμαρτία) — sin, which blood atones for
- **Paired Element (body):** sarx (σάρξ) — flesh, paired in "flesh and blood"
- **Paired Element (purity):** hudor (ὕδωρ) — water, paired in purification and witness
