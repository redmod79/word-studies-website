## Semantic Categories

### 1. Physical/Biological Death
The cessation of bodily life — whether natural, violent, or judicial.

- **Mat 10:21** — "the brother shall deliver up the brother to death" (martyrdom/betrayal)
- **Php 2:8** — "became obedient unto death, even the death of the cross"
- **Heb 2:9** — "for the suffering of death, crowned with glory and honour"
- **John 11:4** — "This sickness is not unto death, but for the glory of God"

### 2. Death as Consequence of Sin
Paul's theological construct: death entered through Adam's sin and spreads universally.

- **Rom 5:12** — "death by sin; and so death passed upon all men, for that all have sinned"
- **Rom 6:23** — "the wages of sin is death; but the gift of God is eternal life"
- **James 1:15** — "sin, when it is finished, bringeth forth death"

### 3. Death as Reigning Cosmic Power
Thanatos as active sovereign force dominating the age from Adam onward.

- **Rom 5:14** — "death reigned from Adam to Moses"
- **Rom 5:17** — "by one man's offence death reigned"
- **1 Cor 15:26** — "The last enemy that shall be destroyed is death"
- **Rom 8:38** — "neither death, nor life...shall be able to separate us from the love of God"

### 4. Christ's Death as Atoning Event
The specific historical death of Christ as the mechanism of redemption.

- **Rom 5:10** — "reconciled to God by the death of his Son"
- **Col 1:22** — "In the body of his flesh through death, to present you holy and unblameable"
- **Heb 9:15** — "by means of death, for the redemption of the transgressions"
- **Heb 2:14** — "that through death he might destroy him that had the power of death"

### 5. Spiritual Death / Passage from Death to Life
Death as a present spiritual state from which believers pass through faith.

- **John 5:24** — "is passed from death unto life" (present tense passage)
- **1 John 3:14** — "We know that we have passed from death unto life, because we love the brethren"
- **Rom 8:6** — "to be carnally minded is death; but to be spiritually minded is life and peace"

### 6. Death Personified / The Second Death
Revelation depicts thanatos as a named rider and a final eschatological category.

- **Rev 6:8** — "his name that sat on him was Death, and Hell followed with him"
- **Rev 20:14** — "death and hell were cast into the lake of fire. This is the second death."
- **Rev 21:4** — "there shall be no more death, neither sorrow, nor crying"

### 7. Shadow of Death (OT Allusion)
NT quotations echoing the OT צַלְמָוֶת (tsalmaveth) — "shadow of death" texts.

- **Mat 4:16** — "them which sat in the region and shadow of death light is sprung up" (quoting Isa 9:2)
- **Luke 1:79** — "To give light to them that sit in darkness and in the shadow of death" (Zechariah's canticle)
