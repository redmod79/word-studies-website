## Semantic Categories

### 1. Foundational Principle of Wisdom
The signature use — yirah as the prerequisite and starting-point of true knowledge and wisdom.
- **Prov 1:7** — "The fear of the LORD is the beginning of knowledge: but fools despise wisdom and instruction."
- **Prov 9:10** — "The fear of the LORD is the beginning of wisdom: and the knowledge of the holy is understanding."
- **Prov 15:33** — "The fear of the LORD is the instruction of wisdom; and before honour is humility."
- **Psalm 111:10** — "The fear of the LORD is the beginning of wisdom: a good understanding have all they that do his commandments."
- **Job 28:28** — "Behold, the fear of the Lord, that is wisdom; and to depart from evil is understanding."

### 2. Moral Compass — Turning from Evil
Yirah as a disposition that actively repels sin and evil conduct.
- **Prov 8:13** — "The fear of the LORD is to hate evil: pride, and arrogancy, and the evil way, and the froward mouth, do I hate."
- **Prov 16:6** — "By mercy and truth iniquity is purged: and by the fear of the LORD men depart from evil."
- **Ex 20:20** — "God is come to prove you, and that his fear may be before your faces, that ye sin not."
- **Neh 5:9** — "Ought ye not to walk in the fear of our God because of the reproach of the heathen our enemies?"

### 3. Source of Life, Security, and Blessing
Yirah as the condition that yields protection, longevity, and provision.
- **Prov 14:26** — "In the fear of the LORD is strong confidence: and his children shall have a place of refuge."
- **Prov 14:27** — "The fear of the LORD is a fountain of life, to depart from the snares of death."
- **Prov 10:27** — "The fear of the LORD prolongeth days: but the years of the wicked shall be shortened."
- **Prov 19:23** — "The fear of the LORD tendeth to life: and he that hath it shall abide satisfied."
- **Prov 22:4** — "By humility and the fear of the LORD are riches, and honour, and life."

### 4. Standard for Just Rule and Conduct
Yirah as the governing principle for leaders and faithful living.
- **2 Sam 23:3** — "He that ruleth over men must be just, ruling in the fear of God."
- **2 Chr 19:9** — "Thus shall ye do in the fear of the LORD, faithfully, and with a perfect heart."
- **Neh 5:15** — "But so did not I, because of the fear of God."
- **Prov 23:17** — "Be thou in the fear of the LORD all the day long."

### 5. Messianic Endowment
Yirah as a quality of the Spirit resting on the coming Branch.
- **Isa 11:2** — "The spirit of the LORD shall rest upon him, the spirit of wisdom and understanding, the spirit of counsel and might, the spirit of knowledge and of the fear of the LORD."
- **Isa 11:3** — "And shall make him of quick understanding in the fear of the LORD."
- **Isa 33:6** — "Wisdom and knowledge shall be the stability of thy times, and strength of salvation: the fear of the LORD is his treasure."

### 6. Dread / Overwhelming Fear (rare)
A small cluster where yirah denotes raw terror rather than reverent awe.
- **Psalm 55:5** — "Fearfulness and trembling are come upon me, and horror hath overwhelmed me."
- **Ezek 1:18** — "As for their rings, they were so high that they were dreadful."
- **Deut 2:25** — "This day will I begin to put the dread of thee and the fear of thee upon the nations."

### 7. Covenant Promise
Yirah as something God himself plants in his people's hearts.
- **Jer 32:40** — "I will put my fear in their hearts, that they shall not depart from me."
- **Isa 63:17** — "O LORD, why hast thou… hardened our heart from thy fear?"
