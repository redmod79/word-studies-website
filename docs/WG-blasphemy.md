## Roles in the Concept

- **Action:** βλασφημέω (G987) — the verbal act of reviling, defaming, or speaking impiously
- **Content:** βλασφημία (G988) — what is spoken: the blasphemous words or charge
- **Character:** βλάσφημος (G989) — describing the person or speech as blasphemous
- **Related acts:** κακολογέω (G2551) — cursing; καταλαλέω (G2635) — slandering
- **Archetype:** διάβολος (G1228) — the slanderer par excellence
