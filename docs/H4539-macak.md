The LXX's use of katapetasma for both macak and poreketh collapses a distinction the Hebrew preserves: macak = outer entrance screen, poreketh = inner dividing veil. This conflation matters for NT interpretation, since the Gospels' "veil of the temple was rent" (katapetasma, Matt 27:51) could in principle refer to either curtain, though context and Hebrews 6:19, 9:3, 10:20 identify the inner veil (poreketh).
