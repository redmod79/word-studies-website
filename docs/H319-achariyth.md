## Semantic Categories

### 1. Eschatological Future — "The Latter Days"
The phrase *beʼachărîyth hayyâmîm* ("in the latter days / last days") is a fixed prophetic formula pointing to an indeterminate future period.
- **Gen 49:1** — "that I may tell you that which shall befall you in the last days"
- **Deut 4:30** — "when thou art in tribulation... in the latter days, if thou turn to the LORD"
- **Deut 31:29** — "evil will befall you in the latter days; because ye will do evil"
- **Isa 2:2** — "in the last days, the mountain of the LORD's house shall be established"
- **Dan 10:14** — "to make thee understand what shall befall thy people in the latter days"
- **Mic 4:1** — "in the last days... the mountain of the house of the LORD shall be established"
- **Hos 3:5** — "shall fear the LORD and his goodness in the latter days"
- **Jer 23:20** — "in the latter days ye shall consider it perfectly"
- **Jer 48:47** — "I will bring again the captivity of Moab in the latter days"
- **Jer 49:39** — "in the latter days, I will bring again the captivity of Elam"
- **Ezek 38:16** — "it shall be in the latter days, and I will bring thee against my land"

### 2. Outcome / Consequence of a Way of Life
Especially dominant in Proverbs — the inevitable result that follows from choices.
- **Prov 5:4** — "her end is bitter as wormwood, sharp as a twoedged sword"
- **Prov 14:12** — "the end thereof are the ways of death"
- **Prov 16:25** — "the end thereof are the ways of death"
- **Prov 20:21** — "the end thereof shall not be blessed"
- **Prov 23:18** — "surely there is an end; and thine expectation shall not be cut off"
- **Eccl 7:8** — "better is the end of a thing than the beginning thereof"

### 3. Personal Destiny / Latter Condition
The final state of an individual or group — how things turn out for them.
- **Job 8:7** — "thy latter end should greatly increase"
- **Job 42:12** — "the LORD blessed the latter end of Job more than his beginning"
- **Psalm 37:37–38** — "the end of that man is peace... the end of the wicked shall be cut off"
- **Psalm 73:17** — "then understood I their end"
- **Deut 32:20** — "I will see what their end shall be"
- **Deut 32:29** — "that they would consider their latter end!"
- **Lam 1:9** — "she remembereth not her last end"

### 4. Posterity / Future Descendants
The word extends temporally from the person to what remains after them — offspring, legacy.
- **Psalm 109:13** — "let his posterity be cut off"
- **Num 24:20** — "his latter end shall be that he perish for ever"
- **Jer 31:17** — "there is hope in thine end... thy children shall come again"
- **Dan 11:4** — "his kingdom shall be... not according to his dominion... nor to his posterity"

### 5. Eschatological Endpoint — End of Indignation / End of the Vision
Specifically in Daniel, the concluding phase of a prophetic period.
- **Dan 8:19** — "I will make thee know what shall be in the last end of the indignation"
- **Dan 8:23** — "in the latter time of their kingdom, when the transgressors are come to the full"
- **Dan 12:8** — "what shall be the end of these things?"

### 6. Spatial Extremity
Rare usage for the farthest physical extent.
- **Psalm 139:9** — "dwell in the uttermost parts of the sea"
- **Amos 9:1** — "I will slay the last of them with the sword"
- **Jer 50:12** — "the hindermost of the nations"

### 7. Divine Knowledge of the Future
God's sovereign awareness of end from beginning.
- **Isa 41:22** — "let them shew... what shall happen... that we may consider... the latter end of them"
- **Isa 46:10** — "declaring the end from the beginning"
- **Isa 47:7** — "thou didst not... remember the latter end of it"
