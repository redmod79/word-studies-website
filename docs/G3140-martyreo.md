## Semantic Categories

### 1. Bearing Witness to Christ's Identity and Mission
The dominant usage — testifying about who Jesus is and what he came to do.
- **John 1:7** — "The same came for a witness, to bear witness of the Light"
- **John 5:36** — "the works which the Father hath given me to finish, the same works that I do, bear witness of me"
- **John 5:37** — "the Father himself, which hath sent me, hath borne witness of me"
- **1 John 4:14** — "we have seen and do testify that the Father sent the Son to be the Saviour of the world"

### 2. Eyewitness Testimony (Declaring What Was Seen/Heard)
The word carries forensic force — testifying to observed facts.
- **John 3:11** — "We speak that we do know, and testify that we have seen"
- **John 3:32** — "what he hath seen and heard, that he testifieth"
- **John 19:35** — "he that saw it bare record, and his record is true"
- **1 John 1:2** — "the life was manifested, and we have seen it, and bear witness"

### 3. The Spirit's Witness
The Holy Spirit as active testifier — to, about, or alongside believers.
- **John 15:26** — "the Spirit of truth... shall bear witness of me"
- **1 John 5:6** — "it is the Spirit that beareth witness, because the Spirit is truth"
- **1 John 5:7-8** — "there are three that bear record... the Spirit, and the water, and the blood"
- **Acts 15:8** — "God, which knoweth the hearts, bare them witness, giving them the Holy Ghost"

### 4. Scripture / Prophetic Testimony
The law, prophets, and Scripture bearing witness as a body of testimony.
- **John 5:39** — "the scriptures... they are they which testify of me"
- **Acts 10:43** — "To him give all the prophets witness"
- **Romans 3:21** — "the righteousness of God... being witnessed by the law and the prophets"
- **Hebrews 7:17** — "For he testifieth, Thou art a priest for ever"

### 5. Character Witness / Good Reputation
Passive forms: a person "being witnessed about" — attested by others.
- **Acts 6:3** — "seven men of honest report, full of the Holy Ghost"
- **Acts 10:22** — "Cornelius the centurion, a just man... of good report among all the nation"
- **Acts 16:2** — "which was well reported of by the brethren"
- **1 Timothy 5:10** — "Well reported of for good works"
- **Hebrews 11:2** — "by it the elders obtained a good report"

### 6. Faith-Heroes Attested by God
Concentrated in Hebrews 11 — God's approval witnessed through faith.
- **Hebrews 11:4** — "Abel... obtained witness that he was righteous, God testifying of his gifts"
- **Hebrews 11:5** — "Enoch... had this testimony, that he pleased God"
- **Hebrews 11:39** — "these all, having obtained a good report through faith"

### 7. Apostolic / Personal Testimony
Paul and other apostles personally attesting to what they know.
- **Acts 23:11** — "as thou hast testified of me in Jerusalem, so must thou bear witness also at Rome"
- **Acts 26:22** — "witnessing both to small and great"
- **Romans 10:2** — "I bear them record that they have a zeal of God"
- **3 John 1:12** — "Demetrius hath good report of all men, and of the truth itself"
