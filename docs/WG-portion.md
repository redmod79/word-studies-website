## Roles in the Concept

- **General share:** cheleq (חֵלֶק) — one's allotted portion of anything (land, spoils, labor, God himself)
- **Permanent inheritance:** nachalah (נַחֲלָה) — inalienable family estate passed through generations
- **Specific field:** chelqah (חֶלְקָה) — a concrete parcel of agricultural land
- **Assigned ration:** manah (מָנָה) — a counted-out or weighed portion given to someone
- **Providential allotment:** menath (מְנָת) — share assigned by law, courtesy, or divine decree
- **Measured territory:** chebel (חֶבֶל) — land measured by surveyor's cord; also one's "lot" in life
