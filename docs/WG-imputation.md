# WG: Imputation

## Overview
The imputation concept in the NT involves the crediting or charging of a quality (righteousness or sin) to a person's account. The group centers on λογίζομαι (logizomai) as the action verb and clusters with the objects being imputed (righteousness, sin), the basis of imputation (faith), the verdict it effects (justification), and a cognate accounting term (ellogeō).
