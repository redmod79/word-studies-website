## Roles in the Concept

- **Core quality:** yashar (יָשָׁר) — the directness and straightness of character; root metaphor is a path without crookedness
- **Legal standard:** tsaddiyq (צַדִּיק) — conformity to the covenantal/relational norm; measured against an external standard
- **Completeness:** tam (תָּם) / tamiym (תָּמִים) — wholeness and integrity; nothing lacking, nothing blemished
- **General goodness:** towb (טוֹב) — the broadest positive quality; frequently paired with yashar to form the hendiadys "right and good"
- **Root action:** yashar (יָשַׁר) verb — the act of being or making straight; provides the verbal basis for the concept
