## Lexicon Definition

A strengthened form of G3199 (melo, "to be of interest") through the idea of expectation; to intend, i.e. be about to be, do, or suffer something. Often used with an infinitive to express imminence, intention, or certainty of a future action.

- **Rev 10:4** — "I was about to write: and I heard a voice from heaven"

### 2. Destined / Divinely Appointed Future
