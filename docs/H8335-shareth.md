## Semantic Categories

With only two occurrences, the data supports two concrete usage categories rather than a larger taxonomy.

### 1. Sanctuary Instruments Used in Holy-Site Ministry
- **Num 4:12** — "And they shall take all the **instruments of ministry**, wherewith they minister in the sanctuary, and put [them] in a cloth of blue..."

Here *shârêth* labels the ministry-character of the tabernacle implements. The verse pairs the noun with the cognate verb *yᵉshârᵉtû* (H8334, "they minister"), so the objects are "instruments of service" because they are used in sanctuary ministry.

### 2. Temple Vessels Prepared for Ongoing Worship Service
- **2 Chr 24:14** — "whereof were made vessels for the house of the LORD, [even] vessels **to minister**, and to offer [withal], and spoons, and vessels of gold and silver..."

Here *shârêth* qualifies restored temple vessels. The immediate context includes the house of YHWH, offering activity, gold and silver vessels, and continual burnt offerings.
