## Semantic Categories

### 1. Inheriting the Kingdom of God
The dominant NT usage — eschatological entry into God's kingdom, stated both positively and negatively.
- **Matt 25:34** — "Come, ye blessed of my Father, inherit the kingdom prepared for you from the foundation of the world"
- **1 Cor 6:9** — "Know ye not that the unrighteous shall not inherit the kingdom of God?"
- **1 Cor 6:10** — "Nor thieves, nor covetous, nor drunkards, nor revilers, nor extortioners, shall inherit the kingdom of God."
- **Gal 5:21** — "they which do such things shall not inherit the kingdom of God"

### 2. Inheriting Eternal Life
Used in the rich-ruler question across the Synoptics — "what shall I do to inherit eternal life?"
- **Matt 19:29** — "every one that hath forsaken houses... shall receive an hundredfold, and shall inherit everlasting life"
- **Mark 10:17** — "Good Master, what shall I do that I may inherit eternal life?"
- **Luke 10:25** — "Master, what shall I do to inherit eternal life?"
- **Luke 18:18** — "Good Master, what shall I do to inherit eternal life?"

### 3. Inheriting Promises, Blessings, and "All Things"
Broader eschatological reception — what the faithful receive by divine appointment.
- **Heb 6:12** — "followers of them who through faith and patience inherit the promises"
- **Heb 12:17** — "afterward, when he would have inherited the blessing, he was rejected"
- **1 Pet 3:9** — "ye are thereunto called, that ye should inherit a blessing"
- **Rev 21:7** — "He that overcometh shall inherit all things"

### 4. Inheriting the Earth
Beatitude promise echoing Psalm 37:11.
- **Matt 5:5** — "Blessed are the meek: for they shall inherit the earth"

### 5. Christ's Inherited Status
Applied uniquely to Christ's superiority over angels.
- **Heb 1:4** — "as he hath by inheritance obtained a more excellent name than they"
- **Heb 1:14** — "ministering spirits, sent forth to minister for them who shall be heirs of salvation"

### 6. Covenant Inheritance — Free vs. Slave
Allegory of Sarah and Hagar applied to inheritance rights.
- **Gal 4:30** — "the son of the bondwoman shall not be heir with the son of the freewoman"
- **1 Cor 15:50** — "flesh and blood cannot inherit the kingdom of God; neither doth corruption inherit incorruption"
