## Lexicon Definition

From G3624 (oikos) and the base of G3551 (nomos); a house-distributor (i.e. manager), or overseer, i.e. an employee in that capacity; by extension, a fiscal agent (treasurer); figuratively, a preacher (of the Gospel).
