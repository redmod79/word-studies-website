## Continuity and Shifts

**What stays the same across testaments:**
- The core meaning (deliberate, calculated deception) remains stable from OT mirmah through LXX dolos to NT dolos
- The tongue/mouth/speech association persists: Psa 34:13 → 1 Pet 3:10; Isa 53:9 → 1 Pet 2:22
- The "absence of deceit" as a mark of the righteous continues: Psa 24:4, Isa 53:9 → John 1:47, 1 Pet 2:22, Rev 14:5

**What shifts:**
- The OT commercial-fraud pole (false balances in Proverbs, Hosea, Amos, Micah) has no NT counterpart in dolos usage
- The OT political-treachery sense (Dan 8:25, 11:23, 2 Ki 9:23) narrows in the NT to plotting against Jesus (Matt 26:4, Mark 14:1)
- NT dolos appears more frequently in vice lists (Mark 7:22, Rom 1:29, 1 Pet 2:1) than OT mirmah does
