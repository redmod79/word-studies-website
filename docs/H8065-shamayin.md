## Lexicon Definition

> (Aramaic) corresponding to [H8064, שָׁמַיִם shamayim]:—heaven.

The Aramaic cognate of Hebrew שָׁמַיִם. Morphologically a dual/plural form, functioning as a singular concept ("the heavens" / "heaven"). Appears only in the Aramaic sections of the OT (Ezra 4:8-6:18, 7:12-26; Daniel 2:4b-7:28; one verse of Jeremiah).

## Semantic Categories

### 1. Divine Title — "God/Lord/King of Heaven"
The most frequent usage. A formal epithet distinguishing YHWH from terrestrial deities, common in Persian-period official speech.
- **Ezr 5:11** — "We are the servants of the God of heaven and earth"
- **Ezr 7:12** — "scribe of the law of the God of heaven"
- **Dan 2:18** — "desire mercies of the God of heaven concerning this secret"
- **Dan 5:23** — "lifted up thyself against the Lord of heaven"

### 2. Spatial Sphere — Locus of Divine Action and Revelation
Heaven as the place from which God speaks, reveals, and acts.
- **Dan 2:28** — "there is a God in heaven that revealeth secrets"
- **Dan 4:13** — "a watcher and an holy one came down from heaven"
- **Dan 4:31** — "there fell a voice from heaven"
- **Dan 6:27** — "he worketh signs and wonders in heaven and in earth"

### 3. Visible Sky — Meteorological / Directional
The physical expanse: clouds, winds, dew, fowl.
- **Dan 2:38** — "the fowls of the heaven"
- **Dan 4:12, 21** — "the fowls of the heaven dwelt in the boughs"
- **Dan 4:33** — "wet with the dew of heaven"
- **Dan 7:2** — "the four winds of the heaven strove upon the great sea"

### 4. Vertical Extreme — Idiomatic Height/Reach
Heaven as the upper limit, used to express cosmic magnitude or arrogance.
- **Dan 4:11** — "the height thereof reached unto heaven"
- **Dan 4:20** — "whose height reached unto the heaven"
- **Dan 4:22** — "thy greatness is grown, and reacheth unto heaven"
- **Dan 4:34** — "I Nebuchadnezzar lifted up mine eyes unto heaven"

### 5. Apocalyptic Source — Throne of the Son of Man
Distinctive Danielic usage: heaven as the origin of eschatological rule.
- **Dan 7:13** — "one like the Son of man came with the clouds of heaven"
- **Dan 7:27** — "the kingdom... under the whole heaven, shall be given to... the saints"
- **Dan 2:44** — "shall the God of heaven set up a kingdom"

### 6. Polemical Contrast — Creator vs. Idols
- **Jer 10:11** — "The gods that have not made the heavens and the earth... shall perish"
