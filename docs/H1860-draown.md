## Semantic Categories

### 1. Object of Horror — Eschatological Corpses

The redeemed behold the carcasses of transgressors; the word names what the transgressors *become* — something that causes active revulsion in those who see them.

- **Isa 66:24** — "And they shall go forth, and look upon the carcases of the men that have transgressed against me: for their worm shall not die, neither shall their fire be quenched; and they shall be an abhorring unto all flesh."

### 2. Everlasting Contempt — Resurrection of the Wicked

The only OT text explicitly naming two resurrection outcomes. דְּרָאוֹן is the named condition of those who awake to judgment — paired with "shame" (H2781 חֶרְפָּה).

- **Dan 12:2** — "And many of them that sleep in the dust of the earth shall awake, some to everlasting life, and some to shame [and] everlasting contempt."
