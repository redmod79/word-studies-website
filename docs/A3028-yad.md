## Semantic Categories

### 1. Divine Sovereignty and Control
- **Dan 4:35** — "none can stay his hand, or say unto him, What doest thou?" — God's unrestrained sovereign action
- **Dan 5:23** — "the God in whose hand thy breath is, and whose are all thy ways" — God holds human life
- **Dan 5:5** — "came forth fingers of a man's hand, and wrote over against the candlestick" — divine judgment written by a supernatural hand
- **Dan 5:24** — "Then was the part of the hand sent from him; and this writing was written" — the hand as agent of divine verdict

### 2. Transfer of Dominion / Political Authority
- **Dan 2:38** — "hath he given into thine hand, and hath made thee ruler over them all" — God granting Nebuchadnezzar authority
- **Ezr 5:12** — "he gave them into the hand of Nebuchadnezzar the king of Babylon" — God transferring Israel to a conqueror
- **Dan 7:25** — "they shall be given into his hand until a time and times and the dividing of time" — saints delivered to the little horn's power

### 3. Deliverance from Power
- **Dan 3:15** — "who is that God that shall deliver you out of my hands?" — Nebuchadnezzar's challenge
- **Dan 3:17** — "he will deliver us out of thine hand, O king" — confidence in God's deliverance
- **Dan 6:27** — "who hath delivered Daniel from the power of the lions" — "power" (yad) as capacity to harm

### 4. Possession and Authority over Torah/Wisdom
- **Ezr 7:14** — "the law of thy God which is in thine hand" — Torah as entrusted possession
- **Ezr 7:25** — "the wisdom of thy God, that is in thine hand" — divine wisdom held by Ezra

### 5. Human Labor and Action
- **Ezr 5:8** — "this work goeth fast on, and prospereth in their hands" — construction labor
- **Ezr 6:12** — "that shall put to their hand to alter and to destroy this house" — interference, hostile action

### 6. Absence of Human Agency
- **Dan 2:34** — "a stone was cut out without hands" — divine action excluding human agency
- **Dan 2:45** — "the stone was cut out of the mountain without hands" — same formula, emphasizing supernatural origin of God's kingdom
