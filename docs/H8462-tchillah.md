## Lexicon Definition

From H2490 in the sense of opening; a commencement; relatively original (adverb, -ly): begin(-ning), first (time).

The root H2490 (châlal) is a primitive root meaning "to bore, to open" — and by extension "to begin" (as if making an opening). The noun tᵉchillâh captures the concrete sense of "the starting point" of an event, period, or narrative sequence. Used adverbially with prepositions (בַּ, מִן, כַּ) to mean "at the beginning," "from the beginning," or "as at the beginning."
