# G615 — ἀποκτείνω (apokteinō)

## Core Data

- **Original:** ἀποκτείνω

- **Part of Speech:** Verb
- **Root:** Compound of ἀπό (apo, G575, "from, away from") + κτείνω (kteinō, "to slay"); the prepositional prefix intensifies, yielding "to kill outright, to put to death"

- **Hapax Legomenon:** No

## Lexicon Definition

From ἀπό (apo) and κτείνω (kteinō, "to slay"); to kill outright; figuratively, to destroy, to abolish, to extinguish. Used of putting to death by any means — sword, stoning, execution, judicial killing, martyrdom, or divine judgment. Occasionally figurative ("the letter killeth," Rom 7:11; 2 Cor 3:6).
