## Lexicon Definition

From H7321; clamor, i.e. acclamation of joy or a battle-cry; especially clangor of trumpets, as an alarm: alarm, blow(-ing) (of, the) (trumpets), joy, jubile, loud noise, rejoicing, shout(-ing), (high, joyful) sound(-ing).
