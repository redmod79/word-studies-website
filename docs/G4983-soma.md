## Semantic Categories

### 1. Physical Human Body (Literal)
The body as the material form of a person — what can be clothed, fed, injured, or killed.
- **Mat 5:29** — "thy whole body should be cast into hell"
- **Mat 6:22** — "The light of the body is the eye: if therefore thine eye be single, thy whole body shall be full of light"
- **Luk 12:22** — "Take no thought...neither for the body, what ye shall put on"
- **1Th 5:23** — "your whole spirit and soul and body be preserved blameless"

### 2. Dead Body / Corpse
The body after death — awaiting burial or resurrection.
- **Mat 27:58–59** — "Joseph...begged the body of Jesus...and wrapped it in a clean linen cloth"
- **Act 9:40** — "Peter...turning [him] to the body said, Tabitha, arise"
- **Jde 1:9** — Michael disputed with the devil about the body of Moses

### 3. Christ's Body — Incarnate, Crucified, and Eucharistic
The physical body of Jesus in the Incarnation, on the cross, and in the Eucharist.
- **Jhn 2:21** — "he spake of the temple of his body"
- **Heb 10:5** — "a body hast thou prepared me" (Psa 40:6)
- **1Pe 2:24** — "Who his own self bare our sins in his own body on the tree"
- **Mat 26:26** — "Take, eat: this is my body"
- **Luk 22:19** — "This is my body which is given for you: this do in remembrance of me"

### 4. Body Under Sin and Mortal Existence (Pauline Ethics)
The unredeemed body as a battleground — subject to sin, decay, and death; needing mortification by the Spirit.
- **Rom 6:12** — "Let not sin therefore reign in your mortal body"
- **Rom 7:24** — "O wretched man that I am! who shall deliver me from the body of this death?"
- **Rom 8:10** — "the body is dead because of sin; but the Spirit is life because of righteousness"
- **Rom 8:13** — "if ye through the Spirit do mortify the deeds of the body, ye shall live"

### 5. Body as Temple / Presented to God
The body as God's property — to be glorified, sanctified, and offered as worship.
- **1Co 6:19** — "your body is the temple of the Holy Ghost which is in you...ye are not your own"
- **1Co 6:20** — "therefore glorify God in your body"
- **Rom 12:1** — "present your bodies a living sacrifice, holy, acceptable unto God"
- **Php 1:20** — "Christ shall be magnified in my body, whether it be by life, or by death"

### 6. The Church as Corporate Body of Christ
The ekklesia as a unified body with many members, under Christ as Head.
- **1Co 12:12–13** — "as the body is one, and hath many members...so also is Christ. For by one Spirit are we all baptized into one body"
- **1Co 12:27** — "ye are the body of Christ, and members in particular"
- **Eph 1:23** — "Which is his body, the fulness of him that filleth all in all"
- **Col 1:18** — "he is the head of the body, the church"
- **Eph 4:4** — "There is one body, and one Spirit"

### 7. The Resurrection Body
The transformed body — raised, glorified, fashioned like Christ's glorious body.
- **1Co 15:44** — "It is sown a natural body; it is raised a spiritual body"
- **Rom 8:23** — "waiting for the adoption, to wit, the redemption of our body"
- **Php 3:21** — "Who shall change our vile body, that it may be fashioned like unto his glorious body"
- **2Co 5:6–8** — "whilst we are at home in the body, we are absent from the Lord... willing rather to be absent from the body, and to be present with the Lord"
