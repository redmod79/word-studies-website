## Semantic Categories

### 1. Temple Building / Main Hall
The physical sanctuary structure, often the nave or main temple building in relation to porch, doors, pillars, or inner rooms.
- **1 Samuel 3:3** — "in the temple of the LORD, where the ark of God was"
- **1 Kings 6:17** — "the house, that is, the temple before it, was forty cubits long"
- **1 Kings 7:21** — "he set up the pillars in the porch of the temple"
- **Ezekiel 41:23** — "the temple and the sanctuary had two doors"

### 2. Temple Foundation, Building, and Restoration
Use in post-destruction or restoration contexts where the sanctuary is being founded, built, or anticipated.
- **Ezra 3:10** — "when the builders laid the foundation of the temple of the LORD"
- **Haggai 2:18** — "from the day that the foundation of the LORD'S temple was laid"
- **Zechariah 6:12** — "he shall build the temple of the LORD"
- **Zechariah 6:13** — "Even he shall build the temple of the LORD"

### 3. Divine Dwelling / Holy Temple
The temple as the place from which YHWH hears, reigns, appears, or is approached in prayer.
- **2 Samuel 22:7** — "he did hear my voice out of his temple"
- **Psalm 11:4** — "The LORD is in his holy temple, the LORD'S throne is in heaven"
- **Jonah 2:7** — "my prayer came in unto thee, into thine holy temple"
- **Habakkuk 2:20** — "the LORD is in his holy temple: let all the earth keep silence before him"

### 4. Temple Defilement, Plunder, and Misplaced Confidence
Contexts where the sanctuary is polluted, emptied, destroyed, or falsely treated as a guarantee of safety.
- **2 Kings 23:4** — "bring forth out of the temple of the LORD all the vessels that were made for Baal"
- **2 Chronicles 29:16** — priests "brought out all the uncleanness that they found in the temple of the LORD"
- **Psalm 79:1** — "thy holy temple have they defiled; they have laid Jerusalem on heaps"
- **Jeremiah 7:4** — "The temple of the LORD, The temple of the LORD, The temple of the LORD"

### 5. Royal Palace / Secular Great House
The non-cultic sense: a large royal or elite building, usually rendered "palace(s)."
- **Psalm 45:15** — "they shall enter into the king's palace"
- **Proverbs 30:28** — "The spider taketh hold with her hands, and is in kings' palaces"
- **Isaiah 13:22** — "dragons in their pleasant palaces"
- **Daniel 1:4** — youths with ability "to stand in the king's palace"

### 6. Pagan Temples / Foreign Sacred Buildings
The same architectural term can describe temples belonging to nations or false worship.
- **2 Chronicles 36:7** — Nebuchadnezzar put temple vessels "in his temple at Babylon"
- **Hosea 8:14** — "Israel hath forgotten his Maker, and buildeth temples"
- **Joel 3:5** — "carried into your temples my goodly pleasant things"
