## Lexicon Definition

From G37 (hagiazo); properly, purification, i.e. (the state) purity; concretely (a purifier). The -μος suffix marks the process or result of the verbal action — the ongoing work or achieved state of being made holy.

All derive from the root ἅγος (hagos, "an awful/sacred thing"). G38 hagiasmos (-mos suffix) emphasizes the process or result of the action, while G41 (-otes) and G42 (-osyne) emphasize the abstract quality itself.
