# H1285 — בְּרִית (beriyth)

## Core Data

- **Original:** בְּרִית
- **Transliteration:** bᵉrîyth
- **Pronunciation:** ber-eeth'
- **Part of Speech:** Feminine noun
- **Root:** From H1262 (in the sense of cutting, like H1254); a compact made by passing between pieces of flesh

- **Hapax Legomenon:** No

## Lexicon Definition

A compact (because made by passing between pieces of flesh): covenant, confederacy, league. The root concept connects to the ritual of cutting animals and passing between the halves (cf. Gen 15:10, 17–18; Jer 34:18–19).
