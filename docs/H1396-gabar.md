## Semantic Categories

### 1. Prevailing Waters / Overwhelming Natural Force
The four Genesis flood uses are the densest clustering — the verb conveys waters that physically
overwhelm and surpass all resistance.

- **Gen 7:18** — "the waters prevailed, and were increased greatly upon the earth"
- **Gen 7:19** — "the waters prevailed exceedingly upon the earth; and all the high hills...were covered"
- **Gen 7:20** — "Fifteen cubits upward did the waters prevail; and the mountains were covered"
- **Gen 7:24** — "the waters prevailed upon the earth an hundred and fifty days"

### 2. Military / Battlefield Superiority
In battle contexts the verb marks which side gains the upper hand — often shifting with changing
conditions (Exo 17:11's alternation is the clearest example).

- **Exo 17:11** — "when Moses held up his hand, that Israel prevailed: and when he let down his hand, Amalek prevailed"
- **2 Sam 11:23** — "Surely the men prevailed against us, and came out unto us into the field"
- **Lam 1:16** — "the comforter that should relieve my soul is far from me...because the enemy prevailed"
- **1 Sam 2:9** — "for by strength shall no man prevail" (negative — warns against self-reliance)

### 3. Comparative Greatness / Surpassing Measure
Qal uses expressing that one thing exceeds another — applied to blessings, rank, and even divine
mercy.

- **Gen 49:26** — "The blessings of thy father have prevailed above the blessings of my progenitors"
- **1 Chr 5:2** — "Judah prevailed above his brethren, and of him came the chief ruler"
- **2 Sam 1:23** — "they were swifter than eagles, they were stronger than lions"
- **Ps 103:11** — "as the heaven is high above the earth, so great is his mercy toward them that fear him"
- **Ps 117:2** — "his merciful kindness is great toward us"

### 4. Arrogant Self-Strengthening / Insolent Prevailing
Hithpael and negative Qal uses mark human pride that asserts itself against God or suppresses truth.

- **Job 15:25** — "he stretcheth out his hand against God, and strengtheneth himself against the Almighty"
- **Job 36:9** — "he sheweth them their work, and their transgressions that they have exceeded"
- **Ps 12:4** — "Who have said, With our tongue will we prevail; our lips are our own"
- **Ps 65:3** — "Iniquities prevail against me: as for our transgressions, thou shalt purge them away"
- **Jer 9:3** — "they are not valiant for the truth upon the earth; for they proceed from evil to evil"

### 5. Divine Strengthening / Covenant Confirmation
Piel and Hiphil uses where God is the subject — making strong, saving, or confirming a covenant.

- **Zech 10:6** — "I will strengthen the house of Judah, and I will save the house of Joseph"
- **Zech 10:12** — "I will strengthen them in the LORD; and they shall walk up and down in his name"
- **Isa 42:13** — "The LORD shall go forth as a mighty man...he shall prevail against his enemies"
- **Dan 9:27** — "he shall confirm the covenant with many for one week" (Hiphil: make strong/firm)
