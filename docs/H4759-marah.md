## Lexicon Definition

Feminine of מַרְאֶה (H4758); a vision; also (causatively) a mirror. KJV renderings: *looking glass, vision*.

A concrete-to-abstract noun: the "thing seen" — either literally (a reflective surface) or experientially (a revelatory scene perceived by a prophet). Unlike the more common abstract words for mental revelation (חָזוֹן chazon, H2377) it is tied to visual sight — what appears to the eye, whether physical or in prophetic perception.

## Semantic Categories

### 1. Night Vision / Dream-Revelation
Visions received during sleep or visionary sleep-like states.
- **Gen 46:2** — "God spake unto Israel in the visions (marʼâh) of the night"
- **Num 12:6** — "I the LORD will make myself known unto him in a vision (marʼâh), and will speak unto him in a dream" — paired with חֲלוֹם (chalom) as the normative mode of non-Mosaic prophecy

### 2. Auditory-Visual Prophetic Call
First-encounter prophetic commissioning experience.
- **1 Sam 3:15** — "Samuel feared to shew Eli the vision (marʼâh)" — the childhood call of Samuel

### 3. Transported / Panoramic Prophetic Experience
Extended, immersive visionary states where the prophet is "brought" somewhere by the Spirit. Consistently paired with אֱלֹהִים (elohim) — "visions of God."
- **Ezek 1:1** — "the heavens were opened, and I saw visions (marʼôth) of God"
- **Ezek 8:3** — "the spirit lifted me up... and brought me in the visions (marʼôth) of God to Jerusalem"
- **Ezek 40:2** — "In the visions (marʼôth) of God brought he me into the land of Israel"

### 4. Climactic Apocalyptic Vision (Daniel 10)
A singular great vision that produces physical weakness and terror in the seer. All three Daniel uses cluster in one pericope.
- **Dan 10:7** — "I Daniel alone saw the vision (marʼâh)... but a great quaking fell upon them"
- **Dan 10:8** — "I... saw this great vision (marʼâh), and there remained no strength in me"
- **Dan 10:16** — "by the vision (marʼâh) my sorrows are turned upon me"

### 5. Mirror / Looking-Glass (concrete sense)
The single causative/concrete use — the "thing-that-reflects-a-sight."
- **Exod 38:8** — "he made the laver of brass... of the lookingglasses (marʼôth) of the women assembling"
