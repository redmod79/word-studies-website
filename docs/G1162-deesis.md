## Related Words

### Synonyms
- **G4335 προσευχή (proseuchē)** — prayer (worship), the broadest and most general prayer term; 37v. Denotes prayer as address to God, including worship-dimension. Co-occurs with deēsis 5x as the comprehensive pair "prayer and supplication."
- **G1783 ἔντευξις (enteuxis)** — intercession, petition; 2v (1Ti 2:1, 4:5). From entygchano "to fall in with, approach"; carries connotation of bold approach or audience with a superior.
- **G2171 εὐχή (euchē)** — wish, prayer, vow; 3v. Broader semantic range including vows (Acts 18:18, 21:23) and prayer (Jas 5:15).
- **G2428 ἱκετηρία (hiketēria)** — supplication, olive-branch of entreaty; 1v (Heb 5:7). The only other prayer term in Heb 5:7 alongside deēsis; denotes the posture of a suppliant.
- **G155 αἴτημα (aitēma)** — petition, request (the thing asked for); 3v. Focuses on the content of the request rather than the act of asking.
- **G1189 δέομαι (deomai)** — to beg, beseech; 8v. The root verb from which deēsis is derived; verbal counterpart.

### Antonyms
