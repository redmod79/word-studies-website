## Lexicon Definition

A primitive root; to desolate — translated: destroy, destruction, bring to nought, overthrow, perish, pluck down, utterly.

The verb carries the sense of total annihilation — not merely damage or ruin, but extermination to the point of no remnant. It is consistently paired with language of wiping out, cutting off from under heaven, or removing utterly from before one's face.

## Verb Stem Distribution

Tallied from 91 verbal occurrences (excluding one proper-noun form at 1 Chr 8:12 "Shemed"):

| Stem | Count | Typical Force |
|------|-------|---------------|
| Hiphil | 69 | causative active — "to destroy, exterminate" (subject actively wipes out an object) |
| Niphal | 21 | passive/ingressive — "to be destroyed, perish" (of the object being exterminated) |
| Haphel (Aramaic-style, Dan 7:26) | 1 | rare hybrid form; "to exterminate" (the dominion shall be destroyed) |

The verb is effectively unattested in Qal in Biblical Hebrew — it functions almost exclusively as a causative Hiphil (the actor annihilates) or as a Niphal reflexive/passive (the object is annihilated). This distribution makes the verb inherently transitive in meaning: destruction is something done to someone or something.

## Semantic Categories

### 1. YHWH Exterminating the Canaanite Nations (Conquest Framework)
The dominant usage pattern: God as subject, Canaanite peoples as object, in the context of Israel's entry into the land.
- **Deut 2:12** — "when [the children of Esau] had destroyed (shâmad) them [the Horims] from before them"
- **Deut 2:21** — "the LORD destroyed them before them [the Anakims]; and they succeeded them"
- **Deut 31:3** — "he will destroy these nations from before thee, and thou shalt possess them"
- **Josh 24:8** — "I destroyed them from before you, and ye possessed their land"

### 2. Covenant Curse Against Apostate Israel (Deuteronomic Threat)
The same verb, turned against Israel herself, as the ultimate covenant sanction.
- **Deut 4:26** — "ye shall soon utterly perish (shâmad)... ye shall not prolong your days upon it"
- **Deut 6:15** — "lest the anger of the LORD thy God be kindled against thee, and destroy thee from off the face of the earth"
- **Deut 28:20, 24, 45, 48, 51, 61, 63** — sevenfold curse refrain: "until thou be destroyed"
- **Deut 9:8** — "the LORD was angry with you to have destroyed you"

### 3. Destruction of Dynasties and Royal Houses
Used of the eradication of kingly lines and the cutting off of royal seed.
- **1 Kings 13:34** — "to cut [it] off, and to destroy [it] from off the face of the earth" (house of Jeroboam)
- **1 Kings 15:29** — "he left not to Jeroboam any that breathed, until he had destroyed him"
- **1 Kings 16:12** — "Thus did Zimri destroy all the house of Baasha"
- **2 Kings 10:17** — "[Jehu] slew all that remained unto Ahab... till he had destroyed him"

### 4. Destruction of Idolatry and False Worship
Used when the objects destroyed are cultic — idols, high places, groves, Baal-worship.
- **Lev 26:30** — "I will destroy your high places, and cut down your images"
- **2 Kings 10:28** — "Thus Jehu destroyed Baal out of Israel"
- **Mic 5:14** — "I will pluck up thy groves... so will I destroy thy cities"

### 5. Extermination Plots Against the People of God
Used of human enemies attempting to annihilate Israel or the Jewish people.
- **Esth 3:6** — "to destroy all the Jews that [were] throughout the whole kingdom of Ahasuerus"
- **Esth 3:13** — "the letters... to destroy, to kill, and to cause to perish, all Jews"
- **Esth 7:4** — "we are sold, I and my people, to be destroyed, to be slain, and to perish"
- **Esth 8:11** — "to stand for their life, to destroy... all the power of the people"

### 6. Eschatological / Prophetic Final Destruction
Used in prophetic visions of end-time or climactic judgment — nations, wicked, dominions.
- **Isa 13:9** — "the day of the LORD cometh... to lay the land desolate: and he shall destroy the sinners thereof"
- **Dan 11:44** — "he shall go forth with great fury to destroy, and utterly to make away many"
- **Hag 2:22** — "I will overthrow the throne of kingdoms, and I will destroy the strength of the kingdoms of the heathen"
- **Zech 12:9** — "I will seek to destroy all the nations that come against Jerusalem"
- **Psa 145:20** — "all the wicked will he destroy"

### 7. Divine Restraint / Non-Destruction (the Verb Negated)
A small but theologically weighty cluster where YHWH refrains from destroying, or is asked to.
- **Psa 106:23** — "he said that he would destroy them, had not Moses his chosen stood before him in the breach"
- **Psa 106:34** — "They did not destroy the nations, concerning whom the LORD commanded them" (Israel's failure)
- **1 Sam 24:21** — "thou wilt not cut off my seed after me, and that thou wilt not destroy my name"
