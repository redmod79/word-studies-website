## Lexicon Definition

Of uncertain affinity; a deity, especially (with the article) the supreme Deity; God. Used both for the God of the Bible and (rarely) for pagan deities.

## Semantic Categories

### 1. The Supreme Deity
God as the one absolute divine being, distinct from all other beings.
- **1 Cor 8:4** — "there is none other God but one"
- **1 Cor 8:5–6** — "though there be that are called gods... to us there is but one God, the Father"
- **John 1:1** — "In the beginning was the Word, and the Word was with God, and the Word was God"
- **Mat 1:23** — "Emmanuel, which being interpreted is, God with us"

### 2. God the Father
God identified as divine Father, source of grace, peace, and blessing — the dominant use in Pauline greeting formulas.
- **Rom 1:7** — "Grace to you and peace from God our Father, and the Lord Jesus Christ"
- **2 Cor 1:3** — "Blessed be God, even the Father of our Lord Jesus Christ, the Father of mercies"
- **Gal 1:1** — "by Jesus Christ, and God the Father, who raised him from the dead"
- **1 Pet 1:2** — "Elect according to the foreknowledge of God the Father"

### 3. Kingdom of God
God's sovereign rule, preached by Jesus as the central message of the gospel.
- **Mark 1:14–15** — "preaching the gospel of the kingdom of God... the kingdom of God is at hand"
- **Luke 1:16** — "shall he turn to the Lord their God"
- **Acts 1:3** — "speaking of the things pertaining to the kingdom of God"
- **Rom 14:17** — "the kingdom of God is not meat and drink"

### 4. Son of God / Christ as God
Christological uses — Christ identified as God's Son or directly as God.
- **Mark 1:1** — "the beginning of the gospel of Jesus Christ, the Son of God"
- **Rom 1:4** — "declared to be the Son of God with power... by the resurrection from the dead"
- **John 20:28** — "Thomas... said unto him, My Lord and my God"
- **Heb 1:8** — "But unto the Son he saith, Thy throne, O God, is for ever and ever"

### 5. Spirit of God
The Spirit as God's active presence and agent.
- **Acts 2:17** — "saith God, I will pour out of my Spirit upon all flesh"
- **1 Cor 2:11** — "the things of God knoweth no man, but the Spirit of God"
- **1 Pet 1:2** — "Elect... through sanctification of the Spirit, unto obedience" (God's Spirit at work)
- **Mat 3:16** — "the Spirit of God descending like a dove"

### 6. God's Character and Nature
God defined by specific attributes across the epistles and gospels.
- **1 John 1:5** — "God is light, and in him is no darkness at all"
- **James 1:13** — "God cannot be tempted with evil, neither tempteth he any man"
- **Titus 1:2** — "God, that cannot lie, promised before the world began"
- **Heb 1:1** — "God, who at sundry times and in divers manners spake in time past unto the fathers"

### 7. False Gods / Pagan Deities
Applied to non-divine beings worshipped by Gentiles; the plural "gods" or in 2 Cor 4:4 "god of this world."
- **1 Cor 8:5** — "there be gods many, and lords many"
- **Acts 14:11** — "The gods are come down to us in the likeness of men"
- **Gal 4:8** — "ye did service unto them which by nature are no gods"
- **2 Cor 4:4** — "the god of this world hath blinded the minds of them which believe not"
