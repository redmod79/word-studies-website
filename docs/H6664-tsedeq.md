## Semantic Categories

### 1. Equitable Legal Standard (Weights, Measures, Judicial Process)
tsedeq as the objective norm of right conduct in commercial and judicial contexts.

- **Lev 19:15** — "Ye shall do no unrighteousness in judgment: thou shalt not respect the person of the poor, nor honour the person of the mighty: but in righteousness shalt thou judge thy neighbour."
- **Lev 19:36** — "Just balances, just weights, a just ephah, and a just hin, shall ye have."
- **Deut 16:20** — "That which is altogether just shalt thou follow, that thou mayest live, and inherit the land."
- **Deut 25:15** — "Thou shalt have a perfect and just weight, a perfect and just measure."

### 2. God's Righteous Character and Throne
tsedeq as the fundamental attribute of God's nature and the foundation of his rule.

- **Ps 89:14** — "Justice and judgment are the habitation of thy throne: mercy and truth shall go before thy face."
- **Ps 97:2** — "Clouds and darkness are round about him: righteousness and judgment are the habitation of his throne."
- **Ps 97:6** — "The heavens declare his righteousness, and all the people see his glory."
- **Ps 50:6** — "And the heavens shall declare his righteousness: for God is judge himself."

### 3. Personal Moral Uprightness
tsedeq as an individual's righteous conduct or standing before God and community.

- **Ps 15:2** — "He that walketh uprightly, and worketh righteousness, and speaketh the truth in his heart."
- **Ps 18:20** — "The LORD rewarded me according to my righteousness; according to the cleanness of my hands hath he recompensed me."
- **Job 29:14** — "I put on righteousness, and it clothed me: my judgment was as a robe and a diadem."
- **Isa 58:2** — "they seek me daily, and delight to know my ways, as a nation that did righteousness."

### 4. God's Commandments/Torah as Righteousness
tsedeq applied to the divine law itself — God's statutes are tsedeq in substance.

- **Ps 119:138** — "Thy testimonies that thou hast commanded are righteous and very faithful."
- **Ps 119:142** — "Thy righteousness is an everlasting righteousness, and thy law is the truth."
- **Ps 119:172** — "My tongue shall speak of thy word: for all thy commandments are righteousness."
- **Ps 23:3** — "He restoreth my soul: he leadeth me in the paths of righteousness for his name's sake."

### 5. The tsedeq–Mishpat Couplet (Righteousness and Justice Paired)
tsedeq almost always appears alongside mishpat (H4941, "justice/judgment"). Together they form the standard ethical pair in the prophets and Psalms.

- **Ps 85:10** — "Mercy and truth are met together; righteousness and peace have kissed each other."
- **Prov 1:3** — "To receive the instruction of wisdom, justice, and judgment, and equity."
- **Hos 2:19** — "I will betroth thee unto me in righteousness, and in judgment, and in lovingkindness."
- **Ps 37:6** — "And he shall bring forth thy righteousness as the light, and thy judgment as the noonday."

### 6. Messianic and Eschatological Righteousness
tsedeq used prophetically for the coming righteous king and the restoration of righteousness to the earth.

- **Jer 23:6** — "And this is his name whereby he shall be called, THE LORD OUR RIGHTEOUSNESS."
- **Isa 32:1** — "Behold, a king shall reign in righteousness, and princes shall rule in judgment."
- **Isa 45:8** — "Drop down, ye heavens, from above, and let the skies pour down righteousness: let the earth open, and let them bring forth salvation, and let righteousness spring up together."
- **Dan 9:24** — "Seventy weeks are determined… to bring in everlasting righteousness."
