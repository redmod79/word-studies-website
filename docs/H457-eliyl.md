## Lexicon Definition

A term meaning idol, thing of nought, worthless thing, or vanity. Unlike words that emphasize the shape or manufacture of an idol, H457 stresses its emptiness and uselessness.

## Semantic Categories

### 1. Prohibited idols in covenant law
- **Lev 19:4** — Israel must not turn to idols.
- **Lev 26:1** — idols are banned alongside graven and standing images.

### 2. Worthless false gods contrasted with YHWH
- **1Ch 16:26** — all the gods of the peoples are idols, but YHWH made the heavens.
- **Psa 96:5** — the same creator/false-god contrast.
- **Isa 2:8, 20** — idol-filled land and discarded idols of silver and gold.

### 3. Prophetic anti-idol polemic
- **Isa 10:10-11** — idols are linked to Samaria and Jerusalem's rival worship systems.
- **Isa 19:1, 3** — Egypt's idols shake at YHWH's coming.
- **Isa 31:7** — every man casts away his idols.

### 4. Worthlessness extended beyond cult statues
- **Job 13:4** — "physicians of no value."
- **Zec 11:17** — the "idol shepherd" is a worthless shepherd.
