## Overview
The Greek perissos- word family forms a tight cognate cluster expressing the concept of surplus, superabundance, and excess. ### Semantic Distinctions Within the Cognate Cluster
- **perisseuma** (G4051) — the concrete thing left over; what overflows from a container
- **perisseia** (G4050) — the abstract state or quality of having surplus
- **perisseuō** (G4052) — the dynamic action/process of abounding or exceeding
- **perissos** (G4053) — adjectival: describing something as beyond what is expected or necessary
