## Lexicon Definition

Properly, to distinguish, i.e. decide (mentally or judicially); by implication to try, condemn, punish. KJV renderings: judge, determine, condemn, go to law, call in question, esteem, decree, sentence, damn, avenge, conclude, ordain, think.
