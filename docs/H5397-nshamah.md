## Lexicon Definition

From H5395; a puff, i.e. wind, angry or vital breath, divine inspiration,
intellect. or (concretely) an animal:—blast, (that) breath(-eth),
inspiration, soul, spirit.

## Semantic Categories

### 1. Breath of Life — Divine Gift at Creation
The neshamah as the animating force God breathes directly into living beings.

- **Gen 2:7** — "And the LORD God...breathed into his nostrils the breath (neshamah) of life; and man became a living soul"
- **Gen 7:22** — "All in whose nostrils [was] the breath (neshamah) of life, of all that [was] in the dry [land], died"
- **Job 33:4** — "The Spirit of God hath made me, and the breath (neshamah) of the Almighty hath given me life"
- **Isa 42:5** — "[God] giveth breath (neshamah) unto the people upon it, and spirit to them that walk therein"

### 2. Divine Blast — God's Wrathful Breath
The same divine breath becomes a destructive force when God acts in judgment.

- **Job 4:9** — "By the blast (neshamah) of God they perish, and by the breath of his nostrils are they consumed"
- **Ps 18:15** — "Then the channels of waters were seen...at the blast of the breath (neshamah) of thy nostrils"
- **Isa 30:33** — "The breath (neshamah) of the LORD, like a stream of brimstone, doth kindle it"
- **Job 37:10** — "By the breath (neshamah) of God frost is given: and the breadth of the waters is straitened"

### 3. Total Annihilation — "No Neshamah Left"
A formula for complete slaughter where no living thing remains.

- **Deut 20:16** — "thou shalt save alive nothing that breatheth (neshamah)"
- **Josh 10:40** — "destroyed all that breathed (neshamah), as the LORD God of Israel commanded"
- **Josh 11:11** — "they smote all the souls...there was not any left to breathe (neshamah)"
- **1 Ki 17:17** — "his sickness was so sore, that there was no breath (neshamah) left in him"

### 4. Human Inner Life — The Spirit of Illumination
Neshamah as the divine spark within a person that enables wisdom, consciousness, and moral awareness.

- **Prov 20:27** — "The spirit (neshamah) of man [is] the candle of the LORD, searching all the inward parts of the belly"
- **Job 32:8** — "there is a spirit in man: and the inspiration (neshamah) of the Almighty giveth them understanding"
- **Job 27:3** — "All the while my breath (neshamah) [is] in me, and the spirit of God [is] in my nostrils"
- **Isa 57:16** — "I will not contend for ever...for the spirit should fail before me, and the souls (neshamah) which I have made"

### 5. Every Living Being
Neshamah as the shared quality that defines all living creatures; universal breath.

- **Ps 150:6** — "Let every thing that hath breath (neshamah) praise the LORD. Praise ye the LORD"
- **Isa 2:22** — "Cease ye from man, whose breath (neshamah) [is] in his nostrils: for wherein is he to be accounted of?"
- **Dan 10:17** — "straightway there remained no strength in me, neither is there breath (neshamah) left in me"
