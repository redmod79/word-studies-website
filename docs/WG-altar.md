## Roles in the Concept

- **Place:** θυσιαστήριον (G2379) — where sacrifice happens
- **Offering:** θυσία (G2378) — the sacrifice itself (act or victim)
- **Action:** θύω (G2380) — the act of sacrificing/slaying
- **Gift:** δῶρον (G1435) — what is brought to the altar
- **Blood:** αἷμα (G129) — the life-substance shed in sacrifice
- **Contrast:** βωμός (G1041) — pagan altar (only Acts 17:23)

## Key Passages Where Multiple Group Words Appear Together
- **Matt 5:23-24** — θυσιαστήριον + δῶρον: "bring thy gift to the altar"
- **Matt 23:18-19** — θυσιαστήριον + δῶρον: "the altar that sanctifieth the gift"

- **Matt 23:35** — θυσιαστήριον + αἷμα: "righteous blood... between the temple and the altar"
