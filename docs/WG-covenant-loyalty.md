# WG: Covenant Loyalty

## Overview
The Hebrew covenant-loyalty concept centers on checed (H2617) and its network of closely paired words. These terms describe the faithful, compassionate, and truthful commitment that characterizes God's relationship with his covenant people and that he requires of them toward one another.
