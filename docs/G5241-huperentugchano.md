## Semantic Categories

### 1. The Spirit's Intercession on Behalf of Believers
- **Rom 8:26** — "the Spirit itself maketh intercession for us with groanings which cannot be uttered"

This is the only category available for a hapax legomenon. The prefix ὑπέρ intensifies the base verb ἐντυγχάνω, indicating an intercession that goes above and beyond — the Spirit intercedes *on behalf of* believers when they are unable to articulate their own prayers. The intercession is further characterized as involving "groanings which cannot be uttered" (στεναγμοῖς ἀλαλήτοις).

## Contextual Usage (Romans 8:26-27)

The full context places G5241 alongside its cognate base verb G1793:

- **Rom 8:26** — "the Spirit itself **maketh intercession** [G5241 ὑπερεντυγχάνει] for us with groanings which cannot be uttered"
- **Rom 8:27** — "he that searcheth the hearts knoweth what is the mind of the Spirit, because **he maketh intercession** [G1793 ἐντυγχάνει] for the saints according to the will of God"

Paul uses the intensified compound (G5241) when describing the Spirit's action, then shifts to the simpler form (G1793) in the explanatory v.27. The ὑπέρ prefix distinguishes this intercession as transcending normal petition — it is beyond what the supplicant can express.
