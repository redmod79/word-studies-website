## Roles in the Concept

- **State/Object:** elpis (G1680) — the hope itself, whether abstract confidence or the concrete thing hoped for
- **Action:** elpizō (G1679) — the act of hoping, trusting, expecting
- **Prior Hope:** proelpizō (G4276) — having hoped in advance (Eph 1:12, Jewish believers who hoped before Gentiles believed)
- **Companion Virtue (present):** pistis (G4102) — faith directed at present reality; the triad partner
- **Companion Virtue (sustaining):** hupomone (G5281) — patient endurance that sustains hope through tribulation (Rom 5:3-4 chain)
- **Mode of Waiting:** apekdechomai (G553) — eager, full expectation (Rom 8:19, 23, 25; Gal 5:5)
- **Intensity of Expectation:** apokaradokia (G603) — literally "stretching the head forward" in anticipation (Rom 8:19, Phil 1:20)
