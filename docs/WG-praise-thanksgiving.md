## Roles in the Concept

- **Acknowledgment:** yadah (יָדָה) — extending the hand in recognition; both thanksgiving and confession
- **Celebration:** halal (הָלַל) — exuberant, often loud and public praise; source of "hallelujah"
- **Music:** zamar (זָמַר) — praise specifically through singing and instruments
- **Commendation:** shabach (שָׁבַח) — addressing God in a loud tone, lauding
- **Offering:** towdah (תּוֹדָה) — the concrete expression: thanksgiving sacrifice or verbal confession
- **Ground:** chesed (חֶסֶד) — God's covenant faithfulness that prompts the praise
- **Duration:** owlam (עוֹלָם) — "for ever" — the endless quality of God's chesed that warrants endless praise
