## Lexicon Definition

A primitive root; to fill or (intransitively) be full of, in a wide application (literally and figuratively): accomplish, confirm, consecrate, be at an end, be expired, be fenced, fill, fulfil, (be, become, draw, give in, go) full(-ly, -ly set, tale), (over-)flow, fulness, furnish, gather (selves, together), presume, replenish, satisfy, set, space, take a (hand-)full, have wholly.
