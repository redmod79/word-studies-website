## Lexicon Definition

Of uncertain (perhaps foreign) derivation; a royal edict or statute:—commandment, commission, decree, law, manner.

Esther dominates overwhelmingly (90% of all occurrences). Deuteronomy 33:2 is the sole pre-exilic occurrence and uses dâth uniquely for divine law.

## Semantic Categories

### 1. Royal Edict / Specific Decree
The most common usage: a particular order issued by the king, often paired with "commandment."
- **Est 3:15** — "The posts went out, being hastened by the king's commandment, and the decree (dâth) was given in Shushan the palace"
- **Est 4:3** — "in every province, whithersoever the king's commandment and his decree (dâth) came, there was great mourning"
- **Est 9:14** — "the king commanded it so to be done: and the decree (dâth) was given at Shushan"
- **Est 4:8** — "the copy of the writing of the decree (dâth) that was given at Shushan to destroy them"

### 2. Established Law / Legal Code
Standing laws of a kingdom or people, not a single edict but an ongoing legal norm.
- **Est 1:19** — "let it be written among the laws (dâth) of the Persians and the Medes, that it be not altered"
- **Est 3:8** — "their laws (dâth) are diverse from all people; neither keep they the king's laws (dâth)"
- **Est 4:11** — "there is one law (dâth) of his to put him to death, except such to whom the king shall hold out the golden sceptre"
- **Est 4:16** — "I also and my maidens will fast... and so will I go in unto the king, which is not according to the law (dâth)"

### 3. Custom / Established Protocol
Prescribed manner or procedure, closer to "regulation" than formal legislation.
- **Est 1:8** — "the drinking was according to the law (dâth); none did compel"
- **Est 1:13** — "for so was the king's manner (dâth) toward all that knew law and judgment"
- **Est 2:12** — "after that she had been twelve months, according to the manner (dâth) of the women"

### 4. Divine Law (Unique Usage)
A single poetic application to God's law given at Sinai — the only pre-exilic occurrence.
- **Deu 33:2** — "he came with ten thousands of saints: from his right hand went a fiery law (dâth) for them"

### 5. Royal Commission / Authorization
Official documents conveying royal authority.
- **Ezr 8:36** — "they delivered the king's commissions (dâth) unto the king's lieutenants"
