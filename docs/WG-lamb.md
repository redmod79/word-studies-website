## Roles in the Concept

- **Title (Revelation):** arníon (G721) — the dominant christological lamb-title; 27 of 28 uses in Revelation
- **Title (Gospels/Epistles):** amnós (G286) — "Lamb of God" (Jhn 1:29, 36); "a lamb without blemish" (1 Pet 1:19)
- **Generic lamb:** arēn (G704) — "lambs among wolves" (Lk 10:3); non-christological
- **The flock:** próbaton (G4263) — the sheep who follow the Lamb (Jhn 10; Jhn 21:16-17)
- **The sacrifice:** sphazō (G4969) — the act that qualifies the Lamb for enthronement
- **The destination:** thrónos (G2362) — the Lamb shares God's throne
