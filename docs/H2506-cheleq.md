## Lexicon Definition

Properly, smoothness (of the tongue); also an allotment: flattery, inheritance, part, partake, portion.

The dual semantic range reflects the root H2505 chalaq, which means both "to divide/apportion" (yielding the dominant "portion" sense) and "to be smooth" (yielding the rare "flattery" sense in Job 17:5 and Prov 7:21).
