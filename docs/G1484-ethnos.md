## Lexicon Definition

A race (as sharing the same habit/custom), i.e., a tribe; especially a foreign (non-Jewish) nation; plural = the nations, the Gentiles (all peoples outside Israel). Used both as a neutral ethno-political term for any national group and as a technical term for non-Jews.

## Semantic Categories

### 1. Nations as Ethnic/Political Groups (neutral)
Any group of people sharing a common ethnic, cultural, or political identity; includes Israel itself in some contexts.

- **Acts 2:5** — "devout men, out of every nation under heaven"
- **Acts 17:26** — "hath made of one blood all nations of men for to dwell on all the face of the earth"
- **Matt 24:7** — "For nation shall rise against nation, and kingdom against kingdom"
- **Rev 7:9** — "a great multitude, which no man could number, of all nations, and kindreds, and people, and tongues"

### 2. Gentiles Contrasted with Jews
Non-Jewish peoples, specifically defined by contrast with Israel, circumcision, and the covenant.

- **Gal 2:15** — "We who are Jews by nature, and not sinners of the Gentiles"
- **Eph 2:11** — "that ye being in time past Gentiles in the flesh, who are called Uncircumcision"
- **Rom 3:29** — "Is he the God of the Jews only? is he not also of the Gentiles? Yes, of the Gentiles also"
- **Acts 10:45** — "they of the circumcision which believed were astonished…because that on the Gentiles also was poured out the gift of the Holy Ghost"

### 3. Gentiles as Recipients of the Gospel
The mission field; nations destined to hear and receive the gospel.

- **Matt 28:19** — "Go ye therefore, and teach all nations, baptizing them"
- **Luke 2:32** — "A light to lighten the Gentiles, and the glory of thy people Israel"
- **Acts 13:47** — "I have set thee to be a light of the Gentiles, that thou shouldest be for salvation unto the ends of the earth"
- **Rom 15:16** — "That I should be the minister of Jesus Christ to the Gentiles, ministering the gospel of God"

### 4. Gentile Political Rulers and Dominion
Worldly power exercised by nations and their kings, often contrasted with servant-leadership.

- **Matt 20:25** — "Ye know that the princes of the Gentiles exercise dominion over them"
- **Luke 22:25** — "The kings of the Gentiles exercise lordship over them"
- **Mark 10:33** — "the Son of man shall be delivered unto the chief priests, and unto the scribes; and they shall condemn him…and shall deliver him to the Gentiles"

### 5. Gentile Conduct as a Moral Contrast
Idolatry, ignorance of God, and worldly striving as characteristic of the nations.

- **Matt 6:32** — "after all these things do the Gentiles seek"
- **1 Cor 12:2** — "Ye know that ye were Gentiles, carried away unto these dumb idols"
- **1 Thess 4:5** — "Not in the lust of concupiscence, even as the Gentiles which know not God"
- **Eph 4:17** — "that ye henceforth walk not as other Gentiles walk, in the vanity of their mind"

### 6. Nations in Eschatology
Nations as objects of final judgment, divine anger, and ultimate redemption.

- **Rev 11:18** — "And the nations were angry, and thy wrath is come"
- **Rev 20:3** — "that he should deceive the nations no more"
- **Rev 21:24** — "the nations of them which are saved shall walk in the light of it"
- **Rev 22:2** — "the tree of life…for the healing of the nations"
