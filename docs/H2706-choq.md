## Semantic Categories

### 1. Statutory Commandments of God (Mosaic Law context)
The dominant use: chôq in paired lists with mishpâṭ (judgments) and mitsvâh (commandments), denoting formally enacted divine laws.

- **Deut 4:1** — "hearken, O Israel, unto the statutes and unto the judgments, which I teach you"
- **Deut 4:5** — "I have taught you statutes and judgments, even as the LORD my God commanded me"
- **1 Kgs 3:14** — "if thou wilt walk in my ways, to keep my statutes and my commandments, as thy father David did walk"
- **Psa 119:5** — "O that my ways were directed to keep thy statutes!"
- **Psa 119:33** — "Teach me, O LORD, the way of thy statutes; and I shall keep it unto the end"

### 2. Cultic Perpetual Ordinances ("for ever")
Specific ritual regulations established as a permanent observance, often for priestly duties or feast observances.

- **Exo 12:24** — "ye shall observe this thing for an ordinance to thee and to thy sons for ever"
- **Lev 6:18** — "It shall be a statute for ever in your generations"
- **Num 18:11** — "the wave offerings of the children of Israel... have I given thee and thy sons and thy daughters with thee, by a statute for ever"
- **Psa 81:4** — "For this was a statute for Israel, and a law of the God of Jacob"

### 3. Cosmic Decree / Fixed Natural Boundaries
God's enacted limits upon natural phenomena — the boundaries of sea, the measure of days, the span of a life.

- **Job 14:5** — "his days are determined, the number of his months are with thee, thou hast appointed his bounds (chôq)"
- **Psa 148:6** — "He hath also stablished them for ever and ever: he hath made a decree (chôq) which shall not pass"
- **Prov 8:29** — "When he gave to the sea his decree (chôq), that the waters should not pass his commandment"
- **Jer 5:22** — "which have placed the sand for the bound (chôq) of the sea... though the waves toss themselves, yet can they not prevail"

### 4. Civil or Royal Decree / Established Custom
A fixed rule or practice established by human authority — a king's edict, a commander's standing order, a social custom.

- **Gen 47:26** — "Joseph made it a law (chôq) over the land of Egypt unto this day, that Pharaoh should have the fifth part"
- **1 Sam 30:25** — "he made it a statute (chôq) and an ordinance for Israel unto this day"
- **Psa 2:7** — "I will declare the decree (chôq): the LORD hath said unto me, Thou art my Son"
- **Jer 32:11** — "the evidence of the purchase, both that which was sealed according to the law and custom (chôq)"

### 5. Prescribed Allotment / Regular Portion
A fixed share of food, income, or provisions — the "appointed measure" sense from the root (to cut/measure out).

- **Gen 47:22** — "the priests had a portion (chôq) assigned them of Pharaoh"
- **Prov 30:8** — "feed me with food convenient (chôq) for me" — the prescribed portion, neither too much nor too little
- **Prov 31:15** — "she giveth meat to her household, and a portion (chôq) to her maidens"
- **Ezek 16:27** — "I have stretched out my hand over thee, and have diminished thine ordinary food (chôq)"
