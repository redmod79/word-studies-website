## Semantic Categories

### 1. Temporal Regnal Marker
Used in date formulas — "in the Nth year of the reign of [king]" — anchoring events to a king's tenure.
- **2 Chr 3:2** — "in the fourth year of his reign" (Solomon begins the temple)
- **Dan 1:1** — "In the third year of the reign of Jehoiakim... came Nebuchadnezzar"
- **Dan 2:1** — "in the second year of the reign of Nebuchadnezzar... he dreamed dreams"
- **Jer 49:34** — "in the beginning of the reign of Zedekiah"

### 2. Established / Confirmed Kingdom
God "establishes," "confirms," or "settles" a human kingdom; the dynasty is divinely secured.
- **1 Chr 14:2** — "the LORD had confirmed him king over Israel, for his kingdom was lifted up on high"
- **1 Chr 17:14** — "I will settle him in mine house and in my kingdom for ever"
- **1 Chr 28:7** — "I will establish his kingdom for ever, if he be constant to do my commandments"
- **2 Chr 1:1** — "Solomon the son of David was strengthened in his kingdom"

### 3. God's Eternal Kingdom
Divine sovereignty over all creation — universal, everlasting, beyond human succession.
- **Psa 45:6** — "the sceptre of thy kingdom [is] a right sceptre"
- **Psa 103:19** — "The LORD hath prepared his throne in the heavens; and his kingdom ruleth over all"
- **Psa 145:11** — "They shall speak of the glory of thy kingdom, and talk of thy power"
- **Psa 145:13** — "Thy kingdom [is] an everlasting kingdom, and thy dominion endureth throughout all generations"

### 4. Royal Regalia / Adjectival Use
In Esther, malkuwth modifies nouns to convey "belonging to the king" — royal apparel, royal house, crown royal, royal commandment.
- **Est 1:11** — "the crown royal, to shew the people and the princes her beauty"
- **Est 1:19** — "let there go a royal commandment from him"
- **Est 5:1** — "Esther put on [her] royal [apparel]"
- **Est 6:8** — "Let the royal apparel be brought which the king [useth] to wear"

### 5. Geopolitical Empire / Realm
The territorial extent of a king's dominion — the full scope of imperial control.
- **Est 1:2** — "king Ahasuerus sat on the throne of his kingdom, which [was] in Shushan the palace"
- **Est 1:20** — "throughout all his empire, (for it is great,)"
- **Dan 9:1** — "Darius... which was made king over the realm of the Chaldeans"
- **Dan 10:13** — "the prince of the kingdom of Persia withstood me one and twenty days"

### 6. Contested or Fractured Kingdoms

- **1 Sam 20:31** — "thou shalt not be established, nor thy kingdom"
- **Dan 8:22** — "four kingdoms shall stand up out of the nation, but not in his power"
- **Dan 11:4** — "his kingdom shall be broken, and shall be divided toward the four winds"
