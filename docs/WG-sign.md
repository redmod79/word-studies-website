## Roles in the Concept

- **Communicative signal:** ʼowth (אוֹת) — the broadest term; any visible sign that points beyond itself (covenant marks, miracles, prophecy confirmations, celestial markers)
- **Spectacular prodigy:** mowpheth (מוֹפֵת) — emphasizes the awe-inspiring, conspicuous nature; frequently paired with ʼowth as "signs and wonders"
- **Physical banner/standard:** nes (נֵס) — a raised signal visible from a distance; military and rallying contexts
- **Aramaic portent:** ʼath (אָת) — Aramaic equivalent of ʼowth, appearing only in Daniel
- **Waymark/monument:** tsiyuwn (צִיּוּן) — a heap or pillar marking a route or grave; the most concrete/physical member
