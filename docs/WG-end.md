## Roles in the Concept

- **Temporal terminus:** qets (קֵץ) — the fixed endpoint, the deadline God has set
- **Spatial boundary:** qatseh (קָצֶה) — the edge, the physical limit
- **Process of ending:** kalah (כָּלָה) — the act of finishing, consuming, completing
- **Aftermath:** acharith (אַחֲרִית) — what comes after the end, the latter part
- **Temporal context:** eth (עֵת) — the season or time in which the end occurs
- **Appointed setting:** moed (מוֹעֵד) — the divinely scheduled occasion tied to the end
