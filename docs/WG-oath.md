## Roles in the Concept

- **Act:** ὀμνύω (omnyo) — performing the oath
- **Content:** ὅρκος (horkos) — the oath as a thing, the words spoken
- **Institution:** ὁρκωμοσία (horkomosia) — oath-taking as a formal ceremony or institution
- **Compulsion:** ὁρκίζω (horkizo) — placing another under oath, adjuring
- **Violation:** ἐπιορκέω (epiorkeo) — breaking an oath, perjury
- **Voluntary parallel:** εὐχή (euche) — a vow made freely to God (prayer-wish)
