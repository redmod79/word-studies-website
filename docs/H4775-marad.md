### 3. National or Corporate Rebellion Named in Prophetic Indictment and Confession

- **Ezek 2:3** — Israel is "a rebellious nation that hath rebelled against me."
- **Dan 9:5** — Daniel's confession includes "we have rebelled."

### 4. Rebels as a Class, Reputation, or Standing Description
