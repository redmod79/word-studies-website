## Lexicon Definition

A primitive root; to depress, i.e. prostrate (especially reflexive, in homage to royalty or God): — bow (self) down, crouch, fall down (flat), humbly beseech, do (make) obeisance, do reverence, make to stoop, worship.

The Hishtaphel is the overwhelmingly dominant stem (99% of verbal occurrences). This is a reflexive/iterative form unique to this verb — the subject prostrates himself/herself. The single Hiphil (Prov 12:25) is causative: anxiety causes the heart to "stoop." The Qal imperative appears in Isa 51:23 as oppressors commanding "Bow down, that we may go over."
