## Lexicon Definition

Feminine of H5707; testimony; specifically the covenant document deposited before God — testimony, witness.

*(Full form: from root H5749 עוּד — to repeat, protest, bear witness; H5715 is the abstract noun form, referring to the formal testimony itself rather than the person who testifies.)*

## Semantic Categories

### 1. Tables of Testimony (Stone Tablets as Covenant Document)
The stone tablets Moses received on Sinai are called "tables of testimony" — God's written witness to Israel.
- **Exo 31:18** — "two tables of testimony in Moses' hand, tables of stone, written with the finger of God"
- **Exo 32:15** — "the two tables of the testimony [were] in his hand: the tables [were] written on both their sides"
- **Exo 34:29** — "Moses came down from mount Sinai with the two tables of testimony in Moses' hand"

### 2. Ark of the Testimony (Chest Housing the Tablets)
The ark is named for its contents — the testimony documents placed inside it.
- **Exo 25:16** — "thou shalt put into the ark the testimony which I shall give thee"
- **Exo 25:22** — "I will commune with thee from above the mercy seat... upon the ark of the testimony"
- **Josh 4:16** — "Command the priests that bear the ark of the testimony, that they come up out of Jordan"

### 3. Tabernacle/Tent of Testimony (Sanctuary Named for Housed Testimony)
The entire dwelling is designated "tabernacle of testimony" — identified by the document it shelters.
- **Num 1:50** — "thou shalt appoint the Levites over the tabernacle of testimony, and over all the vessels thereof"
- **Num 9:15** — "the cloud covered the tabernacle, [namely], the tent of the testimony"
- **Num 17:7** — "Moses laid up the rods before the LORD in the tabernacle of witness"

### 4. Testimony as Royal Covenant Document (Given at Coronation)
When a king is crowned, the testimony (the written covenant) is placed in his hand as his governing charter.
- **2Ki 11:12** — "he brought forth the king's son, and put the crown upon him, and [gave him] the testimony; and they made him king"
- **2Chr 23:11** — "they brought out the king's son, and put upon him the crown, and [gave him] the testimony"
- **Ps 78:5** — "he established a testimony in Jacob, and appointed a law in Israel, which he commanded our fathers"

### 5. God's Testimonies as Precepts (Devotional / Torah Usage)
In Psalms the plural "thy testimonies" functions as a synonym for God's commandments and statutes — the full body of revealed instruction.
- **Ps 19:7** — "the testimony of the LORD [is] sure, making wise the simple"
- **Ps 119:14** — "I have rejoiced in the way of thy testimonies, as [much as] in all riches"
- **Ps 119:111** — "Thy testimonies have I taken as an heritage for ever: for they [are] the rejoicing of my heart"
- **Ps 122:4** — "the tribes go up... unto the testimony of Israel, to give thanks unto the name of the LORD"
