## Lexicon Definition

A sacred place or thing; rarely abstract, sanctity. From qadash (H6942). Translated as: consecrated (thing), dedicated (thing), hallowed (thing), holiness, (most) holy (day, portion, thing), saint, sanctuary.

## Semantic Categories

### 1. Sacred Quality — "Holy" as Modifier
The most frequent use: qodesh modifies a noun in construct, marking it as set apart.
- **Exo 3:5** — "the place whereon thou standest is holy (qodesh) ground"
- **Exo 16:23** — "To morrow is the rest of the holy (qodesh) sabbath unto the LORD"
- **Isa 52:1** — "put on thy beautiful garments, O Jerusalem, the holy (qodesh) city"
- **Isa 58:13** — "call the sabbath a delight, the holy (qodesh) of the LORD"

### 2. Sacred Space — Sanctuary
Qodesh frequently denotes the sanctuary itself, especially the tabernacle/temple holy place.
- **Exo 26:33** — "the vail shall divide unto you between the holy (qodesh) and the most holy (qodesh)"
- **Exo 30:13** — "half a shekel after the shekel of the sanctuary (qodesh)"
- **Psa 20:6** — "he will hear him from his holy (qodesh) heaven"
- **Dan 8:13** — "to give both the sanctuary (qodesh) and the host to be trodden under foot"

### 3. Most Holy — Superlative (Qodesh Qodashim)
The doubled construct qodesh qodashim ("holy of holies") marks the highest degree of sanctity.
- **Exo 26:33-34** — the veil separating the holy place from "the most holy" (qodesh haqqodashim)
- **Lev 27:28** — "every devoted thing is most holy (qodesh qodashim) unto the LORD"
- **Num 18:9** — "of the most holy things (qodesh haqqodashim), reserved from the fire"
- **Eze 43:12** — "Upon the top of the mountain the whole limit thereof... shall be most holy (qodesh qodashim)"
- **Dan 9:24** — "to anoint the most Holy (qodesh qodashim)"

### 4. Abstract Holiness — The Divine Quality
Qodesh used abstractly for the quality of holiness itself.
- **Exo 15:11** — "who is like thee, glorious in holiness (qodesh)"
- **Exo 28:36** — "HOLINESS (qodesh) TO THE LORD" engraved on the high priest's crown
- **Psa 29:2** — "worship the LORD in the beauty of holiness (qodesh)"
- **Psa 93:5** — "holiness (qodesh) becometh thine house, O LORD, for ever"

### 5. Holy Things — Sacred Objects
Items set apart for sacred use.
- **Exo 30:25** — "an oil of holy (qodesh) ointment... a holy anointing oil"
- **Lev 22:3** — "Whosoever... goeth unto the holy things (qodesh) having his uncleanness upon him"
- **1 Chr 23:13** — "Aaron was separated, that he should sanctify the most holy things (qodesh)"
- **Eze 44:23** — "teach my people the difference between the holy (qodesh) and profane"

### 6. Consecrated Status — Set-Apart State of Persons/Things
Qodesh marking something or someone as belonging to God.
- **Jer 2:3** — "Israel was holiness (qodesh) unto the LORD, and the firstfruits of his increase"
- **Psa 51:11** — "take not thy holy (qodesh) spirit from me"
- **Isa 63:10** — "they rebelled, and vexed his holy (qodesh) Spirit"
- **Zec 14:20** — "HOLINESS UNTO THE LORD" on the bells of the horses
- **Oba 1:17** — "upon mount Zion shall be deliverance, and there shall be holiness (qodesh)"
