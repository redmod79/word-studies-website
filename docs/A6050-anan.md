## Semantic Categories

### 1. Theophanic / Eschatological Cloud
The sole occurrence presents the cloud as the vehicle of a divine figure's approach to the throne of God.

- **Dan 7:13** — "I saw in the night visions, and, behold, one like the Son of man came with the clouds of heaven, and came to the Ancient of days, and they brought him near before him."

The plural construct עֲנָנֵי שְׁמַיָּא ("clouds of heaven") functions as both the medium of transportation and a marker of divine-realm origin. The figure arrives *with* (עִם) the clouds — not merely *in* or *through* them.
