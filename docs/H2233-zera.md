## Semantic Categories

### 1. Offspring / Descendants (Covenant Promise)
The dominant usage — descendants promised, identified, or addressed as a collective lineage.
- **Gen 12:7** — "Unto thy seed will I give this land"
- **Gen 15:5** — "So shall thy seed be" (stars analogy)
- **Gen 22:17** — "in multiplying I will multiply thy seed as the stars of the heaven, and as the sand"
- **Gen 22:18** — "in thy seed shall all the nations of the earth be blessed"
- **2 Sam 7:12** — "I will set up thy seed after thee, which shall proceed out of thy bowels"
- **Isa 53:10** — "he shall see his seed, he shall prolong his days"

### 2. National / Ethnic Identity

### 3. Agricultural Seed (Literal)
Physical seed for planting, harvest, and agricultural activity.
