## Roles in the Concept

- **Action:** opheilō (G3784) — the owing itself; also extended to moral "ought"
- **Thing Owed:** opheilēma (G3783) — the concrete debt/obligation (-μα result noun)
- **State:** opheilē (G3782) — the condition of indebtedness, the sum due
- **Person:** opheiletēs (G3781) — the debtor, the one under obligation
- **Compound Person:** chreopheiletēs (G5533) — specifically a loan-debtor
- **Compound Action:** prosopheilō (G4359) — additional/further indebtedness
- **Release:** aphiēmi (G863) — to send away, forgive, cancel the debt
- **Gracious Release:** charizomai (G5483) — to forgive freely, as a favor
