## Semantic Categories

Used of individuals or nations characterized by dangerous vehemence, especially when they show no mercy.

- **Gen 49:7** — "Cursed [be] their anger, for [it was] fierce (ʻaz); and their wrath, for it was cruel"

### 2. Strong People, City, or Fortification
