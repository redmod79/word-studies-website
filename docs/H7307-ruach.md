## Lexicon Definition

Wind; by resemblance breath, i.e. a sensible (or even violent) exhalation; figuratively, life, anger, unsubstantiality; by extension, a region of the sky; by resemblance spirit, but only of a rational being (including its expression and functions): air, anger, blast, breath, cool, courage, mind, quarter, side, spirit(-ual), tempest, vain, (whirl-)wind(-y).

## Semantic Categories

### 1. Spirit of God / Divine Spirit
The Spirit as God's active presence and power in creation, prophecy, and empowerment.
- **Gen 1:2** — "the Spirit (ruach) of God moved upon the face of the waters"
- **Exo 31:3** — "I have filled him with the spirit of God, in wisdom, and in understanding"
- **Isa 11:2** — "the spirit of the LORD shall rest upon him, the spirit of wisdom and understanding, the spirit of counsel and might, the spirit of knowledge and of the fear of the LORD"
- **Isa 61:1** — "The Spirit of the Lord GOD is upon me; because the LORD hath anointed me"
- **Joel 2:28** — "I will pour out my spirit upon all flesh"
- **Eze 36:27** — "I will put my spirit within you, and cause you to walk in my statutes"

### 2. Wind / Meteorological Force
Physical wind — east wind, storm wind, the four winds.
- **Gen 8:1** — "God made a wind (ruach) to pass over the earth, and the waters asswaged"
- **Exo 10:13** — "the LORD brought an east wind (ruach) upon the land all that day"
- **Exo 15:10** — "Thou didst blow with thy wind (ruach), the sea covered them"
- **Jer 49:36** — "upon Elam will I bring the four winds (ruchoth) from the four quarters of heaven"
- **Jon 4:8** — "God prepared a vehement east wind (ruach)"

### 3. Breath / Life-Force
The animating breath that sustains life; its departure means death.
- **Gen 6:17** — "all flesh, wherein is the breath (ruach) of life"
- **Job 33:4** — "The Spirit (ruach) of God hath made me, and the breath of the Almighty hath given me life"
- **Psa 104:30** — "Thou sendest forth thy spirit (ruach), they are created"
- **Ecc 3:21** — "Who knoweth the spirit (ruach) of man that goeth upward, and the spirit of the beast that goeth downward"
- **Ecc 12:7** — "the spirit (ruach) shall return unto God who gave it"

### 4. Human Spirit / Disposition
Inner state — attitude, courage, grief, resolve, emotional condition.
- **Gen 41:8** — "his spirit (ruach) was troubled" (Pharaoh's agitation)
- **Exo 6:9** — "they hearkened not unto Moses for anguish of spirit (ruach)"
- **Psa 51:10** — "renew a right spirit (ruach) within me"
- **Pro 16:32** — "he that ruleth his spirit (ruach) than he that taketh a city"
- **Pro 18:14** — "The spirit (ruach) of a man will sustain his infirmity; but a wounded spirit who can bear?"
- **Dan 2:1** — "his spirit (ruach) was troubled, and his sleep brake from him"

### 5. Spirit Beings (Non-Divine)
Evil spirits, lying spirits, spirits of whoredom.
- **1 Sam 16:14** — "the Spirit of the LORD departed from Saul, and an evil spirit (ruach) from the LORD troubled him"
- **1 Ki 22:21** — "there came forth a spirit (ruach), and stood before the LORD, and said, I will persuade him"
- **Hos 4:12** — "the spirit (ruach) of whoredoms hath caused them to err"

### 6. Vanity / Futility (Ecclesiastes)
"Vexation of spirit" — pursuit of wind, emptiness.
- **Ecc 1:14** — "all is vanity and vexation of spirit (ruach)"
- **Ecc 2:11** — "all was vanity and vexation of spirit (ruach)"
- **Ecc 4:6** — "than both the hands full with travail and vexation of spirit (ruach)"

### 7. Compass Direction / Side
Spatial orientation — sides of a structure, quarters of heaven.
- **Eze 42:16–20** — measurements of the temple on each "side" (ruach)
- **Jer 52:23** — "ninety and six pomegranates on a side (ruach)"
