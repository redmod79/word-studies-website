## Roles in the Concept

- **The Institution:** σάββατον (G4521) — the day itself, whether kept, broken, or debated
- **The Venue:** συναγωγή (G4864) — where sabbath-keeping takes observable form; sabbath and synagogue appear together in 15% of all sabbath verses
- **The Controversy:** ἔξεστι (G1832) — the permission question that organizes Jesus' sabbath disputes; appears in 30% of all ἔξεστι uses
- **The Eschatological Depth:** σαββατισμός (G4520) — the sabbath reframed as a remaining rest for the people of God (Heb 4:9)
- **The Temporal Frame:** ἡμέρα (G2250) — "the sabbath day," "that day," "the first day of the week" — calibrates sabbath in relation to other days
