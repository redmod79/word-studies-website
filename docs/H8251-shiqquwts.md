## Lexicon Definition

Disgusting, i.e. filthy; especially idolatrous or (concretely) an idol. Also written שִׁקֻּץ (shiqquts). From שָׁקַץ (shaqats): to be filthy, to loathe, pollute. Used for abominable filth, idol, detestable thing.
