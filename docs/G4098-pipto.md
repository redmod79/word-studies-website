## Semantic Categories

### 1. Prostration in Worship or Reverence
Falling before a superior as an act of worship, awe, or submission. Frequently paired with proskyneo (G4352).
- **Mat 2:11** — wise men "fell down, and worshipped him"
- **Rev 4:10** — twenty-four elders "fall down before him that sat on the throne, and worship"
- **Rev 5:14** — "the four and twenty elders fell down and worshipped"
- **Rev 7:11** — angels "fell before the throne on their faces, and worshipped God"
- **Rev 11:16** — elders "fell upon their faces, and worshipped God"
- **Rev 19:4** — elders and beasts "fell down and worshipped God"
- **1Co 14:25** — "falling down on his face he will worship God"

### 2. Prostration in Awe, Fear, or Supplication
Falling before someone out of fear, desperation, or supplication — not necessarily worship.
- **Mat 17:6** — disciples "fell on their face, and were sore afraid"
- **Mat 18:26** — servant "fell down, and worshipped him, saying, Lord, have patience"
- **Mat 18:29** — fellowservant "fell down at his feet, and besought him"
- **Rev 1:17** — "I fell at his feet as dead"
- **Rev 19:10** — "I fell at his feet to worship him" (corrected by the angel)
- **Rev 22:8** — "I fell down to worship before the feet of the angel"

### 3. Destruction or Ruin of Cities and Powers
Catastrophic collapse of kingdoms, cities, or institutions — a definitive end.
- **Rev 14:8** — "Babylon is fallen, is fallen, that great city"
- **Rev 18:2** — "Babylon the great is fallen, is fallen"
- **Rev 16:19** — "the cities of the nations fell"
- **Rev 17:10** — "five are fallen, and one is"
- **Luk 11:17** — "a house divided against a house falleth"
- **Heb 11:30** — "the walls of Jericho fell down"

### 4. Physical Falling — Seeds and Objects
Literal downward movement of objects, seeds, or persons.
- **Mat 13:4–8** — seeds "fell by the way side... upon stony places... among thorns... into good ground"
- **Mat 7:25, 27** — house "fell not" / "it fell: and great was the fall of it"
- **Mat 10:29** — sparrow "shall not fall on the ground without your Father"
- **Rev 8:10** — "there fell a great star from heaven"
- **Rev 6:13** — "the stars of heaven fell unto the earth"
- **Mat 24:29** — "the stars shall fall from heaven"

### 5. Cosmic and Angelic Falling
Stars or angelic beings cast down from heavenly position.
- **Luk 10:18** — "I beheld Satan as lightning fall from heaven"
- **Rev 9:1** — "I saw a star fall from heaven unto the earth"
- **Rev 8:10** — "there fell a great star from heaven, burning as it were a lamp"

### 6. Spiritual or Moral Falling
Loss of standing, faith, or spiritual position.
- **Rom 11:11** — "Have they stumbled that they should fall? God forbid"
- **Rom 11:22** — "on them which fell, severity"
- **1Co 10:12** — "let him that thinketh he standeth take heed lest he fall"
- **Heb 4:11** — "lest any man fall after the same example of unbelief"
- **Rev 2:5** — "remember therefore from whence thou art fallen"

### 7. Judgment Falling Upon / Crushing
The stone or mountains falling upon someone in judgment.
- **Mat 21:44** — "on whomsoever it shall fall, it will grind him to powder"
