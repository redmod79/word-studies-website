## Semantic Categories

### 1. Written Prophetic Record
- **2 Chr 9:29** — "are they not written in the book of Nathan the prophet, and in the prophecy of Ahijah the Shilonite, and in the visions of Iddo the seer?" — nebuw'ah names a written document attributed to a specific prophet, listed alongside "the book of Nathan" and "the visions of Iddo."

### 2. Spoken Prophetic Message Prompting Action
- **2 Chr 15:8** — "And when Asa heard these words, and the prophecy of Oded the prophet, he took courage, and put away the abominable idols" — nebuw'ah denotes an oral prophetic declaration that led to national reform.

### 3. False / Hired Prophetic Pronouncement
