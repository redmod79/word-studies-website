# WG: Camp / Encampment

## Overview
The camp concept in Hebrew Scripture centers on the organized dwelling-space of a community, encompassing the physical encampment, the act of pitching camp, the military host within it, the tents that compose it, and the people who inhabit it.

## Roles in the Concept

- **Space:** machaneh (מַחֲנֶה) — the bounded area of the encampment
- **Action:** chanah (חָנָה) — the act of setting up camp, pitching tents
- **Force:** tsaba (צָבָא) — the organized military or service contingent within the camp
- **Structure:** ohel (אֹהֶל) — the individual dwelling unit (tent) that composes the camp
- **Inhabitants:** am (עַם) — the people who dwell in and constitute the camp
- **Assembly:** qahal (קָהָל) — the people gathered as a formal body within the camp
