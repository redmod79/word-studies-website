## Roles in the Concept

- **The act (abstract):** πορνεία (G4202) — fornication as a category of sin
- **The action (verb):** πορνεύω (G4203) — actively committing the act
- **The female agent:** πόρνη (G4204) — the harlot/prostitute; used literally and of Babylon
- **The male agent:** πόρνος (G4205) — the male fornicator; in exclusion lists (1 Cor 5:9; Heb 12:16; Rev 21:8)
- **Parallel sin (narrower):** μοιχεία (G3430) — adultery, i.e. sex involving a married person; porneia is the genus, moicheia a species
- **Broader category:** ἀκαθαρσία (G167) — moral impurity/uncleanness; porneia is a subset of this
- **Attitudinal dimension:** ἀσέλγεια (G766) — licentiousness, outrageous sensuality; the shamelessness behind the acts
