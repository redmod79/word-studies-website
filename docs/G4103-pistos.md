## Lexicon Definition

From πιστεύω (G4100); objectively, trustworthy; subjectively, trustful. Used of persons who can be relied upon, and of those who place their trust/faith in God or Christ.

## Semantic Categories

### 1. Faithful as a Character Quality of Persons (Servants, Ministers, Disciples)
Applied to human beings who prove reliable in their responsibilities.
- **Matt 24:45** — "Who then is a faithful and wise servant, whom his lord hath made ruler over his household"
- **Matt 25:21** — "Well done, thou good and faithful servant: thou hast been faithful over a few things"
- **Luke 16:10** — "He that is faithful in that which is least is faithful also in much"
- **2 Tim 2:2** — "Commit thou to faithful men, who shall be able to teach others also"

### 2. God / Christ Described as Faithful (Divine Attribute)
πιστός as a divine title — God and Christ are reliable and keep their word.
- **1 Cor 1:9** — "God is faithful, by whom ye were called unto the fellowship of his Son Jesus Christ our Lord"
- **1 Cor 10:13** — "God is faithful, who will not suffer you to be tempted above that ye are able"
- **1 John 1:9** — "He is faithful and just to forgive us our sins"
- **Rev 19:11** — "He that sat upon him was called Faithful and True"

### 3. "Faithful Sayings" Formula (Pastoral Epistles)
A formulaic use — πιστὸς ὁ λόγος — affirming trustworthiness of a doctrinal statement.
- **1 Tim 1:15** — "This is a faithful saying, and worthy of all acceptation, that Christ Jesus came into the world to save sinners"
- **1 Tim 4:9** — "This is a faithful saying and worthy of all acceptation"
- **2 Tim 2:11** — "It is a faithful saying: For if we be dead with him, we shall also live with him"
- **Titus 3:8** — "This is a faithful saying, and these things I will that thou affirm constantly"

### 4. Believing / Those Who Believe (Active Sense)
Occasionally πιστός describes those who hold faith — believers as a class.
- **Acts 10:45** — "They of the circumcision which believed were astonished" (οἱ πιστοί)
- **Eph 1:1** — "To the saints which are at Ephesus, and to the faithful in Christ Jesus"
- **1 Tim 4:12** — "Be thou an example of the believers"
- **1 Tim 6:2** — "They that have believing masters, let them not despise them"

### 5. Christ as the Faithful Witness / Faithful High Priest
Christ's faithfulness as the foundation of the new covenant.
- **Rev 1:5** — "From Jesus Christ, who is the faithful witness, and the first begotten of the dead"
- **Rev 3:14** — "These things saith the Amen, the faithful and true witness"
- **Heb 2:17** — "A merciful and faithful high priest in things pertaining to God"
- **Heb 3:2** — "Who was faithful to him that appointed him, as also Moses was faithful in all his house"
