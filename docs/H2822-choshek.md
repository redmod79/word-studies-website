## Semantic Categories

### 1. Primordial/Created Darkness
The physical darkness of creation, over which God exercises sovereignty.
- **Gen 1:2** — "and darkness was upon the face of the deep"
- **Gen 1:4** — "God divided the light from the darkness"
- **Gen 1:18** — "to divide the light from the darkness"
- **Isa 45:7** — "I form the light, and create darkness"
- **Psalm 104:20** — "Thou makest darkness, and it is night"

### 2. Theophanic Darkness — God's Concealing Presence
Darkness as the envelope surrounding God's manifestation, especially at Sinai.
- **Deut 4:11** — "the mountain burned with fire... with darkness, clouds, and thick darkness"
- **Deut 5:23** — "ye heard the voice out of the midst of the darkness"
- **2 Sam 22:12** — "He made darkness pavilions round about him"
- **Psalm 18:11** — "He made darkness his secret place"

### 3. Judgment and the Day of the LORD
Darkness as the signature of divine judgment, especially in prophetic eschatology.
- **Exo 10:21-22** — plague of darkness over Egypt, "a thick darkness in all the land"
- **Joel 2:2** — "A day of darkness and of gloominess"
- **Joel 2:31** — "The sun shall be turned into darkness, and the moon into blood"
- **Amos 5:18, 20** — "the day of the LORD is darkness, and not light... even very dark, and no brightness in it"
- **Zeph 1:15** — "a day of darkness and gloominess"
- **Nahum 1:8** — "darkness shall pursue his enemies"
- **Isa 60:2** — "the darkness shall cover the earth, and gross darkness the people"

### 4. Death, Sheol, and the Grave
Darkness as the condition of death and the realm of the dead.
- **Job 10:21** — "the land of darkness and the shadow of death"
- **Job 17:13** — "I have made my bed in the darkness"

### 5. Moral/Spiritual Darkness — Wickedness and Ignorance
