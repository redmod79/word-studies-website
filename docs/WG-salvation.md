## Relationships

### Shared Root
All five words derive from the root י-ש-ע (y-sh-ayin). Yeshuah (H3444) is a feminine passive participle of yasha (H3467) — literally "something saved." Yesha (H3468) is the masculine noun form. Teshuah (H8668) adds the tav-prefix, emphasizing the act/event of rescue.
