## Lexicon Definition

From an unused root meaning to apportion, i.e. bestow; a donation; euphemistically, tribute; specifically a sacrificial offering (usually bloodless and voluntary): — gift, oblation, (meat) offering, present, sacrifice.

## Semantic Categories

### 1. Levitical Grain/Cereal Offering (Ritual)
The dominant usage — the prescribed offering of fine flour, oil, and frankincense presented to YHWH.
- **Lev 2:1** — "when any will offer a meat offering unto the LORD, his offering shall be of fine flour; and he shall pour oil upon it, and put frankincense thereon"
- **Lev 2:13** — "every oblation of thy meat offering shalt thou season with salt; neither shalt thou suffer the salt of the covenant of thy God to be lacking"
- **Num 28:5** — "a tenth part of an ephah of flour for a meat offering, mingled with the fourth part of an hin of beaten oil"
- **Lev 6:14** — "this is the law of the meat offering: the sons of Aaron shall offer it before the LORD, before the altar"

### 2. Companion to Other Sacrifices
Regularly paired with the burnt offering (olah) and drink offering (nesek) as a composite liturgical unit.
- **Num 29:6** — "beside the burnt offering of the month, and his meat offering, and the daily burnt offering, and his meat offering, and their drink offerings"
- **Ezek 45:17** — "it shall be the prince's part to give burnt offerings, and meat offerings, and drink offerings"
- **Joel 1:9** — "the meat offering and the drink offering is cut off from the house of the LORD"
- **Neh 10:33** — "for the continual meat offering, and for the continual burnt offering"

### 3. General Gift or Present (Secular/Diplomatic)
Used for gifts between humans — tribute, diplomatic presents, or gifts to gain favor.
- **Gen 32:13** — "took of that which came to his hand a present for Esau his brother"
- **Gen 43:11** — "take of the best fruits in the land in your vessels, and carry down the man a present"
- **Judg 3:17** — "he brought the present unto Eglon king of Moab"
- **2 Kings 8:8-9** — "Take a present in thine hand, and go, meet the man of God"

### 4. Tribute from Vassal Nations
Political submission expressed through gift-giving.
- **2 Sam 8:2** — "the Moabites became David's servants, and brought gifts"

- **1 Kings 4:21** — "they brought presents, and served Solomon all the days of his life"
- **2 Chron 17:11** — "the Philistines brought Jehoshaphat presents, and tribute silver"

### 5. Pre-Levitical Offering to God
Early worship offerings before the Mosaic system was established.
- **Gen 4:3** — "Cain brought of the fruit of the ground an offering unto the LORD"

### 6. Evening Sacrifice / Time Marker
The minchah as a time reference — "the time of the evening offering."
- **1 Kings 18:29** — "when midday was past, and they prophesied until the time of the offering of the evening sacrifice"
- **1 Kings 18:36** — "at the time of the offering of the evening sacrifice, that Elijah the prophet came near"
- **Ezra 9:5** — "at the evening sacrifice I arose up from my heaviness"

### 7. Prophetic Critique and Eschatological Hope
Prophets condemning corrupt offerings or anticipating purified future worship.
- **Isa 1:13** — "bring no more vain oblations"
- **Amos 5:22** — "though ye offer me burnt offerings and your meat offerings, I will not accept them"
- **Mal 1:10-11** — "neither do ye kindle fire on mine altar for nought... in every place incense shall be offered unto my name, and a pure offering"
- **Mal 3:3-4** — "he shall purify the sons of Levi... then shall the offering of Judah and Jerusalem be pleasant unto the LORD"
