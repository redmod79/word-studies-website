## Roles in the Concept

- **Act:** σταυρόω (G4717) — the specific act of crucifixion
- **Instrument:** σταυρός (G4716) — the stake/cross itself
- **Intensification:** ἀνασταυρόω (G388) — crucifying again (figurative, Heb 6:6)
- **Physical detail:** προσπήγνυμι (G4362) — the fastening/nailing (Acts 2:23)
- **Broader category:** ἀποκτείνω (G615) — killing in general; crucifixion is a species of this
- **Judicial overlap:** ἀναιρέω (G337) — used in Acts for Jesus's execution
- **Result:** θάνατος (G2288) — death; the outcome of crucifixion
