## Roles in the Concept

- **θεός (G2316)** — the supreme divine being; used absolutely for the Father, but also applied to Christ (John 20:28; Heb 1:8) and in the "Word was God" formula (John 1:1)
- **κύριος (G2962)** — title of sovereign authority; in LXX stands for YHWH; in NT applied to both God the Father and Christ
- **Χριστός (G5547)** — the anointed Messiah; the divine-human figure who unites θεός and κύριος in a single person
- **υἱός (G5207)** — relational term defining Christ's identity as God's Son; marks the unique divine relationship
- **πατήρ (G3962)** — the first person of the divine relationship; God as originator and sustainer
- **πνεῦμα (G4151)** — God's active presence and agent; "Spirit of God" distinguishes divine activity from created spirit
- **βασιλεία (G932)** — the realm and exercise of God's sovereignty; "kingdom of God" links theological identity to historical mission
