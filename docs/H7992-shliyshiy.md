## Lexicon Definition

Ordinal from H7969; **third**; feminine **a third (part)**; by extension, a third (day, year or time); specifically, a third-story cell. KJV: third (part, rank, time), three (years old).

## Semantic Categories

### 1. Third Day — Pivotal Temporal Marker
The most frequent use: marking the third day as a moment of resolution, arrival, or divine action.
- **Gen 1:13** — "And the evening and the morning were the third day." (creation)
- **Gen 22:4** — "Then on the third day Abraham lifted up his eyes, and saw the place afar off." (binding of Isaac)
- **Exo 19:16** — "And it came to pass on the third day in the morning, that there were thunders and lightnings..." (Sinai theophany)
- **Hos 6:2** — "After two days will he revive us: in the third day he will raise us up, and we shall live in his sight."

### 2. Third in Sequence — Birth Order and Ranked Lists
Used extensively in genealogies and priestly/military divisions to mark the third position.
- **1 Chr 2:13** — "And Jesse begat his firstborn Eliab, and Abinadab the second, and Shimma the third"
- **1 Chr 3:15** — "the firstborn Johanan, the second Jehoiakim, the third Zedekiah, the fourth Shallum"
- **1 Chr 24:8** — "The third to Harim, the fourth to Seorim"
- **1 Chr 26:2** — "Zechariah the firstborn, Jediael the second, Zebadiah the third"

### 3. A Third Part — Fractional Division
The feminine/partitive form: dividing something into three portions, often with ominous force.
- **Eze 5:2** — "Thou shalt burn with fire a third part in the midst of the city... and thou shalt take a third part, and smite about it with a knife: and a third part thou shalt scatter in the wind"
- **Eze 5:12** — "A third part of thee shall die with the pestilence... a third part shall fall by the sword... and I will scatter a third part into all the winds"
- **Zec 13:8-9** — "two parts therein shall be cut off and die; but the third shall be left therein. And I will bring the third part through the fire, and will refine them as silver is refined"
- **2 Sam 18:2** — "And David sent forth a third part of the people under the hand of Joab, and a third part under the hand of Abishai"

### 4. Third Year / Month — Calendrical Marker
Marking the third unit in annual or monthly cycles.
- **1 Ki 18:1** — "the word of the LORD came to Elijah in the third year, saying, Go, shew thyself unto Ahab"
- **Exo 19:1** — "In the third month, when the children of Israel were gone forth out of the land of Egypt"
- **2 Chr 31:7** — "In the third month they began to lay the foundation of the heaps"
- **Deut 26:12** — "When thou hast made an end of tithing all the tithes of thine increase the third year"

### 5. Third Repetition — Intensity or Confirmation
Marking a third attempt or occurrence, often signifying decisiveness.
- **1 Sam 3:8** — "And the LORD called Samuel again the third time."
- **1 Sam 19:21** — "Saul sent messengers again the third time, and they prophesied also."
- **2 Ki 1:13** — "And he sent again a captain of the third fifty with his fifty."

### 6. Third Story / Structural Division
Architectural usage for the third level of a building.
- **Gen 6:16** — "with lower, second, and third [stories] shalt thou make it." (Noah's ark)
- **1 Ki 6:6** — "The nethermost chamber was five cubits broad, and the middle was six cubits broad, and the third was seven cubits broad"
- **Eze 42:3** — "Over against the twenty cubits... gallery against gallery in three stories"
