## Semantic Categories

### 1. Inauguration of the Old Covenant by Blood
The Mosaic covenant required blood to be formally established as a binding instrument.
- **Heb 9:18** — "Whereupon neither the first [testament] was dedicated without blood."

### 2. Inauguration of the New and Living Way
Christ's death opened/consecrated a new access route into the heavenly sanctuary.
- **Heb 10:20** — "By a new and living way, which he hath consecrated for us, through the veil, that is to say, his flesh."
