## Lexicon Definition

An appointment, i.e. a fixed time or season; specifically a festival; conventionally a year; by implication an assembly (as convened for a definite purpose); technically the congregation; by extension the place of meeting; also a signal (as appointed beforehand). Feminine form מוֹעָדָה (mo'adah) in 2 Chr 8:13.

## Semantic Categories

### 1. Place of Meeting — "Tabernacle of the Congregation" (ohel mo'ed)
The dominant usage (150x). Mo'ed in this phrase denotes the tent as the appointed place where God meets Israel.
- **Exo 27:21** — "In the tabernacle of the congregation without the vail... Aaron and his sons shall order it"
- **Exo 33:7** — "Moses took the tabernacle, and pitched it without the camp... and called it the Tabernacle of the congregation"
- **Lev 1:1** — "the LORD called unto Moses, and spake unto him out of the tabernacle of the congregation"
- **Jos 18:1** — "the whole congregation of the children of Israel assembled together at Shiloh, and set up the tabernacle of the congregation"

### 2. Appointed Festivals / Sacred Feasts
Mo'ed as the divinely appointed feasts and holy days.
- **Lev 23:2** — "the feasts of the LORD, which ye shall proclaim to be holy convocations, even these are my feasts"
- **Lev 23:4** — "These are the feasts of the LORD, even holy convocations, which ye shall proclaim in their seasons"
- **Lev 23:44** — "Moses declared unto the children of Israel the feasts of the LORD"
- **Num 29:39** — "These things ye shall do unto the LORD in your set feasts"

### 3. Appointed / Fixed Time
A divinely determined point in time, especially in promises and prophecy.
- **Gen 17:21** — "my covenant will I establish with Isaac, which Sarah shall bear unto thee at this set time in the next year"
- **Gen 18:14** — "At the time appointed I will return unto thee... and Sarah shall have a son"
- **Dan 8:19** — "at the time appointed the end shall be"
- **Dan 11:27** — "the end shall be at the time appointed"
- **Dan 11:35** — "to the time of the end: because it is yet for a time appointed"
- **Hab 2:3** — "the vision is yet for an appointed time, but at the end it shall speak, and not lie"

### 4. Seasons / Natural Cycles
The appointed rhythms of nature.
- **Gen 1:14** — "let them be for signs, and for seasons, and for days, and years"
- **Psa 104:19** — "He appointed the moon for seasons: the sun knoweth his going down"
- **Jer 8:7** — "the stork in the heaven knoweth her appointed times"

### 5. Assembly / Congregation (non-tabernacle)
A gathering convened at an appointed place.
- **Psa 74:4** — "Thine enemies roar in the midst of thy congregations"
- **Psa 74:8** — "they have burned up all the synagogues of God in the land"
- **Isa 14:13** — "I will sit also upon the mount of the congregation, in the sides of the north"

### 6. Feasts Destroyed / Ceased (Lament)
Mo'ed in contexts of judgment and loss, where appointed meetings have been cut off.
- **Lam 1:4** — "The ways of Zion do mourn, because none come to the solemn feasts"
- **Lam 2:6** — "the LORD hath caused the solemn feasts and sabbaths to be forgotten in Zion"
- **Lam 2:22** — "Thou hast called as in a solemn day my terrors round about"
- **Hos 2:11** — "I will also cause all her mirth to cease, her feast days, her new moons, and her sabbaths, and all her solemn feasts"
