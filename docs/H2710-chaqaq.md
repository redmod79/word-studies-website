## Lexicon Definition

A primitive root; properly, to hack, i.e. engrave (Judges 5:14, to be a scribe simply); by implication, to enact (laws being cut in stone or metal tablets in primitive times) or (generally) prescribe: — appoint, decree, governor, grave, lawgiver, note, pourtray, print, set.

## Semantic Categories

### 1. Physical Engraving / Carving (Qal)
The literal act of cutting into a surface — stone, rock, brick, or a body.

- **Isa 49:16** — "Behold, I have graven (Qal Perf) thee upon the palms of my hands; thy walls are continually before me"
- **Eze 4:1** — "take thee a tile, and lay it before thee, and pourtray (Qal Perf) upon it the city, even Jerusalem"
- **Isa 22:16** — "that graveth (Qal Ptcp) an habitation for himself in a rock"
- **Eze 23:14** — "images of the Chaldeans pourtrayed (Qal Ptcp) with vermilion"

### 2. Inscribing for Permanent Record (Qal / Hophal)
Engraving as the act of preserving words or testimony in a durable medium.

- **Job 19:23** — "Oh that my words were now written! oh that they were printed (Hophal Impf) in a book!"
- **Isa 30:8** — "Now go, write it before them in a table, and note it (Qal Impv) in a book, that it may be for the time to come for ever and ever"

### 3. Issuing a Decree / Enacting Law (Qal / Piel Imperfect)
The legislative use: to cut (i.e., formalize) an ordinance — whether righteous or corrupt.

- **Prov 8:15** — "By me kings reign, and princes decree (Piel Impf) justice"
- **Isa 10:1** — "Woe unto them that decree (Qal Ptcp) unrighteous decrees, and that write grievousness which they have prescribed"

### 4. The Lawgiver / Commander (Piel Participle as Title)
The mᵉchôqêq — "the one who enacts" — used as a substantive title for a ruler, commander, or sovereign lawgiver. The defining form of this word in legal and messianic contexts.

- **Gen 49:10** — "The sceptre shall not depart from Judah, nor a lawgiver (mᵉchôqêq) from between his feet, until Shiloh come"
- **Num 21:18** — "the nobles of the people digged it, by the direction of the lawgiver (mᵉchôqêq), with their staves"
- **Isa 33:22** — "For the LORD is our judge, the LORD is our lawgiver (mᵉchôqêq), the LORD is our king; he will save us"
- **Psa 60:7 / 108:8** — "Judah is my lawgiver (mᵉchôqêq)"

### 5. God's Fixed Cosmic Ordinances (Qal Infinitive Construct)
God's act of setting immovable boundaries into the fabric of creation.

- **Prov 8:27** — "when he set (Qal InfCon) a compass upon the face of the depth"
- **Prov 8:29** — "When he gave to the sea his decree (Qal InfCon), that the waters should not pass his commandment; when he appointed the foundations of the earth"

### 6. That Which Is Decreed — The Law as Object (Pual)
The passive of the Piel: not the act of enacting, but the enacted thing — the law itself as a fixed, inscribed object.

- **Prov 31:5** — "Lest they drink, and forget the law (mᵉchuqqâq, Pual Ptcp), and pervert the judgment of any of the afflicted"
