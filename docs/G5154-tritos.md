## Semantic Categories

### 1. The Third Day (Resurrection)
The resurrection formula — Christ's death and rising on the third day. In Synoptic passion predictions and the early creed of 1 Cor 15:4.

- **Mat 16:21** — "he must go unto Jerusalem, and suffer many things...and be raised again the third day"
- **Mat 17:23** — "they shall kill him, and the third day he shall be raised again"
- **1Co 15:4** — "he rose again the third day according to the scriptures"
- **Act 10:40** — "Him God raised up the third day, and shewed him openly"

### 2. Ordinal Sequence
Third in an enumerated series. Used in genealogies, listings, and apocalyptic sequences.

- **Mat 22:26** — "Likewise the second also, and the third, unto the seventh"
- **Rev 4:7** — "the third beast had a face as a man"
- **Rev 6:5** — "when he had opened the third seal...the third beast say, Come and see"
- **1Co 12:28** — "first apostles, secondarily prophets, thirdly teachers"

### 3. Time Reference (Hour / Watch)
The third hour of the Jewish day (~9 a.m.) and the third watch of the night.

- **Mat 20:3** — "he went out about the third hour, and saw others standing idle"
- **Mar 15:25** — "it was the third hour, and they crucified him"
- **Act 2:15** — "it is but the third hour of the day"
- **Luk 12:38** — "if he shall come in the...third watch, and find them so, blessed are those servants"

### 4. The Third Part (Fractional Judgment)
One-third of creation struck in Revelation's trumpet and dragon visions. Signals severe but partial devastation.

- **Rev 8:9** — "the third part of the creatures which were in the sea...died; and the third part of the ships were destroyed"
- **Rev 8:12** — "the third part of the sun was smitten, and the third part of the moon, and the third part of the stars"
- **Rev 9:18** — "By these three was the third part of men killed"
- **Rev 12:4** — "his tail drew the third part of the stars of heaven, and did cast them to the earth"

### 5. The Third Time (Repetition / Climax)
An action done a third time, often marking a climactic or emphatic repetition.

- **Mat 26:44** — "he went away again, and prayed the third time, saying the same words"
- **Jhn 21:17** — "He saith unto him the third time, Simon, son of Jonas, lovest thou me?"
- **Mar 14:41** — "he cometh the third time, and saith unto them, Sleep on now, and take your rest"
- **2Co 13:1** — "This is the third time I am coming to you"
