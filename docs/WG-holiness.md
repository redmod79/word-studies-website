## Roles in the Concept

- **Quality/State:** qodesh (קֹדֶשׁ) — the fundamental category of "set apart for God"
- **Action:** qadash (קָדַשׁ) — the process of making something holy or declaring it holy
- **Attribute:** qadowsh (קָדוֹשׁ) — the characteristic of being holy (esp. of God: "the Holy One")
- **Location:** miqdash (מִקְדָּשׁ) — the dedicated physical space where holiness dwells
- **Agent:** kohen (כֹּהֵן) — the person authorized to operate in the sphere of holiness
- **Sacred geography:** har (הַר) — "holy mountain" as the place where God's presence is manifest
