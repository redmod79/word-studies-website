- **Exo 12:5** - the Passover lamb is "without blemish."

- **Num 6:14** - Nazirite offerings include lambs and a ram "without blemish."

### 2. Moral Integrity / Uprightness
- **Gen 6:9** - Noah is described as "perfect in his generations."
- **Deut 18:13** - Israel is commanded to be "perfect with the LORD thy God."
- **Prov 2:21** - "the perfect shall remain" in the land.
- **Prov 28:18** - the one who walks "uprightly" is contrasted with crooked ways.

### 3. Whole / Complete State
- **Lev 25:30** - a house remains through a "full year."

- **Josh 24:14** - serve YHWH "in sincerity and in truth."
- **Judg 9:16** - action "truly and sincerely" toward Jerubbaal.
- **Amos 5:10** - one who speaks "uprightly" in the gate.
