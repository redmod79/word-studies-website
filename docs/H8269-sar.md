## Lexicon Definition

A head person (of any rank or class): captain (that had rule), chief (captain), general, governor, keeper, lord, (-task-)master, prince(-ipal), ruler, steward.

The breadth of the definition is key: sar is a functional title for whoever holds authority *over* a defined group — a captain over soldiers, a prince over nobles, an official over a household. Rank and sphere vary; the relational superiority does not.
