# WG: Dream / Vision / Prophetic Revelation

## Overview

In the Hebrew Scriptures, divine communication through sleep-state imagery (dream) and waking-state imagery (vision) forms a tightly-knit cluster paired with the human channel (the prophet). The words in this group appear together in formulaic lists, polemics against false prophets, and eschatological promises of universal revelation.

## Relationships

### Shared Roots
- **chalom (H2472), chalam (H2492), chelem (H2493)** — all from the root חלם; chelem is the Aramaic cognate of Hebrew chalom
- **chazon (H2377), chizzayon (H2384)** — both from חזה (chazah, to see/perceive); chazon is the stable noun, chizzayon the intensive form typically linked to "night vision"

### Multi-Word Passages (3+ members in one verse)

- **Deut 13:1, 3, 5** — chalom + chalam + nabi — law on the false "prophet or dreamer of dreams"
- **Jer 29:8** — chalom + chalam + nabi — warning against prophets, diviners, and dreamers in Babylon

These three verse-clusters are the high-density center of the concept: a legal frame (Deut), a polemical frame (Jer), and an eschatological-promise frame (Joel).

## Roles in the Concept

- **Mode — sleep:** chalom (חֲלוֹם), chelem (Aramaic), chalam (verb) — revelation while asleep
- **Mode — sight:** chazon (חָזוֹן), chizzayon (חִזָּיוֹן) — revelation as seen image; chizzayon specifically at night, bridging to dream
- **Channel:** nabi (נָבִיא) — the human prophet who receives and proclaims
- **Shared frame:** all presuppose God as speaker/agent and require authentication (Deut 13 test; Jer 23 "word of the LORD" test)

## Key Passages Where Multiple Group Words Appear Together

- **Num 12:6** — "If there be a prophet (nabi) among you, I the LORD will make myself known unto him in a vision (mareh), and will speak unto him in a dream (chalom)" — the foundational triad
- **Deut 13:1–5** — "prophet (nabi), or a dreamer of dreams (chalam + chalom)" — the test for revelation
- **1 Sam 28:6** — "neither by dreams (chalom), nor by Urim, nor by prophets (nabi)" — list of legitimate inquiry channels
- **Jer 23:25–32** — extended polemic linking false prophets, false dreams, false visions
- **Joel 2:28 → Acts 2:17** — "old men shall dream dreams (chalam + chalom), young men shall see visions (chizzayon)" — eschatological democratization
