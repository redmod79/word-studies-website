## Semantic Categories

### 1. Cosmic Re-creation / Messianic Renewal
- **Matt 19:28** — "And Jesus said unto them, Verily I say unto you, That ye which have followed me, in the regeneration when the Son of man shall sit in the throne of his glory, ye also shall sit upon twelve thrones, judging the twelve tribes of Israel."

Here paliggenesia refers to the eschatological renewal of all things — the re-created world order coinciding with the Son of Man's enthronement. The dative (παλινγενεσίᾳ) marks a temporal sphere: "in the regeneration" = in/during the renewed age.

### 2. Personal Spiritual Rebirth
- **Titus 3:5** — "Not by works of righteousness which we have done, but according to his mercy he saved us, by the washing of regeneration, and renewing of the Holy Ghost;"

Here paliggenesia describes individual spiritual rebirth — the inner transformation effected by divine mercy, coordinated with "washing" (λουτρόν, G3067) and "renewing" (ἀνακαίνωσις, G342). The genitive (παλινγενεσίας) modifies "washing."
