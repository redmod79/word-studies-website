## Continuity and Shifts

**What stays the same across testaments:**
- The blessing-as-divine-favor sense carries unchanged from berakah through eulogia
- The antithetic pairing with cursing persists: Deu 11:26 berakah/qelalah → Jas 3:10 eulogia/katara
- The Abrahamic blessing as covenantal promise is explicitly traced (Gal 3:14)

**What shifts:**
- NT eulogia develops a "generosity/bounty" sense (2 Cor 9:5–6 "bountifully") that, while present in OT berakah-as-gift (Gen 33:11), becomes more prominent
- The material-gift/present sense of berakah (1 Sam 25:27, 2 Ki 5:15) does not appear in NT eulogia
- NT adds "cup of blessing" (1 Cor 10:16) as a eucharistic technical term with no direct OT parallel
