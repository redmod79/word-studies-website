## Lexicon Definition

Or לָבֵשׁ *labesh*; a primitive root; properly, wrap around, i.e. (by implication) to put on a garment or clothe (oneself, or another), literally or figuratively: — (in) apparel, arm, array (self), clothe (self), come upon, put (on, upon), wear.

The Qal/Hiphil distinction is semantically significant: Qal is reflexive ("put on oneself"), while Hiphil is causative ("clothe someone else"). The Hiphil regularly has God or an authority figure as subject, clothing another with garments, honor, or attributes.
