## Semantic Categories

### 1. Unclean Spirits / Demonic Beings (21 of 30 tokens)
The dominant usage. Akathartos modifies πνεῦμα (pneuma, "spirit") to designate hostile spiritual entities Jesus and the apostles confronted.

- **Mat 10:1** — "he gave them power against unclean spirits, to cast them out, and to heal all manner of sickness"
- **Mark 1:23** — "there was in their synagogue a man with an unclean spirit; and he cried out"
- **Mark 3:11** — "unclean spirits, when they saw him, fell down before him, and cried, Thou art the Son of God"
- **Mark 5:8** — "Come out of the man, thou unclean spirit"
- **Luke 4:33** — "a man, which had a spirit of an unclean devil, and cried out with a loud voice"
- **Acts 5:16** — "bringing sick folks, and them which were vexed with unclean spirits: and they were healed every one"
- **Acts 8:7** — "unclean spirits, crying with loud voice, came out of many that were possessed"

### 2. Wandering / Homeless Unclean Spirit (Teaching Parable)
Jesus' teaching about a displaced spirit returning to its "house."

- **Mat 12:43** — "When the unclean spirit is gone out of a man, he walketh through dry places, seeking rest"
- **Luke 11:24** — "When the unclean spirit is gone out of a man, he walketh through dry places, seeking rest"

### 3. Levitical / Dietary Uncleanness
Peter's vision and the debate over clean/unclean foods — applying the Levitical category (טָמֵא) to the Gentile-inclusion question.

- **Acts 10:14** — "Not so, Lord; for I have never eaten any thing that is common or unclean"
- **Acts 10:28** — "God hath shewed me that I should not call any man common or unclean"
- **Acts 11:8** — "nothing common or unclean hath at any time entered into my mouth"

### 4. Moral / Ethical Impurity
Paul uses akathartos for moral defilement apart from demonic possession — sexual impurity, covetousness, or participation in pagan culture.

- **1 Cor 7:14** — "else were your children unclean: but now are they holy"
- **2 Cor 6:17** — "come out from among them, and be ye separate... and touch not the unclean thing"
- **Eph 5:5** — "no whoremonger, nor unclean person, nor covetous man, who is an idolater, hath any inheritance in the kingdom"

### 5. Eschatological / Apocalyptic Impurity
Revelation applies akathartos to demonic spirits and Babylon's spiritual contamination.

- **Rev 16:13** — "I saw three unclean spirits like frogs come out of the mouth of the dragon"
- **Rev 18:2** — "Babylon... is become the habitation of devils, and the hold of every foul spirit, and a cage of every unclean and hateful bird"
