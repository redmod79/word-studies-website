## Roles in the Concept

- **General darkness:** skotos (G4655) — the standard, broadly used term
- **Johannine darkness:** skotia (G4653) — John's preferred term for the dualistic light/darkness motif
- **Deep gloom:** zophos (G2217) — murky, subterranean darkness; Tartarus and final judgment
- **Process of darkening:** skotizo (G4654) — becoming dark (sun, moon, understanding)
- **Quality of darkness:** skoteinos (G4652) — adjectival ("full of darkness")
- **Antithesis:** phos (G5457) — the light that exposes, overcomes, and replaces darkness
