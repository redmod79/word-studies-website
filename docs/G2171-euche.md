## Semantic Categories

### 1. Vow — A Formal Obligation Before God
- **Acts 18:18** — "having shorn his head in Cenchrea: for he had a vow (εὐχήν)"
- **Acts 21:23** — "We have four men which have a vow (εὐχήν) on them"

Both instances describe Nazarite-like vows involving ritual obligations (head-shaving, purification period). The vow is a binding commitment with specific cultic actions attached.

### 2. Prayer — A Petition Addressed to God
- **James 5:15** — "And the prayer (εὐχή) of faith shall save the sick, and the Lord shall raise him up"

Here εὐχή denotes prayer offered on behalf of the sick, qualified by πίστις ("faith"). The context is elder-led intercession with anointing oil.
