## Lexicon Definition

From G2919 (κρίνω); a decision (the function or the effect, for or against). Thayer distinguishes three senses:

1. **A decree** — the decision itself (plural: God's unsearchable decrees, Rom 11:33)
2. **Judgment / sentence** — condemnation of wrong, the verdict of a judge; especially God's penal judgment (Rom 2:2; 5:16; 2 Pet 2:3; Jude 1:4); *λαμβάνειν κρίμα* = to receive condemnation (Matt 23:14; Rom 13:2)
3. **A legal case / lawsuit** — a matter to be judicially decided (1 Cor 6:7: κρίματα ἔχειν μετά τινος)

KJV renders: *judgment, damnation, condemnation, go to law, condemned.*
LXX uses κρίμα very often for H4941 מִשְׁפָּט (mishpat).

## Semantic Categories

### 1. Reciprocal Standard of Judgment
The *krimati* by which one judges becomes the standard by which one is judged.

- **Matt 7:2** — "For with what judgment (*krimati*) ye judge, ye shall be judged"

### 2. God's Judicial Verdicts / Decrees
κρίμα as God's authoritative verdict — accurate, inevitable, coming.

- **Rom 2:2** — "the judgment (*krima*) of God is according to truth against them which commit such things"
- **Rom 11:33** — "how unsearchable are his judgments (*krimata*), and his ways past finding out"
- **Acts 24:25** — "as he reasoned of righteousness, temperance, and judgment (*krimatos*) to come, Felix trembled"
- **2 Pet 2:3** — "whose judgment (*krima*) now of a long time lingereth not, and their damnation slumbereth not"

### 3. Condemnatory Sentence Received (*λαμβάνειν κρίμα*)
The idiomatic phrase "receive judgment/condemnation," used especially for those who exploit the vulnerable.

- **Matt 23:14** — "ye devour widows' houses... ye shall receive the greater damnation"
- **Mark 12:40** — "these shall receive greater damnation"
- **Rom 13:2** — "they that resist shall receive to themselves damnation (*krima*)"
- **James 3:1** — "we shall receive the greater condemnation (*krima*)"

### 4. Self-Incurred Condemnation
κρίμα that one brings on oneself by acting against the light received.

- **1 Cor 11:29** — "eateth and drinketh damnation (*krima*) to himself, not discerning the Lord's body"
- **Luke 23:40** — "thou art in the same condemnation (*krimati*)"
- **1 Tim 3:6** — "lest being lifted up with pride he fall into the condemnation (*krima*) of the devil"
- **Gal 5:10** — "he that troubleth you shall bear his judgment (*krima*)"

### 5. Eschatological and Christological Judgment
κρίμα in relation to final judgment, Christ's coming, and the vindication of the saints.

- **John 9:39** — "For judgment (*krima*) I am come into this world, that they which see not might see"
- **1 Pet 4:17** — "the time is come that judgment (*krima*) must begin at the house of God"
- **Heb 6:2** — "resurrection of the dead, and of eternal judgment (*krimatos*)"
- **Rev 20:4** — "judgment (*krima*) was given unto them... souls of them that were beheaded for the witness of Jesus"

### 6. Legal Disputes Between Humans
κρίμα in its narrow sense of a lawsuit or case in court.

- **1 Cor 6:7** — "ye go to law (*krimata*) one with another"
