## Continuity and Shifts

**What stays the same across testaments:**
- The physical "seize/grasp" sense carries directly from OT chazaq (Hiphil) through LXX krateo into NT krateo: angels seize Lot (Gen 19:16), Jesus takes the girl by the hand (Matt 9:25, Mark 1:31), soldiers seize Jesus (Matt 26:48–50)
- The "hold fast/retain" sense persists: Job holds fast his integrity (Job 27:6), believers hold fast traditions (2 Thess 2:15) and the faith (Heb 4:14)

**What shifts:**
- The OT's dominant "be strong/be courageous" exhortation sense (Josh 1:6, Dan 10:19) was NOT carried by krateo in the LXX — that sense went to krataioo (G2901), andrizomai (G407), and enischyo (G1765) instead. The NT krateo narrows to the "seize" and "hold fast" meanings.
- The "harden heart" sense mapped to skleruno (G4645) in the LXX rather than krateo, so this semantic thread bypasses krateo entirely in the NT (see Heb 3:8, 13, 15 quoting Ps 95 using skleruno).
- The "repair/restore" sense (Nehemiah) has no NT continuation through krateo at all.
