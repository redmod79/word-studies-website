## Roles in the Concept

- **The Sound:** qowl (קוֹל) — the voice, sound, or noise itself; ranges from whisper to thunder
- **The Response:** shama (שָׁמַע) — to hear, listen, obey; the human/divine response to the voice
- **The Utterance:** qara (קָרָא) — to call out, cry, proclaim; the act of producing directed speech
- **The Blast:** truah (תְּרוּעָה) — shout or trumpet blast; ritual and military sound-signal
- **The Rush:** shaon (שָׁאוֹן) — impersonal uproar, rushing; chaotic/destructive sound
- **The Roar:** hamon (הָמוֹן) — crowd-tumult, multitude-noise; collective commotion
