## Lexicon Definition

From ἀνά and παύω; (reflexively) to repose — literally (to rest, recline) or figuratively (to be refreshed, relieved, eased). The compound conveys the sense of causing labor or activity to cease, then by extension the state of repose that follows. In active voice the subject gives rest to another; in middle/passive the subject takes rest or is refreshed.
