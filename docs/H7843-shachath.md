## Semantic Categories

### 1. Moral / Spiritual Corruption (Niphal/Piel — becoming or causing to be corrupt)
- **Gen 6:11–12** — "The earth also was corrupt before God... all flesh had corrupted his way upon the earth" — universal moral decay as grounds for the Flood
- **Deut 4:16** — "Lest ye corrupt yourselves, and make you a graven image" — idolatry as self-corruption
- **Deut 32:5** — "They have corrupted themselves, their spot is not of his children"
- **Hos 9:9** — "They have deeply corrupted themselves, as in the days of Gibeah"

### 2. Physical Destruction of Cities, Nations, or Armies (Hiphil — active, causative)
- **Gen 19:13–14** — "We will destroy this place... the LORD will destroy this city" — Sodom's destruction

- **Lam 2:5–6** — "He hath swallowed up all her palaces: he hath destroyed his places of assembly"
