## Roles in the Concept

- **Broad moral category:** to'ebah (תּוֹעֵבַה) — the general term for what God finds detestable (moral, social, ritual)
- **Idolatrous object:** shiqquwts (שִׁקּוּץ) — the physically disgusting idol or cult object
- **Worthless idol:** gillul (גִּלּוּל) — the contemptible image (emphasizes futility, from "dung")
- **Ritual uncleanness:** sheqets (שֶׁקֶץ) — the unclean creature or object (dietary/purity laws)
- **Root action:** shaqats (שָׁקַץ) — the act of detesting or making abominable
- **Resulting state:** tame' (טָמֵא) — the defilement that results from contact with these things
