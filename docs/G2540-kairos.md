## Semantic Categories

### 1. Divinely Appointed Moment
The proper time fixed by God's plan — not just any time, but *the* right time.
- **Mark 1:15** — "The time is fulfilled, and the kingdom of God is at hand"
- **Matt 26:18** — "The Master saith, My time is at hand; I will keep the passover"
- **John 7:6** — "My time is not yet come: but your time is alway ready"
- **Matt 8:29** — "art thou come hither to torment us before the time?"

### 2. Eschatological Times
End-time events, judgment, and prophetic fulfillment.
- **Rev 1:3** / **Rev 22:10** — "the time is at hand"
- **Rev 11:18** — "the time of the dead, that they should be judged"
- **1 Pet 1:5** — "salvation ready to be revealed in the last time"
- **2 Tim 3:1** — "in the last days perilous times shall come"

### 3. God's Sovereign Timing
Times held in God's own authority; dispensational moments He reveals and controls.
- **Acts 1:7** — "the times or the seasons, which the Father hath put in his own power"
- **1 Tim 6:15** — "Which in his times he shall shew, the blessed and only Potentate"
- **Tit 1:3** — "in due times manifested his word through preaching"
- **Rom 5:6** — "in due time Christ died for the ungodly"

### 4. Opportune Moment / Season to Act
The right occasion for human action — to work, preach, act while opportunity exists.
- **Eph 5:16** / **Col 4:5** — "redeeming the time, because the days are evil"
- **Gal 6:9–10** — "in due season we shall reap" / "as we have therefore opportunity"
- **Acts 24:25** — Felix dismissed Paul, asking for "a convenient season"
- **Heb 11:15** — "they might have had opportunity" to return

### 5. Agricultural / Natural Season
The proper time of year for harvest or fruiting — the divinely ordered natural season.
- **Matt 13:30** — "in the time of harvest I will say to the reapers"
- **Matt 21:34** — "when the time of the fruit drew near"
- **Mark 11:13** — "for the time of figs was not yet"
