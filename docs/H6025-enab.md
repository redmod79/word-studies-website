- **Jer 8:13** — "there shall be no grapes on the vine, nor figs on the fig tree" (judgment oracle)

### 5. Grape in Eschatological Abundance

- **Amos 9:13** — "the treader of grapes him that soweth seed" (millennial overproduction)

### 6. Grape in Prophetic Object Lesson
