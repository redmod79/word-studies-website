**Distinction:** Bad is the technical term specifically for the white linen fabric of ordinary priestly garments and the Day of Atonement vestments. Shesh is the fine linen of the glory garments and tabernacle. Pishteh is the generic flax/linen thread. Buwts is a later term for fine white fabric.
