Concentrated in eschatological literature: Revelation (highest density), Petrine epistles, John's Gospel. Matthew and Mark carry Jesus' "first/last" reversal sayings. The eight Johannine occurrences cluster entirely in the "last day" resurrection formula.

## Semantic Categories

### 1. Divine Self-Designation: "The First and the Last"
Christ identifies himself as the absolute eschatos — no one comes after or beyond him.
- **Rev 1:11** — "I am Alpha and Omega, the first and the last"
- **Rev 1:17** — "Fear not; I am the first and the last"
- **Rev 2:8** — "These things saith the first and the last, which was dead, and is alive"
- **Rev 22:13** — "I am Alpha and Omega, the beginning and the end, the first and the last"

### 2. The Last Day (Resurrection Anchor)
John uses ἡ ἐσχάτη ἡμέρα as a formulaic anchor for the final resurrection — repeated four times in a single discourse (John 6).
- **Jhn 6:39–40** — "I should lose nothing… but should raise it up again at the last day"
- **Jhn 6:44** — "I will raise him up at the last day"
- **Jhn 11:24** — "I know that he shall rise again in the resurrection at the last day"
- **Jhn 12:48** — "the word that I have spoken, the same shall judge him in the last day"

### 3. The Last Days / Last Times (Eschatological Era)
The apostolic formula declaring the present age as the final epoch before consummation.
- **Acts 2:17** — "it shall come to pass in the last days, saith God, I will pour out of my Spirit"
- **2Ti 3:1** — "in the last days perilous times shall come"
- **Heb 1:2** — "Hath in these last days spoken unto us by his Son"
- **1Pe 1:5** — "salvation ready to be revealed in the last time"
- **2Pe 3:3** — "there shall come in the last days scoffers"
- **1Jo 2:18** — "it is the last time: and as ye have heard that antichrist shall come"

### 4. Social Reversal: "The Last Shall Be First"
Jesus inverts the calculus of status — those who are last in earthly ranking shall be first in the kingdom.
- **Mat 19:30** — "many that are first shall be last; and the last shall be first"
- **Mat 20:16** — "So the last shall be first, and the first last"
- **Mar 9:35** — "If any man desire to be first, the same shall be last of all, and servant of all"
- **Luk 13:30** — "there are last which shall be first, and there are first which shall be last"

### 5. The Last in Eschatological Events
Final items in an eschatological sequence — plagues, trumpet, enemy.
- **Rev 15:1** — "seven angels having the seven last plagues; for in them is filled up the wrath of God"
- **Rev 21:9** — "one of the seven angels which had the seven last plagues"
- **1Co 15:26** — "The last enemy that shall be destroyed is death"
- **1Co 15:45** — "the last Adam was made a quickening spirit"
- **1Co 15:52** — "at the last trump: for the trumpet shall sound, and the dead shall be raised"

### 6. Spatial / Sequential Extremity
The farthest or outermost position — used of geographic ends of the earth, the lowest seat, the final penny.
- **Mat 5:26** — "till thou hast paid the uttermost farthing"
- **Luk 14:9–10** — "begin with shame to take the lowest room… Go and sit down in the lowest room"
- **Acts 1:8** — "witnesses unto me… unto the uttermost part of the earth"
- **Acts 13:47** — "I have set thee to be a light… to the ends of the earth"
