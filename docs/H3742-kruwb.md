## Semantic Categories

### 1. Eden Boundary Guardians
- **Gen 3:24** — cherubims are placed east of Eden with a flaming sword.

### 2. Ark and Mercy Seat Figures
- **Exo 25:18-20** — two cherubims of gold are made at the ends of the mercy seat.
- **Exo 25:22** — speech comes "from between the two cherubims."
- **Num 7:89** — the voice is heard from above the mercy seat, between the cherubims.

### 3. Woven or Carved Sanctuary Imagery
- **Exo 26:1** — cherubims are worked into the tabernacle curtains.
- **Exo 26:31** — cherubims are worked into the veil.
- **1Ki 6:29** — cherubims are carved on temple walls with palm trees and open flowers.

### 4. Large Temple Figures
- **1Ki 6:23-28** — two large cherubims of olive wood stand in the oracle.
- **2Ch 3:10-13** — cherubims in the most holy house have outstretched wings.
- **1Ki 8:6-7** — the cherubims cover the ark and staves.

### 5. Throne/Presence Language
- **1Sa 4:4** — "the LORD of hosts, which dwelleth between the cherubims."
- **2Ki 19:15** — Hezekiah addresses YHWH as dwelling between the cherubims.
- **Isa 37:16** — same formula in Isaiah's parallel account.

### 6. Ezekiel Visionary Cherubim
- **Eze 10:1-5** — cherubim appear with firmament, wheels, cloud, and glory.
- **Eze 10:20** — identified with the living creature seen by Chebar.
- **Eze 28:14,16** — singular "cherub" appears in an oracle setting.
