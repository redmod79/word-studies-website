## Roles in the Concept

- **Sequence marker:** All four ordinals enumerate items in a series (seals, angels, foundations, watches, months)
- **Third day (resurrection timing):** τρίτος + ἡμέρα — the creedal formula for Christ's resurrection (Mat 16:21; 1Co 15:4)
- **Third part (fractional judgment):** τρίτος neuter — one-third of creation struck in Revelation trumpet/dragon visions (Rev 8:7–12; 9:15, 18; 12:4)
- **Third time (climactic repetition):** τρίτος as adverb — action done three times marks emphasis or completion (Jhn 21:17; Mat 26:44)
