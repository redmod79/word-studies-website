# WG: Strife (Hebrew Legal Contention)

## Overview
The Hebrew legal-contention concept involves a cluster of words covering the dispute brought, the judgment rendered, the act of judging, and the advocate who takes up the case. These words frequently co-occur in legal proceedings passages (Deu 17:8, 25:1), the prophetic covenant-lawsuit genre (Hos 4:1, Mic 6:2), and psalmic pleas for vindication (Psa 43:1, 119:154).
