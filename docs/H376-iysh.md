## Lexical-Control Note for `ish itti`

In Lev 16:21, H376 supplies the ordinary noun "man/person." The specificity of the phrase comes from H6261 (`itti, "fit/timely/ready"), not from H376 alone. Therefore `ish itti should be treated lexically as "a man/person qualified as fit/ready," without turning H376 into a ritual office term.
