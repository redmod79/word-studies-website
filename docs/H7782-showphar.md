## Lexicon Definition

From an unused root in the original sense of incising; a cornet (as giving a clear sound) or curved horn — **cornet, trumpet**.

The shôwphâr is the ram's horn instrument, a natural hollow horn used as a signal device. Distinct from the priestly silver trumpet (H2689 חֲצֹצְרָה).
