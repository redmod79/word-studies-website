### -σις vs. -μα Suffix Distinction
The Greek suffix -σις (dikaiō**sis**) denotes the *act or process* of justifying — the declaring righteous. The suffix -μα (dikaiō**ma**) denotes the *result or product* — the righteous deed, decree, or regulation. In Rom 5:16–18 Paul uses both: the δικαίωμα (righteous act, v. 18) is the ground; the δικαίωσις (justification, vv. 16, 18) is the verdict that follows from it.
