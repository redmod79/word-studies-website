## Roles in the Concept

- **Collective body:** plēthos (πλῆθος) — the group as a unified whole, often acting with one will
- **Unstructured crowd:** ochlos (ὄχλος) — the assembled mass, often spontaneous, mobile, reactive
- **Identified people:** laos (λαός) — the people with covenant or political identity
- **Civic body:** dēmos (δῆμος) — the formally constituted public assembly
- **Quantity modifier:** polys (πολύς) — "great/many," amplifies any of the nouns
- **Growth action:** plēthynō (πληθύνω) — the process of increasing in number
