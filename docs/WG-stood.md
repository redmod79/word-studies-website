## Roles in the Concept

| Word | Core Role |
|------|-----------|
| ἵστημι (G2476) | Stand/set — the full range: physical position, causative placement, figurative stability, legal establishment |
| ἀνίστημι (G450) | Directional rising: resurrection, standing up from seated/dead, raising a successor |
| παρίστημι (G3936) | Relational standing: *beside* someone; presenting/offering to someone |
| στήκω (G4739) | Volitional steadfastness: "stand firm" in faith or duty (always figurative in NT) |
| στάσις (G4714) | The act or event of standing: position → dissension → insurrection (people "taking their stand") |
