## Semantic Categories

With only two occurrences, semantic range is narrow but both verses pair hosiotes with dikaiosyne ("righteousness"), establishing a consistent pattern.

### 1. Covenantal Devotion Before God
- **Luk 1:75** — "In holiness (hosiotes) and righteousness before him, all the days of our life."
  Zechariah's prophecy describes the purpose of Israel's deliverance: lifelong service characterized by hosiotes paired with dikaiosyne, performed "before him" (enopion autou) — indicating God-facing piety.

### 2. The Renewed Character of the New Creation
- **Eph 4:24** — "And that ye put on the new man, which after God is created in righteousness and true holiness (hosiotes)."
  Paul describes the moral character of the believer's new nature as patterned after God's own character, again pairing dikaiosyne with hosiotes, modified by aletheia ("truth/true").
