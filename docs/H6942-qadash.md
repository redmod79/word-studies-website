## Semantic Categories

### 1. Sanctifying Sacred Time
The most ancient usage: God hallows the seventh day at creation; human counterpart is keeping the Sabbath holy.
- **Gen 2:3** — "And God blessed the seventh day, and sanctified it"
- **Deut 5:12** — "Keep the sabbath day to sanctify it, as the LORD thy God hath commanded thee"
- **Ezek 20:12** — "I gave them my sabbaths, to be a sign between me and them, that they might know that I am the LORD that sanctify them"
- **Joel 1:14** — "Sanctify ye a fast, call a solemn assembly, gather the elders"

### 2. Sanctifying Sacred Space and Objects
Consecrating places and things as set apart for divine use — the tabernacle, temple, altar, dedicated spoils.
- **Exo 13:2** — "Sanctify unto me all the firstborn, whatsoever openeth the womb among the children of Israel"
- **Num 7:1** — "Moses had fully set up the tabernacle, and had anointed it, and sanctified it"
- **1 Kgs 8:64** — "The same day did the king hallow the middle of the court that was before the house of the LORD"
- **2 Sam 8:11** — "Which also king David did dedicate unto the LORD, with the silver and gold"

### 3. Consecrating Persons for Sacred Service
Priests, prophets, and Levites set apart from birth or by ritual for divine office.
- **Jer 1:5** — "Before thou camest forth out of the womb I sanctified thee; and I ordained thee a prophet"
- **Exo 19:10** — "Sanctify them today and tomorrow, and let them wash their clothes"
- **1 Chr 15:12** — "Sanctify yourselves, both ye and your brethren, that ye may bring up the ark"
- **1 Chr 15:14** — "So the priests and the Levites sanctified themselves to bring up the ark of the LORD"

### 4. Ritual Self-Preparation Before Encountering God
Reflexive or corporate call to ritual purity before a decisive divine encounter or battle.
- **Jos 3:5** — "Joshua said unto the people, Sanctify yourselves: for tomorrow the LORD will do wonders"
- **Jos 7:13** — "Up, sanctify the people, and say, Sanctify yourselves against tomorrow"
- **1 Sam 16:5** — "Sanctify yourselves, and come with me to the sacrifice"
- **Joel 2:16** — "Gather the people, sanctify the congregation, assemble the elders"

### 5. God Sanctified Among the Nations (Niphal/Passive)
God is "sanctified" — acknowledged as holy and vindicated — through His judgments in history.
- **Isa 5:16** — "But the LORD of hosts shall be exalted in judgment, and God that is holy shall be sanctified in righteousness"
- **Isa 8:13** — "Sanctify the LORD of hosts himself; and let him be your fear"
- **Ezek 7:24** — "I will also make the pomp of the strong to cease; and their holy places shall be defiled"
- **Ezek 20:41** — "I will be sanctified in you before the heathen" (implied from context)

### 6. Consecrating for War / Ritual Preparation for Battle
An unusual extension: mustering warriors or formally proclaiming war as a consecrated act.
- **Isa 13:3** — "I have commanded my sanctified ones, I have also called my mighty ones for mine anger"
- **Jer 6:4** — "Prepare ye war against her; arise, and let us go up at noon"
- **Zeph 1:7** — "For the day of the LORD is at hand: for the LORD hath prepared a sacrifice"
- **2 Kgs 10:20** — "And Jehu said, Proclaim a solemn assembly for Baal. And they proclaimed it"
