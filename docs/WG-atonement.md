## Roles in the Concept

- **Action:** kaphar (כָּפַר) — the atoning/covering act itself
- **Price:** kopher (כֹּפֶר) — the ransom or satisfaction paid
- **Event:** kippur (כִּפֻּר) — the occasion of atonement (Day of Atonement)
- **Place:** kapporeth (כַּפֹּרֶת) — the mercy seat where atonement is made
- **Agent:** kohen (כֹּהֵן) — the priest who performs atonement
- **Instrument:** dam (דָּם) — blood as the means of atonement
- **Offering:** chattaah (חַטָּאָה) — the sin offering presented
- **Location:** mizbeach (מִזְבֵּחַ) — the altar where blood is applied
- **Problem:** avon (עָוֺן) — iniquity/guilt that requires atonement
- **Result (divine):** salach (סָלַח) — God's forgiveness that follows
- **Result (ritual):** taher (טָהֵר) — cleansing/purification achieved
