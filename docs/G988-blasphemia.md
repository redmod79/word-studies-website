## Semantic Categories

### 1. Blasphemy Against God / the Holy Spirit
Speech that directly vilifies God, his name, or his Spirit.
- **Matt 12:31** — "All manner of sin and blasphemy shall be forgiven unto men: but the blasphemy [against] the [Holy] Ghost shall not be forgiven unto men."
- **Rev 13:6** — "And he opened his mouth in blasphemy against God, to blaspheme his name, and his tabernacle, and them that dwell in heaven."
- **Rev 2:9** — "I know... the blasphemy of them which say they are Jews, and are not, but [are] the synagogue of Satan."

### 2. Charge of Blasphemy Against Jesus
The accusation that Jesus' claims or actions constituted blasphemy — claiming divine prerogatives.
- **Matt 26:65** — "He hath spoken blasphemy; what further need have we of witnesses?"
- **Mark 14:64** — "Ye have heard the blasphemy: what think ye? And they all condemned him to be guilty of death."
- **Mark 2:7** — "Why doth this [man] thus speak blasphemies? who can forgive sins but God only?"
- **John 10:33** — "For a good work we stone thee not; but for blasphemy; and because that thou, being a man, makest thyself God."
- **Luke 5:21** — "Who is this which speaketh blasphemies? Who can forgive sins, but God alone?"

### 3. Apocalyptic Blasphemy — the Beast
Blasphemy as a defining characteristic of the beast power in Revelation.
- **Rev 13:1** — "upon his heads the name of blasphemy"
- **Rev 13:5** — "a mouth speaking great things and blasphemies"
- **Rev 17:3** — "a scarlet coloured beast, full of names of blasphemy, having seven heads and ten horns"

### 4. Vice Lists — Blasphemy Among Sins of Speech
Blasphemia grouped with other sins of the tongue or character flaws.
- **Matt 15:19** — "out of the heart proceed evil thoughts, murders, adulteries, fornications, thefts, false witness, blasphemies"
- **Mark 7:22** — "Thefts, covetousness, wickedness, deceit, lasciviousness, an evil eye, blasphemy, pride, foolishness"
- **Col 3:8** — "put off all these; anger, wrath, malice, blasphemy, filthy communication out of your mouth"
- **Eph 4:31** — "Let all bitterness, and wrath, and anger, and clamour, and evil speaking, be put away from you"
- **Mark 3:28** — "All sins shall be forgiven unto the sons of men, and blasphemies wherewith soever they shall blaspheme"

### 5. Railing / Reviling — Speech Against Persons or Powers
When directed at supernatural beings or persons rather than directly at God, KJV renders as "railing."
- **Jude 1:9** — "Michael the archangel... durst not bring against him a railing accusation"
- **1 Tim 6:4** — "doting about questions and strifes of words, whereof cometh envy, strife, railings"
