# WG: Earthquake / Eschatological Signs

## Overview

This cluster maps the three Greek words for earth-disturbance signs in the Olivet Discourse and the four-element theophanic formula in Revelation. Two sub-clusters emerge from tool data:

1. **Olivet triad** — σεισμός + λιμός + λοιμός (earthquake + famine + pestilence) — the signs Jesus lists as "beginning of sorrows" in Matt 24:7, Mark 13:8, Luke 21:11
2. **Theophanic tetrad** — σεισμός + ἀστραπή + βροντή + φωνή (earthquake + lightning + thunder + voices) — the judgment climax formula in Rev 8:5, 11:19, 16:18, and anticipated in Rev 4:5

## Roles in the Concept

- **Earth disturbance:** σεισμός (G4578) — the ground-shaking event; center of both sub-clusters
- **Sky phenomena 1:** ἀστραπή (G796) — flashing lightning; paired with thunder
- **Sky phenomena 2:** βροντή (G1027) — rolling thunder; always co-occurs with astrapḗ in Revelation
- **Auditory signal:** φωνή (G5456) — voices/sounds; the broadest word (129 occurrences), here functioning as the divine utterance accompanying theophany
- **Earthly catastrophe 1:** λιμός (G3042) — famine; paired with pestilence in Olivet Discourse
- **Earthly catastrophe 2:** λοιμός (G3061) — pestilence/plague; smallest word in group (3 occurrences)
