## Roles in the Concept

- **Person:** G4396 prophētēs — who delivers the message (OT prophets; NT prophets; the Prophet)
- **Act:** G4395 prophēteúō — the activity of delivering inspired speech
- **Message/Gift:** G4394 prophēteía — the content or spiritual gift itself
- **Quality:** G4397 prophētikós — describing the scriptures or word as prophetic
- **Source:** G4151 pneûma — the Spirit as the origin and enabler (2 Pet 1:21; Rev 19:10)
- **Contrast:** G1100 glōssa — tongues, regularly paired with prophecy in charismatic contexts to distinguish the two gifts
