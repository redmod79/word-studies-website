## Lexicon Definition

A consecrated thing or place, especially a palace, sanctuary (whether of Jehovah or of idols) or asylum: chapel, hallowed part, holy place, sanctuary.

From H6942 (qadash — to be set apart as holy). The word designates a specific sacred *location*, not holiness as an abstract quality. Can refer to the Tabernacle, the Jerusalem Temple, a future eschatological sanctuary, idolatrous shrines, or metaphorically to God himself as a refuge.
