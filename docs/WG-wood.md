## Roles in the Concept

- **Core material:** ets (עֵץ) — the tree/wood itself, in all its states
- **Paired material:** even (אֶבֶן) — stone, the complement in construction and the idol formula
- **Consuming agent:** esh (אֵשׁ) — fire that burns the wood (sacrifice, judgment, or destruction)
- **Product:** periy (פְּרִי) — fruit that the living tree yields (blessing, provision)
- **Terrain:** yaar (יַעַר) — the forest where trees grow collectively
- **Aramaic parallel:** iylan (אִילָן) — the tree in Daniel's visions (Dan 4)
