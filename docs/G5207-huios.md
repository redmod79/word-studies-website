## Semantic Categories

### 1. Biological / Genealogical Son
Direct male offspring or patrilineal descent.

- **Matt 1:1** — "the son of David, the son of Abraham" — genealogical chain at the outset of the NT
- **Matt 7:9** — "what man is there of you, whom if his son ask bread, will he give him a stone?"
- **Mark 6:3** — "Is not this the carpenter, the son of Mary?" — Jesus identified by paternal/maternal line
- **Jas 2:21** — "Abraham our father...when he had offered Isaac his son upon the altar"

### 2. Son of God (Divine / Christological)
Jesus as the uniquely divine Son declared by the Father, confessed by disciples, recognized by demons. Dominant in John and 1 John.

- **Mark 1:1** — "The beginning of the gospel of Jesus Christ, the Son of God"
- **Matt 3:17** — "This is my beloved Son, in whom I am well pleased" — Father's declaration at baptism
- **Matt 16:16** — "Thou art the Christ, the Son of the living God" — Peter's confession
- **Heb 1:2** — "Hath in these last days spoken unto us by [his] Son, whom he hath appointed heir of all things"
- **1 Jhn 1:3** — "our fellowship is with the Father, and with his Son Jesus Christ"

### 3. Son of Man (Messianic Self-Designation)
Jesus' primary self-title — drawn from Dan 7:13 and Ezek 2:1. Conveys authority, suffering, and eschatological parousia.

- **Matt 8:20** — "the Son of man hath not where to lay his head" — humility
- **Mark 2:10** — "the Son of man hath power on earth to forgive sins" — divine authority
- **Matt 16:27–28** — "the Son of man shall come in the glory of his Father with his angels"
- **Matt 24:30** — "shall appear the sign of the Son of man in heaven...coming in the clouds"
- **Rev 1:13** — "one like unto the Son of man, clothed with a garment down to the foot"

### 4. Son of David (Royal / Messianic Title)
Connects Jesus to the Davidic covenant (2 Sam 7:14); used as a cry for mercy and a messianic acclamation.

- **Matt 1:1** — "Jesus Christ, the son of David" — opening proclamation
- **Matt 9:27** — "two blind men...saying, [Thou] Son of David, have mercy on us"
- **Matt 21:9** — "Hosanna to the Son of David: Blessed is he that cometh in the name of the Lord"
- **Mark 12:35** — "How say the scribes that Christ is the Son of David?" — Jesus questions the title's adequacy

### 5. Qualitative / Membership Sonship ("sons / children of X")
Having the character of, or belonging to, a defined category. The genitive defines the nature of the group.

- **Matt 5:9** — "Blessed are the peacemakers: for they shall be called the children of God"
- **Matt 13:38** — "the good seed are the children of the kingdom; the tares are the children of the wicked one"
- **Mark 3:28** — "All sins shall be forgiven unto the sons of men" — humanity as a class
- **1 Thess 5:5** — "Ye are all the children of light, and the children of the day"
- **Gal 3:7** — "they which are of faith, the same are the children of Abraham"

### 6. Adoptive / Covenantal Sonship (Believers as Sons)
Believers positioned as legal sons/heirs of God through Christ — distinct from biological begetting.

- **Matt 5:45** — "That ye may be the children of your Father which is in heaven"
- **Gal 1:16** — "To reveal his Son in me, that I might preach him among the heathen"
- **Eph 4:13** — "the knowledge of the Son of God, unto a perfect man" — corporate goal of maturity
- **Heb 2:10** — "in bringing many sons unto glory" — the Father's purpose through Christ's suffering
