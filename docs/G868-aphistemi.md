## Lexicon Definition

From ἀπό and ἵστημι; to remove, i.e. (actively) instigate to revolt; usually (reflexively) to desist, desert, etc.: — depart, draw (fall) away, refrain, withdraw self.

Core idea: to cause or undergo standing-at-a-distance. Active/transitive: to cause someone to stand apart (incite to revolt). Middle/intransitive (dominant NT use): to remove oneself — to withdraw, depart, leave, fall away.
