## Roles in the Concept

- **Object:** beriyth (בְּרִית) — the covenant compact itself
- **Action (making):** karath (כָּרַת) — the act of cutting/establishing the covenant
- **Action (keeping):** shamar (שָׁמַר) — the obligation of keeping/guarding
- **Action (breaking):** avar (עָבַר) — the transgression of covenant terms
- **Duration:** olam (עוֹלָם) — the everlasting character of God's covenants
- **Container:** aron (אָרוֹן) — the physical repository of covenant documents
