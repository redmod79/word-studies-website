## Lexicon Definition

Strictly "froth at the mouth" — i.e., (figuratively) fury, especially of God's displeasure with sin.
KJV rendering: angry, indignation, rage.

## Semantic Categories

- **Psa 38:3** — "There is no soundness in my flesh because of thine anger (zaʻam); neither is there any rest in my bones because of my sin."

- **Jer 15:17** — "I sat alone because of thy hand: for thou hast filled me with thy indignation."

### 2. God's Indignation Against Nations (Prophetic Judgment)
