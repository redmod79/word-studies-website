## Roles in the Concept

- **Position:** dexios (δεξιός) — the honored right side; the spatial referent for authority
- **Contrast:** euonymos (εὐώνυμος) / aristeros (ἀριστερός) — the left side; antithetic position in judgment (Mat 25:33) and spatial pairing
- **Posture:** kathemai (κάθημαι) — ongoing seated state at the right hand
- **Action:** kathizo (καθίζω) — the act of taking the seat; enthronement verb
- **Instrument:** cheir (χείρ) — the hand itself; makes the session metaphor concrete
