## Lexicon Definition

Flesh (as stripped of the skin); by extension the body; by implication human nature with its frailties and passions; total humanity ("all flesh"); in Paul, the sinful principle in the believer opposing the Spirit.

## Semantic Categories

### 1. Physical Flesh / Bodily Substance
Literal tissue — human or animal.

- **Luke 24:39** — "a spirit hath not flesh and bones, as ye see me have"
- **1 Cor 15:39** — "All flesh is not the same flesh: one of men, another of beasts, another of fishes, another of birds"
- **Heb 2:14** — "the children are partakers of flesh and blood, he also himself likewise took part of the same"
- **Rev 19:18** — "That ye may eat the flesh of kings, and the flesh of captains, and the flesh of mighty men"

### 2. Human Beings Collectively ("All Flesh")
Sarx as synecdoche for total humanity.

- **Luke 3:6** — "all flesh shall see the salvation of God" (quoting Isa 40:5)
- **John 17:2** — "as thou hast given him power over all flesh, that he should give eternal life"
- **Acts 2:17** — "I will pour out of my Spirit upon all flesh" (quoting Joel 2:28)
- **1 Pet 1:24** — "all flesh is as grass" (quoting Isa 40:6)

### 3. Incarnation / Embodied Human Existence
The enfleshed state — Jesus entering mortal life; believers living in physical bodies.

- **John 1:14** — "And the Word was made flesh, and dwelt among us"
- **1 Tim 3:16** — "God was manifest in the flesh, justified in the Spirit"
- **Heb 5:7** — "Who in the days of his flesh, when he had offered up prayers and supplications"
- **1 John 4:2** — "Every spirit that confesseth that Jesus Christ is come in the flesh is of God"

### 4. Human Nature / Sinful Principle
The flesh as the seat of self-centered desires opposed to the Spirit — dominant in Paul.

- **Rom 7:18** — "I know that in me (that is, in my flesh,) dwelleth no good thing"
- **Rom 8:5** — "they that are after the flesh do mind the things of the flesh; but they that are after the Spirit the things of the Spirit"
- **Gal 5:17, 19** — "the flesh lusteth against the Spirit... Now the works of the flesh are manifest: Adultery, fornication, uncleanness, lasciviousness..."
- **Gal 5:24** — "they that are Christ's have crucified the flesh with the affections and lusts"

### 5. Ethnic / Kinship Identity
Natural descent, blood relation, genealogical belonging.

- **Rom 1:3** — "made of the seed of David according to the flesh"
- **Rom 9:3** — "my kinsmen according to the flesh" (Paul's fellow Israelites)
- **Eph 6:5** — "Servants, be obedient to them that are your masters according to the flesh"
- **Phlm 1:16** — "a brother beloved, specially to me, but how much more unto thee, both in the flesh, and in the Lord"

### 6. Religious Externalism
Circumcision and outward ordinances as "fleshly"; misplaced confidence in human status or ritual.

- **Rom 2:28** — "neither is that circumcision, which is outward in the flesh"
- **Phil 3:3** — "we are the circumcision, which worship God in the spirit... and have no confidence in the flesh"
- **Gal 6:12** — "as many as desire to make a fair shew in the flesh, they constrain you to be circumcised"
- **Col 2:11** — "circumcised with the circumcision made without hands, in putting off the body of the sins of the flesh"
