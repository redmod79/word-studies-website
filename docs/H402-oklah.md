## Semantic Categories

### 1. Food Granted for Human and Animal Consumption
- **Gen 1:29** — seed-bearing herbs and fruit trees are given "for meat"
- **Gen 1:30** — every green herb is given "for meat" to beasts, fowl, and creeping things
- **Gen 9:3** — "Every moving thing that liveth shall be meat for you"

### 2. Stored Food Supply
- **Gen 6:21** — Noah is told to gather "all food that is eaten"
- **Exo 16:15** — manna is identified as food given by YHWH

### 3. Permitted Food Category
- **Lev 11:39** — a beast "of which ye may eat"
- **Lev 25:6** — the sabbath produce of the land is "meat for you"

### 4. Being Food for Beasts / Prey
- **Jer 12:9** — birds are summoned to assemble beasts "to devour"
- **Ezek 34:5, 8** — the flock becomes "meat" to beasts because there is no shepherd
- **Ezek 39:4** — fallen enemies are given to birds and beasts "to be devoured"

### 5. Fuel for Fire
- **Ezek 15:4, 6** — the vine tree is given "for fuel" to the fire
- **Ezek 21:32** — judgment imagery: "Thou shalt be for fuel to the fire"
