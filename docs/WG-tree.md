# WG: Tree / Wood

## Overview
The tree/wood concept cluster in the NT centers on ξύλον (xylon), which spans from raw timber to the crucifixion tree to the eschatological tree of life. Related words distinguish living trees (dendron), the structural cross (stauros), the act of hanging (kremannymi), life itself (zoe), and the weapons paired with wooden clubs (machaira).

## Roles in the Concept

- **Core term:** xylon (G3586) — the tree/wood itself in all senses
- **Crucifixion action:** kremannymi (G2910) — the act of hanging on the xylon
- **Execution structure:** stauros (G4716) — the cross as instrument and theological symbol; never called xylon
- **Botanical tree:** dendron (G1186) — living, growing tree in parables and natural imagery
- **Life quality:** zoe (G2222) — the life that flows from the eschatological tree
- **Paired weapon:** machaira (G3162) — sword paired with xylon-as-club in arrest narratives
