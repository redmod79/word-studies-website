## Semantic Categories

### 1. Divine Judgment Plague — Mass Mortality Sent by God
The dominant use: a lethal epidemic or mass death that YHWH sends against Israel or the nations as punishment for sin.
- **Num 16:48** — "And he stood between the dead and the living; and the plague was stayed."
- **Num 16:49** — "Now they that died in the plague were fourteen thousand and seven hundred"
- **Num 25:8–9** — "So the plague was stayed from the children of Israel. And those that died in the plague were twenty and four thousand."
- **2 Sam 24:21** — "To buy the threshingfloor of thee, to build an altar unto the LORD, that the plague may be stayed from the people."

### 2. Plague Stayed / Restrained Through Intercession
A recurring sub-pattern: the maggephah is "stayed" (H6113, atsar) by priestly or royal intercession — Aaron with the censer (Num 16:48), Phinehas with the spear (Num 25:8), David with the altar (2 Sam 24:25).
- **Num 16:48, 50** — Aaron stood between the dead and the living; the plague was stayed.
- **Psa 106:30** — "Then stood up Phinehas, and executed judgment: and so the plague was stayed."
- **2 Sam 24:25** — "So the LORD was intreated for the land, and the plague was stayed from Israel."

### 3. Threatened / Announced Plague
God announces the maggephah before it falls, as warning or consequence.
- **Exo 9:14** — "For I will at this time send all my plagues upon thine heart"
- **2 Chr 21:14** — "Behold, with a great plague will the LORD smite thy people"
- **Num 31:16** — "there was a plague among the congregation of the LORD" (retrospective)

### 4. Military Slaughter / Defeat
The word extends to large-scale battlefield casualties — defeat as divine striking.
- **1 Sam 4:17** — "there hath been also a great slaughter among the people, and thy two sons also... are dead"
- **2 Sam 18:7** — "there was there a great slaughter that day of twenty thousand men"
- **2 Sam 17:9** — "There is a slaughter among the people that follow Absalom"

### 5. Eschatological Plague
Zechariah 14 uses maggephah for the plague YHWH will send on the nations that fight against Jerusalem in the last days.
- **Zec 14:12** — "this shall be the plague wherewith the LORD will smite all the people that have fought against Jerusalem"
- **Zec 14:15** — "so shall be the plague of the horse, of the mule, of the camel, and of the ass"
- **Zec 14:18** — "there shall be the plague, wherewith the LORD will smite the heathen"

### 6. Personal Stroke / Sudden Loss
One occurrence applies to personal bereavement rather than mass death.
- **Eze 24:16** — "I take away from thee the desire of thine eyes with a stroke" (Ezekiel's wife)

## Related Words

### Word Family / Cognates
- **H5062 נָגַף (nagaph)** — root verb: to push, gore, strike, plague (49 occurrences). The maggephah is the result-noun of this action.
- **H5063 נֶגֶף (negeph)** — masculine noun from same root: a trip, stumbling, infliction of disease (7 occurrences). Narrower: focused on "stumbling" and the striking-event itself.
