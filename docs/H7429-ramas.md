## Lexicon Definition

A primitive root; to tread upon (as a potter, in walking, or abusively) — "oppressor, stamp upon, trample (under feet), tread (down, upon)." The base image is the physical action of the foot pressing down on something beneath it: mud for a potter, enemies in battle, grapes in a winepress, or pasture underfoot.
