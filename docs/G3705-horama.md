## Lexicon Definition

From G3708; something gazed at, i.e. a spectacle (especially supernatural) — a sight, vision. The noun denotes the concrete object of seeing, whether a waking spectacle (Matt 17:9 at the Transfiguration) or, far more commonly in Acts, a divinely given visionary appearance that communicates guidance or revelation.
