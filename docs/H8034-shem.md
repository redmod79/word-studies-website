## Lexicon Definition

A primitive word [perhaps rather from through the idea of definite and conspicuous position; compare שׂוּם]; an appellation, as a mark or memorial of individuality; by implication honor, authority, character — base, (in-) fame(-ous), named(-d), renown, report.
