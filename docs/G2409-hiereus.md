## Semantic Categories

### 1. Priestly Certification of Ritual Purity (Show Yourself to the Priest)
- **Matt 8:4** — "go thy way, shew thyself to the priest, and offer the gift that Moses commanded"
- **Mark 1:44** — "go thy way, shew thyself to the priest, and offer for thy cleansing those things which Moses commanded"
- **Luke 5:14** — "shew thyself to the priest, and offer for thy cleansing, according as Moses commanded"
- **Luke 17:14** — "Go shew yourselves unto the priests. And it came to pass, that, as they went, they were cleansed."

### 2. Levitical Priests as Active Officials (NT Era Historical)
- **Luke 1:5** — "There was in the days of Herod...a certain priest named Zacharias, of the course of Abia"
- **John 1:19** — "the Jews sent priests and Levites from Jerusalem to ask him, Who art thou?"
- **Acts 4:1** — "the priests, and the captain of the temple, and the Sadducees, came upon them"
- **Acts 6:7** — "a great company of the priests were obedient to the faith"

### 3. Priestly Privilege as Argument (Sabbath / Shewbread)
- **Matt 12:5** — "the priests in the temple profane the sabbath, and are blameless"
- **Mark 2:26** — "in the days of Abiathar the high priest...he did eat the shewbread, which is not lawful...but for the priests"

### 4. Pagan Priest
- **Acts 14:13** — "the priest of Jupiter, which was before their city, brought oxen and garlands unto the gates"

### 5. Christ as Eternal Priest After the Order of Melchisedec (Hebrews)
- **Heb 5:6** — "Thou art a priest for ever after the order of Melchisedec"
- **Heb 7:1** — "Melchisedec, king of Salem, priest of the most high God"
- **Heb 7:11** — "if...perfection were by the Levitical priesthood...what further need was there that another priest should rise after the order of Melchisedec"
- **Heb 10:11** — "every priest standeth daily ministering and offering oftentimes the same sacrifices, which can never take away sins"

### 6. Believers as Royal Priests (Revelation)
- **Rev 1:6** — "hath made us kings and priests unto God and his Father"
- **Rev 5:10** — "hast made us unto our God kings and priests: and we shall reign on the earth"
- **Rev 20:6** — "they shall be priests of God and of Christ, and shall reign with him a thousand years"
