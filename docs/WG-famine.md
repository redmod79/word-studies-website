# WG: Famine — Judgment Triad

## Overview
The famine concept in Hebrew Scripture operates within a tightly bound judgment vocabulary. The noun raab (H7458) appears in a fixed prophetic formula alongside chereb (sword, H2719) and deber (pestilence, H1698) — a triad that appears together in 24 verses, concentrated in Jeremiah and Ezekiel. The word family includes the root verb (H7456), adjective (H7457), and a rare noun synonym (H7459). The Greek NT equivalent limos (G3042) continues the same triad pattern in Revelation.

- **Action:** raeb (H7456) — the experience of hungering
