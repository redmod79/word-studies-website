## Lexicon Definition

From ἀπό (G575) and λούω (G3068); to wash fully, i.e. (figuratively) have remitted. The ἀπό prefix adds directional force: washing *away from* — complete removal through bathing.

- **Acts 22:16** — "arise, and be baptized, and wash away (ἀπόλουσαι) thy sins, calling on the name of the Lord"
