## Semantic Categories

### 1. General Speech / Conversation
Ordinary speaking or talking between persons.
- **Mat 12:46** — "While he yet talked to the people, behold, his mother and his brethren stood without, desiring to speak with him"
- **Jhn 4:27** — "And upon this came his disciples, and marvelled that he talked with the woman"
- **Luk 24:32** — "Did not our heart burn within us, while he talked with us by the way"
- **3Jo 1:14** — "I trust I shall shortly see thee, and we shall speak face to face"

### 2. Authoritative / Divine Speech
God, Christ, angels, or the Spirit speaking with authority.
- **Heb 1:1-2** — "God, who at sundry times and in divers manners spake in time past unto the fathers by the prophets, hath in these last days spoken unto us by his Son"
- **Mat 10:20** — "For it is not ye that speak, but the Spirit of your Father which speaketh in you"
- **Jhn 3:34** — "For he whom God hath sent speaketh the words of God"
- **Act 3:21** — "which God hath spoken by the mouth of all his holy prophets"

### 3. Tongues-Speaking / Ecstatic Utterance
Speaking in tongues or other Spirit-empowered speech, concentrated in Acts and 1 Corinthians.
- **Act 2:4** — "they were all filled with the Holy Ghost, and began to speak with other tongues"
- **1Co 14:2** — "For he that speaketh in an unknown tongue speaketh not unto men, but unto God"
- **1Co 14:5** — "I would that ye all spake with tongues, but rather that ye prophesied"
- **Act 19:6** — "the Holy Ghost came on them; and they spake with tongues, and prophesied"

### 4. Bold / Public Proclamation
Speaking boldly or publicly, often in the face of opposition; overlaps with "preach."
- **Act 4:29** — "grant unto thy servants, that with all boldness they may speak thy word"
- **Act 4:31** — "they were all filled with the Holy Ghost, and they spake the word of God with boldness"
- **Eph 6:20** — "that therein I may speak boldly, as I ought to speak"
- **Act 14:1** — "they went both together into the synagogue of the Jews, and so spake, that a great multitude...believed"

### 5. Restoring Speech / Power to Speak
Healing of the mute; the physical ability to produce speech.
- **Mat 9:33** — "And when the devil was cast out, the dumb spake"
- **Mat 12:22** — "he healed him, insomuch that the blind and dumb both spake and saw"
- **Mrk 7:35** — "the string of his tongue was loosed, and he spake plain"
- **Luk 1:64** — "his mouth was opened immediately, and his tongue loosed, and he spake"

### 6. Blasphemous / Evil Speech
Speaking blasphemy, lies, or great boasts.
- **Mrk 2:7** — "Why doth this man thus speak blasphemies?"
- **Rev 13:5** — "there was given unto him a mouth speaking great things and blasphemies"
- **Jhn 8:44** — "When he speaketh a lie, he speaketh of his own: for he is a liar"
- **Act 6:13** — "This man ceaseth not to speak blasphemous words against this holy place"

### 7. Reported / Prophetic Speech
Things spoken or told by prophets; passive uses conveying messages.
- **Luk 1:45** — "there shall be a performance of those things which were told her from the Lord"
- **Luk 2:17** — "they made known abroad the saying which was told them concerning this child"
- **2Pe 1:21** — "holy men of God spake as they were moved by the Holy Ghost"
- **Heb 3:5** — "Moses verily was faithful...for a testimony of those things which were to be spoken after"
