## Roles in the Concept

- **Action (wearing):** labash (H3847) — the primary verb for putting on / being clothed
- **Generic garment:** beged (H899) — the most common garment noun, also connotes treachery (same root as bagad)
- **Sacred material:** bad (H906) — linen specifically, marking priestly purity
- **Inner garment:** kethoneth (H3801) — the tunic/coat, from creation (Gen 3:21) through priesthood
- **Covering action:** kasah (H3680) — to cover/conceal, overlapping with labash but emphasizing hiddenness
- **Adorning action:** ʻadah (H5710) — to deck/adorn, the ornamental complement to labash
