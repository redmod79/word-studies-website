## Lexicon Definition

From G1832 (in the sense of ability); privilege, i.e. (subjectively) force, capacity, competency,
freedom, or (objectively) mastery; also delegated influence, potentate, token of control.

## Semantic Categories

### 1. Divine / Delegated Authority
Exousia explicitly granted from a higher source — God, the Father, or ordained structures.

- **Mat 28:18** — "All power is given unto me in heaven and in earth."
- **John 17:2** — "As thou hast given him power over all flesh, that he should give eternal life"
- **John 19:11** — "Thou couldest have no power at all against me, except it were given thee from above"
- **Rom 13:1** — "there is no power but of God: the powers that be are ordained of God"

### 2. Personal Capacity / Right / Liberty
Exousia as one's own sphere of legitimate freedom — what one is entitled or permitted to do.

- **Acts 5:4** — "Whiles it remained, was it not thine own? and after it was sold, was it not in thine own power?"
- **1 Cor 7:37** — "having no necessity, but hath power over his own will"
- **1 Cor 8:9** — "take heed lest by any means this liberty of yours become a stumblingblock"
- **John 10:18** — "I have power to lay it down, and I have power to take it again"

### 3. Governmental / Judicial / Institutional Authority
Exousia exercised through official roles — legal, civic, priestly, or military command.

- **Mat 8:9** — "I am a man under authority, having soldiers under me"
- **Luke 23:7** — "he belonged unto Herod's jurisdiction"
- **Acts 9:14** — "here he hath authority from the chief priests to bind all that call on thy name"
- **Mat 21:23** — "By what authority doest thou these things? and who gave thee this authority?"

### 4. Cosmic / Angelic Authorities
Exousia applied to supernatural hierarchical powers — heavenly and demonic ranks.

- **Eph 1:21** — "Far above all principality, and power, and might, and dominion"
- **Eph 6:12** — "against principalities, against powers, against the rulers of the darkness of this world"
- **Col 1:16** — "whether they be thrones, or dominions, or principalities, or powers"
- **1 Pet 3:22** — "angels and authorities and powers being made subject unto him"

### 5. Apocalyptic / Beast's Authority (Revelation)
Exousia granted to the beast and eschatological powers; also promised to overcomers.

- **Rev 13:2** — "the dragon gave him his power, and his seat, and great authority"
- **Rev 13:7** — "power was given him over all kindreds, and tongues, and nations"
- **Rev 2:26** — "to him will I give power over the nations"
- **Rev 12:10** — "Now is come salvation, and strength, and the kingdom of our God, and the power of his Christ"
