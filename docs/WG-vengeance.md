# WG: Vengeance / Retribution

## Overview
The vengeance concept spans both Testaments through a tight word family. Hebrew has the root n-q-m producing a verb (H5358), a masculine noun (H5359), and a feminine noun (H5360) — together appearing in 70 distinct verses. The LXX translators rendered this family primarily through the ekdik- group (G1557, G1556), rooted in dikē (G1349, "justice"), framing vengeance as the execution of justice rather than mere retaliation.
