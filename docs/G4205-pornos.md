## Lexicon Definition

From πέρνημι (pernēmi, "to sell"); a (male) prostitute (by extension, a debauchee). The word originally denoted a man who sells his body, parallel to πόρνη (pornē), the female counterpart.

## Semantic Categories

### 1. Exclusion from Church Fellowship
- **1Co 5:9** — "I wrote unto you in an epistle not to company with fornicators"
- **1Co 5:10** — "Yet not altogether with the fornicators of this world, or with the covetous, or extortioners, or with idolaters"
- **1Co 5:11** — "not to keep company, if any man that is called a brother be a fornicator, or covetous, or an idolater"

Paul distinguishes between associating with outsiders who are pornoi and tolerating a pornos within the church. The word appears three times in this single disciplinary passage.

### 2. Exclusion from the Kingdom of God
- **1Co 6:9** — "neither fornicators, nor idolaters, nor adulterers, nor effeminate, nor abusers of themselves with mankind"
- **Eph 5:5** — "no whoremonger, nor unclean person, nor covetous man, who is an idolater, hath any inheritance in the kingdom of Christ and of God"

Pornos heads the list of those barred from inheriting God's kingdom.

### 3. Vice Lists with Paired Sins
- **1Ti 1:10** — "For whoremongers, for them that defile themselves with mankind, for menstealers, for liars, for perjured persons"
- **Heb 13:4** — "whoremongers and adulterers God will judge"

Pornos appears alongside specific paired sins: arsenokoitai (1 Tim), moichoi (Heb 13:4).

### 4. Warning Example — Esau
- **Heb 12:16** — "Lest there be any fornicator, or profane person, as Esau, who for one morsel of meat sold his birthright"

Unique usage: pornos parallels "profane person" (bebēlos) with Esau as the example — appetite overriding covenant inheritance. May carry the broader sense of one who sells what is sacred for bodily gratification.

### 5. Final Exclusion (Eschatological)
- **Rev 21:8** — "the fearful, and unbelieving, and the abominable, and murderers, and whoremongers, and sorcerers, and idolaters, and all liars, shall have their part in the lake which burneth with fire and brimstone"
- **Rev 22:15** — "without are dogs, and sorcerers, and whoremongers, and murderers, and idolaters, and whosoever loveth and maketh a lie"

In both Revelation exclusion lists, pornoi appear alongside murderers, sorcerers, and idolaters — those permanently outside the New Jerusalem.
