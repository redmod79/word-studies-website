## Semantic Categories

### 1. Cultic Images to Be Rejected or Destroyed
The noun names visible figured objects associated with forbidden worship.
- **Lev 26:1** — "neither shall ye set up any image of stone in your land" — a carved or figured cult-object set up for veneration.
- **Num 33:52** — "destroy all their pictures, and destroy all their molten images" — pictured objects paired with overt idol vocabulary.

### 2. Pictured or Worked Visual Surfaces
The noun can name crafted visual representation on a surface or setting.
- **Pro 25:11** — "apples of gold in pictures of silver" — ornamental silver settings, carvings, or filigreed work.
- **Eze 8:12** — "every man in the chambers of his imagery" — pictured chambers used for hidden idolatry; the visual/object sense remains concrete even in a corrupt cultic setting.

### 3. Inward Imagination, Conceit, or Heart-Desire
The noun also moves from external figure to internal mental picture.
- **Pro 18:11** — "as an high wall in his own conceit" — imagined security, not literal fortification.
- **Psa 73:7** — "they have more than heart could wish" — overflowing inward desire or imagination.
