## Semantic Categories

### 1. Communal/National Guilt Before God
The dominant usage — collective trespass against YHWH requiring corporate repentance.
- **Ezra 9:6** — "our iniquities are increased over our head, and our trespass is grown up unto the heavens"
- **Ezra 9:7** — "Since the days of our fathers have we been in a great trespass unto this day"
- **Ezra 9:13** — "after all that is come upon us for our evil deeds, and for our great trespass"
- **Ezra 9:15** — "we are before thee in our trespasses: for we cannot stand before thee because of this"

### 2. Cause of Guilt / Liability
The word denotes a condition that brings guilt upon a person or community — a cause of trespass.
- **1 Chr 21:3** — "why will he be a cause of trespass to Israel?" (Joab warning David about the census)
- **2 Chr 28:13** — "ye intend to add more to our sins and to our trespass: for our trespass is great"
- **Ezra 10:10** — "Ye have transgressed, and have taken strange wives, to increase the trespass of Israel"

### 3. Cultic/Ritual Trespass
Used in Levitical legislation describing offenses against holy things.
- **Lev 4:3** — "If the priest that is anointed do sin according to the sin of the people" (ashmah as the sin-condition)
- **Lev 6:5** — "he shall even restore it... in the day of his trespass offering"
- **Lev 6:7** — "it shall be forgiven him for any thing of all that he hath done in trespassing therein"
- **Lev 22:16** — "suffer them to bear the iniquity of trespass, when they eat their holy things"

### 4. Idolatrous Guilt
Applied to apostasy and idol-worship.
- **2 Chr 24:18** — "they left the house of the LORD... and wrath came upon Judah and Jerusalem for this their trespass"
- **2 Chr 33:23** — "Amon trespassed more and more" (following his father Manasseh's idolatry)
- **Amos 8:14** — "They that swear by the sin of Samaria" (the calf-idol at Bethel)

### 5. Personal Acknowledgment of Sin
- **Psa 69:5** — "O God, thou knowest my foolishness; and my sins are not hid from thee"
- **2 Chr 28:10** — "are there not with you, even with you, sins against the LORD your God?"
