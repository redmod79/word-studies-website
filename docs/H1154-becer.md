## Semantic Categories

### 1. Premature / Unripe Fruit (metaphor for divine judgment)
- **Job 15:33** — "He shall shake off his unripe grape (beser) as the vine, and shall cast off his flower as the olive."

Eliphaz uses beser as a metaphor for the wicked man's works coming to nothing — like a vine that drops its fruit before it ripens. The image emphasizes premature loss: the grape never reaches maturity. Paired with the olive casting off its blossom, the verse describes total agricultural failure as a figure for judgment on the ungodly.
