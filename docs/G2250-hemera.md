## Lexicon Definition

Feminine noun; from a derivative of ἧμαι (hēmai, "to sit"). A day — the period from sunrise to sunset (as distinct from night), or a civil/legal day of 24 hours, or figuratively an era, epoch, or appointed time. The word covers literal daylight hours, calendar days, and extended periods ("the days of Noah," "the last days").
