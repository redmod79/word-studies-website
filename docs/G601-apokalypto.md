## Lexicon Definition

From ἀπό (G575) and καλύπτω (G2572); to take off the cover, i.e. disclose — reveal. The literal sense is "to uncover, unveil" — removing something that conceals so the hidden thing becomes visible or known. Exclusively translated "reveal/revealed" in the KJV.

## Semantic Categories

### 1. Divine Disclosure of Hidden Truth to Chosen Recipients
God or Christ actively reveals spiritual realities that human wisdom cannot access.
- **Mat 11:25** — "thou hast hidden these things from the wise ... and hast revealed them unto babes"
- **Mat 16:17** — "flesh and blood hath not revealed it unto thee, but my Father which is in heaven"
- **1Co 2:10** — "God hath revealed them unto us by his Spirit"
- **Gal 1:16** — "to reveal his Son in me, that I might preach him among the heathen"

### 2. Eschatological Unveiling — Glory, Salvation, Judgment
Future realities that will be disclosed at the end of the age.
- **Rom 8:18** — "the glory which shall be revealed in us"
- **1Pe 1:5** — "salvation ready to be revealed in the last time"
- **1Pe 5:1** — "a partaker of the glory that shall be revealed"
- **1Co 3:13** — "the day shall declare it, because it shall be revealed by fire"

### 3. The Revelation of the Man of Sin / Lawless One
The apocalyptic uncovering of an eschatological adversary.
- **2Th 2:3** — "except ... that man of sin be revealed, the son of perdition"
- **2Th 2:6** — "that he might be revealed in his time"
- **2Th 2:8** — "then shall that Wicked be revealed, whom the Lord shall consume"

### 4. The Revealing of the Son of Man
The public manifestation of Christ.
- **Luk 17:30** — "Even thus shall it be in the day when the Son of man is revealed"

### 5. Uncovering What Is Hidden — General Principle
Nothing remains permanently concealed.
- **Mat 10:26** — "there is nothing covered, that shall not be revealed"
- **Luk 12:2** — "there is nothing covered, that shall not be revealed"
- **Luk 2:35** — "that the thoughts of many hearts may be revealed"

### 6. Present Ongoing Revelation — Righteousness and Wrath
God's character attributes currently being disclosed.
- **Rom 1:17** — "therein is the righteousness of God revealed from faith to faith"
- **Rom 1:18** — "the wrath of God is revealed from heaven against all ungodliness"

### 7. The Faith Itself as Revealed Content
The gospel system arriving at its appointed time.
- **Gal 3:23** — "shut up unto the faith which should afterwards be revealed"
