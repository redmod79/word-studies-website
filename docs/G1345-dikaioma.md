## Semantic Categories

### 1. Righteous Regulation / Ordinance
The concrete requirements or statutes of God's law.

- **Luke 1:6** — "walking in all the commandments and **ordinances** of the Lord blameless"
- **Hebrews 9:1** — "the first [covenant] had also **ordinances** of divine service, and a worldly sanctuary"
- **Hebrews 9:10** — "meats and drinks, and divers washings, and carnal **ordinances**, imposed on them until the time of reformation"

### 2. Righteous Requirement of the Law
The just standard the law demands, viewed as fulfillable.

- **Romans 2:26** — "if the uncircumcision keep the **righteousness** of the law, shall not his uncircumcision be counted for circumcision?"
- **Romans 8:4** — "That the **righteousness** of the law might be fulfilled in us, who walk not after the flesh, but after the Spirit"

### 3. Judicial Decree / Sentence
God's righteous verdict or pronouncement.

- **Romans 1:32** — "Who knowing the **judgment** of God, that they which commit such things are worthy of death"
- **Revelation 15:4** — "for thy **judgments** are made manifest"

### 4. Righteous Act (with justifying effect)
A single righteous act that produces legal consequences.

- **Romans 5:16** — "the free gift is of many offences unto **justification**"
- **Romans 5:18** — "by the **righteousness** of one the free gift came upon all men unto justification of life"

### 5. Righteous Deeds (of believers)
Concrete acts of righteousness collectively.

- **Revelation 19:8** — "the fine linen is the **righteousness** of saints"
