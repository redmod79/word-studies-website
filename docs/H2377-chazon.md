## Lexicon Definition

A sight (mentally), i.e. a dream, revelation, or oracle: — vision. From חָזָה (chazah, H2372). Denotes a divine communication received through prophetic sight — not ordinary seeing but revelatory perception granted by God.

## Semantic Categories

### 1. Prophetic Oracle — Book Title/Superscription
The formal heading for a prophet's collected revelations.
- **Isa 1:1** — "The vision of Isaiah the son of Amoz, which he saw concerning Judah and Jerusalem"
- **Nah 1:1** — "The burden of Nineveh. The book of the vision of Nahum the Elkoshite"
- **Oba 1:1** — "The vision of Obadiah"
- **2 Chr 32:32** — "written in the vision of Isaiah the prophet"

### 2. Apocalyptic/Eschatological Vision
Specific revelation of future events, especially end-time scenarios; dominant in Daniel.
- **Dan 8:1** — "a vision appeared unto me, even unto me Daniel"
- **Dan 8:13** — "How long shall be the vision concerning the daily sacrifice, and the transgression of desolation?"
- **Dan 8:17** — "at the time of the end shall be the vision"
- **Dan 8:26** — "the vision of the evening and the morning which was told is true"
- **Dan 9:24** — "to seal up the vision and prophecy, and to anoint the most Holy"
- **Dan 10:14** — "the vision is for many days"

### 3. Divine Communication to Prophets
God speaking to or through individuals via revelatory perception.
- **1 Sam 3:1** — "the word of the LORD was precious in those days; there was no open vision"
- **Ps 89:19** — "Then thou spakest in vision to thy holy one"
- **Hos 12:10** — "I have also spoken by the prophets, and I have multiplied visions"
- **1 Chr 17:15** — "According to all these words, and according to all this vision, so did Nathan speak unto David"

### 4. False or Absent Vision
Prophetic claims without divine origin, or withdrawal of prophetic gift.
- **Jer 14:14** — "they prophesy unto you a false vision and divination"
- **Jer 23:16** — "they speak a vision of their own heart, and not out of the mouth of the LORD"
- **Eze 12:24** — "there shall be no more any vain vision nor flattering divination"
- **Eze 13:16** — "the prophets of Israel which... see visions of peace for her, and there is no peace"
- **Lam 2:9** — "her prophets also find no vision from the LORD"
- **Mic 3:6** — "night shall be unto you, that ye shall not have a vision"

### 5. Vision Needing Preservation or Fulfillment
Vision as something to be recorded, awaited, or established.
- **Pro 29:18** — "Where there is no vision, the people perish"
- **Hab 2:2** — "Write the vision, and make it plain upon tables"
- **Hab 2:3** — "the vision is yet for an appointed time, but at the end it shall speak, and not lie"
- **Dan 11:14** — "the robbers of thy people shall exalt themselves to establish the vision"
