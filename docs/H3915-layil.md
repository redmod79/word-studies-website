## Lexicon Definition

Or (Isa 21:11) leyl; also laylah; from the same as H3883; properly, a twist (away of the light), i.e. night; figuratively, adversity: — (mid-)night (season).

## Semantic Categories

### 1. Creation Ordinance — Day/Night Cycle
God's naming and ordering of the light-darkness cycle.
- **Gen 1:5** — "And God called the light Day, and the darkness he called Night."

- **Gen 8:22** — "day and night shall not cease"
- **Jer 33:20** — "If ye can break my covenant of the day, and my covenant of the night"

The "day and night" formula signaling unbroken, continuous action.
- **Josh 1:8** — "thou shalt meditate therein day and night"

- **1 Kings 8:29** — "thine eyes may be open toward this house night and day"
