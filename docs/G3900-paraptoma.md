## Lexicon Definition

From G3895 (parapiptō); a side-slip (lapse or deviation), i.e. (unintentional) error or (wilful) transgression. The etymological image is of falling beside the path — a misstep rather than a direct assault. However, NT usage extends well beyond unintentional lapses to encompass the full gravity of human sin (Rom 5:15–20).
