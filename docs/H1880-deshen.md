## Lexicon Definition

From H1878 (dashan); the fat; abstractly fatness, i.e. (figuratively) abundance; specifically the (fatty) ashes of sacrifices: — ashes, fatness.

The noun carries two distinct semantic poles. Concretely, it refers to the fat-saturated residue of burnt offerings — the greasy ashes left on the altar after sacrificial combustion. Abstractly, it denotes richness, abundance, or prosperity, often used figuratively for God's generous provision.
