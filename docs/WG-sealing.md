## Roles in the Concept

- **Action (verb):** σφραγίζω / חָתַם — the act of sealing
- **Instrument (noun):** σφραγίς / חוֹתָם — the seal or signet ring used
- **Intensive (verb):** κατασφραγίζω — emphatic sealing (completely sealed)
- **Agent (collocation):** πνεῦμα ἅγιον — the Holy Spirit as the one who seals believers
- **Result:** authentication, security, ownership, concealment (depending on context)
