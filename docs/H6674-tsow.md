## Lexicon Definition

Or צֹא; from an unused root meaning to issue; soiled (as if excrementitious): — filthy.

The definition explicitly ties the word to excremental soiling. This is not generic dirtiness or ritual impurity but filth associated with bodily waste — a visceral, physically repulsive defilement.

## Semantic Categories

### 1. Excremental Defilement of Priestly Garments
- **Zec 3:3** — "Now Joshua was clothed with filthy (tsow) garments, and stood before the angel."
- **Zec 3:4** — "And he answered and spake unto those that stood before him, saying, Take away the filthy (tsow) garments from him. And unto him he said, Behold, I have caused thine iniquity to pass from thee, and I will clothe thee with change of raiment."

Both occurrences describe the high priest Joshua standing in the divine court wearing garments soiled with excremental filth. The removal of these garments is explicitly equated with the removal of iniquity (avon H5771) in verse 4.
