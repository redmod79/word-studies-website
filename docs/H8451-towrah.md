## Lexicon Definition

A precept or statute, especially the Decalogue or Pentateuch. From יָרָה (yârah), pointing to the root meaning of "direction, instruction" — what God teaches and directs. Rendered uniformly "law" in KJV, but the underlying concept is closer to "instruction" or "teaching" than to a legal code in the modern sense.

## Semantic Categories

### 1. Priestly Case-Law Formulas
Levitical regulatory headings: "This is the torah of the X offering." Torah here = the specific procedure/rule for a ritual case.
- **Lev 6:9** — "This [is] the law of the burnt offering: It [is] the burnt offering, because of the burning upon the altar all night"
- **Lev 14:57** — "To teach when [it is] unclean, and when [it is] clean: this [is] the law of leprosy"
- **Num 6:13** — "this [is] the law of the Nazarite, when the days of his separation are fulfilled"
- **Num 5:29** — "This [is] the law of jealousies, when a wife goeth aside"

### 2. The Mosaic Covenant Corpus
Torah as the written body of legislation given through Moses — "this book of the law," "the law of Moses." Encompasses the whole Pentateuch or specific covenantal legislation.
- **Deut 1:5** — "Moses began to declare this law"
- **Josh 1:8** — "This book of the law shall not depart out of thy mouth; but thou shalt meditate therein day and night"
- **2 Kgs 22:8** — "I have found the book of the law in the house of the LORD"
- **Dan 9:13** — "As [it is] written in the law of Moses, all this evil is come upon us"

### 3. Covenant Faithfulness Standard
Torah as the measure by which covenant loyalty is judged — walking in, forsaking, transgressing, or casting behind one's back.
- **Jer 9:13** — "Because they have forsaken my law which I set before them, and have not obeyed my voice"
- **Neh 9:26** — "they...cast thy law behind their backs, and slew thy prophets"
- **Jer 31:33** — "I will put my law in their inward parts, and write it in their hearts"
- **Ezek 22:26** — "Her priests have violated my law, and have profaned mine holy things"

### 4. Personal Meditation and Delight
Psalms usage: torah as the object of personal love, meditation, and joy. The interior relationship to God's instruction.
- **Psalm 1:2** — "his delight [is] in the law of the LORD; and in his law doth he meditate day and night"
- **Psalm 119:97** — "O how love I thy law! it [is] my meditation all the day"
- **Psalm 40:8** — "yea, thy law [is] within my heart"
- **Psalm 119:165** — "Great peace have they which love thy law: and nothing shall offend them"

### 5. Prophetic and Eschatological Torah
Torah going out to the nations from Zion; future torah written on hearts or magnified by God.
- **Isa 2:3** — "out of Zion shall go forth the law, and the word of the LORD from Jerusalem"
- **Isa 42:21** — "he will magnify the law, and make [it] honourable"
- **Isa 51:4** — "a law shall proceed from me, and I will make my judgment to rest for a light of the people"
- **Jer 31:33** — "I will put my law in their inward parts, and write it in their hearts" (new covenant)

### 6. Priestly Teaching Function
Priests as the custodians and oral dispensers of torah — they instruct the people; torah "perishes" when priests fail.
- **Mal 2:6-7** — "The law of truth was in his mouth...they should seek the law at his mouth: for he [is] the messenger of the LORD"
- **Hag 2:11** — "Ask now the priests [concerning] the law"
- **Ezek 7:26** — "then shall they seek a vision of the prophet; but the law shall perish from the priest"
- **Hosea 4:6** — "because thou hast rejected knowledge, I will also reject thee...seeing thou hast forgotten the law of thy God"

### 7. Wisdom Instruction
Proverbs extends torah beyond the Mosaic code to parental/teacher instruction — "the torah of your mother." Torah as guidance that illumines and gives life.
- **Prov 1:8** — "forsake not the law of thy mother"
- **Prov 6:23** — "the commandment [is] a lamp; and the law [is] light; and reproofs of instruction [are] the way of life"
- **Prov 13:14** — "The law of the wise [is] a fountain of life, to depart from the snares of death"
- **Prov 31:26** — "in her tongue [is] the law of kindness"
