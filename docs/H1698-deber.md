## Semantic Categories

### 1. Triad of Covenant Judgment: Sword, Famine, Pestilence
The dominant usage pattern. Deber appears alongside chereb (sword) and ra'ab (famine) as the standard trio of covenant-curses, particularly in Jeremiah and Ezekiel.
- **Jer 14:12** — "I will consume them by the sword, and by the famine, and by the pestilence"
- **Jer 21:9** — "He that abideth in this city shall die by the sword, and by the famine, and by the pestilence"
- **Eze 5:12** — "A third part of thee shall die with the pestilence, and with famine shall they be consumed... a third part shall fall by the sword"
- **Eze 6:12** — "He that is far off shall die of the pestilence; and he that is near shall fall by the sword; and he that remaineth... shall die with the famine"

### 2. Direct Divine Weapon Against Nations
God sends or threatens pestilence as a direct instrument of judgment, without the triad framing.
- **Exo 9:15** — "I will stretch out my hand, that I may smite thee and thy people with pestilence"
- **Num 14:12** — "I will smite them with the pestilence, and disinherit them"
- **2 Sam 24:15** — "the LORD sent a pestilence upon Israel... and there died of the people... seventy thousand men"
- **Eze 28:23** — "I will send into her pestilence, and blood into her streets"

### 3. Covenant Warning and Prayer Context
Deber appears in covenant-warning passages and in prayers that anticipate or respond to God's judgments on Israel.
- **Lev 26:25** — "when ye are gathered together within your cities, I will send the pestilence among you"
- **Deut 28:21** — "The LORD shall make the pestilence cleave unto thee, until he have consumed thee"
- **1 Ki 8:37** — "If there be in the land famine, if there be pestilence..."
- **2 Chr 20:9** — "the sword, judgment, or pestilence, or famine, we stand before this house... and cry unto thee"

### 4. David's Census Judgment
A specific narrative triad: famine, military defeat, or pestilence offered as choices.
- **2 Sam 24:13** — "Shall seven years of famine come unto thee... or that there be three days' pestilence in thy land?"
- **1 Chr 21:12** — "three years' famine; or three months to be destroyed... or else three days the sword of the LORD, even the pestilence"
- **1 Chr 21:14** — "the LORD sent pestilence upon Israel: and there fell of Israel seventy thousand men"

### 5. Personified / Eschatological Force
Rare poetic uses where pestilence is personified as a force attending God.
- **Hab 3:5** — "Before him went the pestilence, and burning coals went forth at his feet"
- **Psa 91:3** — "he shall deliver thee from the snare of the fowler, and from the noisome pestilence"
- **Psa 91:6** — "the pestilence that walketh in darkness"
- **Hos 13:14** — "O death, I will be thy plagues (deber)"

### 6. Livestock Disease (Murrain)
One occurrence uses deber for animal plague specifically.
- **Exo 9:3** — "the hand of the LORD is upon thy cattle... there shall be a very grievous murrain"
