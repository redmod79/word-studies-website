## Semantic Categories

### 1. Divine Vindication of the Elect
God acting on behalf of his people to secure justice for them.
- **Luk 18:7** — "And shall not God avenge [ποιήσῃ τὴν ἐκδίκησιν] his own elect, which cry day and night unto him, though he bear long with them?"
- **Luk 18:8** — "I tell you that he will avenge [ποιήσει τὴν ἐκδίκησιν] them speedily."

### 2. Eschatological / Prophetic Judgment
Appointed times of divine retribution on a national or cosmic scale.
- **Luk 21:22** — "For these be the days of vengeance [ἡμέραι ἐκδικήσεως], that all things which are written may be fulfilled."
- **2Th 1:8** — "In flaming fire taking vengeance [διδόντος ἐκδίκησιν] on them that know not God, and that obey not the gospel of our Lord Jesus Christ."

### 3. Divine Prerogative — "Vengeance Is Mine"
Retribution as a right belonging exclusively to God, forbidding personal retaliation.
- **Rom 12:19** — "Dearly beloved, avenge not yourselves, but rather give place unto wrath: for it is written, Vengeance [ἐκδίκησις] is mine; I will recompense, saith the Lord."
- **Heb 10:30** — "For we know him that hath said, Vengeance [ἐκδίκησις] belongeth unto me, I will recompense, saith the Lord."

### 4. Human Justice / Civil Punishment
Vindication carried out through human agents or governing authorities.
- **Act 7:24** — "And seeing one of them suffer wrong, he defended him, and avenged [ἐποίησεν ἐκδίκησιν] him that was oppressed, and smote the Egyptian."
- **1Pe 2:14** — "Or unto governors, as unto them that are sent by him for the punishment [ἐκδίκησιν] of evildoers, and for the praise of them that do well."

### 5. Moral Earnestness / Zeal to Make Right
Internal resolve to correct wrongdoing within a community.
- **2Co 7:11** — "For behold this selfsame thing, that ye sorrowed after a godly sort, what carefulness it wrought in you, yea, what clearing of yourselves, yea, what indignation, yea, what fear, yea, what vehement desire, yea, what zeal, yea, what revenge [ἐκδίκησιν]!"
